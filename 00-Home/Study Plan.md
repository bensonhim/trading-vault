## Phase 3 — Deep Work Exercise (Week 3–4)

This is the single most impactful exercise in the entire system.

> *"My number one recommended book for traders is a book called Deep Work by Cal Newport."*

### Deep Work Protocol

1. **Pick ONE setup** (from Phase 2 choice)
2. **Lock yourself away** for 24–48 hours
3. **Study 5,000+ examples** using TC2000 or your screener
4. **Document each example:**
   - Pre-move price
   - Catalyst (can you name it in 1 sentence?)
   - Sector
   - Market cap
   - Volume on trigger day
   - Number of legs
   - Duration of move (days)
5. **Do 3 passes** — question quality improves each round
6. **Result:** Your brain develops procedural memory — you recognize valid setups instantly

## Key Formulas — Quick Reference Card

| Formula | Calculation | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Primary Indicator** | Stocks up 25% (65d) − Stocks down 25% (65d) | 1582 − 902 = **680** (strong bull) |
| **Position Size** | Account × Risk% ÷ Stop Width | $100K × 1% ÷ 2.5% = **$40K** |
| **4% Breakout** | Close ≥ 1.04 × Previous Close | Stock at $10 → $10.40+ |
| **9M Volume** | Volume ≥ 9,000,000 | Institutional participation confirmed |
| **Dollar Breakout** | Close − Open ≥ $0.90 (for stocks >$60) | FHP variant |
| **3T Tight Days** | 3 consecutive days with <1% daily change | ANTS prerequisite |
| **Max Overnight Risk** | 6% of total capital (all stops simultaneously) | 6 positions × 1% each = 6% |
| **80/20 Exit** | Sell 80% at target, trail 20% | 1000 shares → sell 800, hold 200 |
| **Magna 53** | M=Massive earnings, A=Acceleration sales, G=Gap 4%+, N=Neglect, 5=SI>5, 3=3 analysts | Life-changing EP filter |
| **Cap 10×10** | Market cap <$10B, IPO'd within 10 years | Bucket 0 sweet spot |
| **39% Rule** | Sales growth ≥39% for 2 consecutive quarters | Strongest single predictor |

## The Checklist — Before Every Trade

Print this or keep it visible. Check every box before entering.

- [ ] Is Market Monitor green? (for longs) / red? (for shorts)
- [ ] Is there a clear catalyst I can explain in 10 minutes?
- [ ] Is the stock on my Sugar Baby list or institutional quality (1000+ funds)?
- [ ] Is this the FIRST valid setup? (not second/third leg)
- [ ] Is my stop defined and placed?
- [ ] Is my position size calculated (risk ÷ stop width)?
- [ ] Am I entering in the right time window? (morning = breakouts; afternoon = ANTS/reversals)
- [ ] Am I buying day 1? (not day 2 or 3)
- [ ] Does this setup have confluence? (multiple edges aligned)
- [ ] Am I prepared to move to breakeven tomorrow if positive?

**If you can't check all boxes, don't trade.**
