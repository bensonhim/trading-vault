### SIPs Methodology — Detailed Explanation

**Priority system for SIPs:**
1. Identify stocks with catalyst (20% study, premarket gainers, NTRT/MTRT)
2. Check: Is it a start of a swing? (Pradeep's condition)
3. Is there high short interest? (adds fuel)
4. Enter as OPG, move stop immediately if going in favor
5. 80/20 pill: take 80% profit, keep 20% swing

**Why OPG, not premarket:** No control in premarket. Can't put stops. Risk management requires control. "The moment I put a position in premarket, control is gone."

**20% study is the key:**
> "If you study 20% studies, you will know. The stocks which have catalyst, they go up. You have to know what is the reason why they go up."
