### Sugar Babies and Wider Stops

> "If you're buying something which is a sugar baby or a 9M breakout, can you put a stop like half day or something and expect it to work? No."

Sugar babies and 9M breakouts are higher volatility trades. They need wider stops. These are two different kinds of trades — don't use tight stops on high-volatility setups.
