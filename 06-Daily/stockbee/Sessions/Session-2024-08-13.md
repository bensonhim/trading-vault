# High Volatility Good for Day Trading, Difficult for Swing, 9M and Delayed Reactions Work, Find Solutions

Good market for high-volatility intraday trading. Slightly difficult for swing trades due to intraday fades. 9M EPs have worked well. Focus on 9M and delayed reactions with quicker profits and smaller targets. Many smaller stocks had earnings this season — delayed reaction EPs will set up eventually. Find solutions when things don't work.

---

## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: High Volatility, Day Trade Market

> "It's a good market for high volatility intraday trading. Slightly difficult for swing trades because there are a lot of intraday fades."

SA assessment:
- **Good for day trading** — High volatility environment
- **Difficult for swing** — Intraday fades kill swing positions
- **9M EPs have worked very well** — Lumen, Palantir examples
- **Quicker profits, smaller targets** — Adjust for environment
- **Delayed reaction EPs will set up** — Many earnings stocks setting up

### Find Solutions

> "The most important thing in trading is to have the ability to find solutions. This thing is not working. Okay, so what should I do about it? I need to do something about it."

When a setup doesn't work, don't just accept the loss. Find a solution — adjust the approach, change the stop, modify the entry.

### Key Takeaways

- High volatility = good for day trading, difficult for swing
- 9M EPs and delayed reactions are the working setups
- Take quicker profits, smaller targets
- Delayed reaction EPs will set up from earnings season stocks
- Find solutions when things don't work
