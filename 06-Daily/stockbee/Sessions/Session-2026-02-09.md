### Key Lesson: Enter on Hypothesis, Not Confirmation

On delayed reaction EPs, the trigger IS the event. If you wait for confirmation, you defeat the purpose.

> "On Google, when did I short? I shorted in the first one minute. My hypothesis was — what's special about Google? Like Amazon, like Meta, it should sell off. That's when you get a good entry. If you wait for confirmation, there is nothing."

### Don't Balance Trades

> "These are individual trades. How will you balance SNDK with a short? Both can fail. The risk is two times. There is no risk-free way to do that."

No hedging — each trade stands on its own. Both can fail.

### Never Give Up on Delayed Reactions

> "I gave up on Google because if it tried to follow through, it should have followed through. But never give up on a delayed reaction. It might set up way after a long time. You never know."

### Amazon: No Setup

> "Is there any setup here in Amazon by our standard? No. So there's nothing there."

Scan for setups, not tickers. Start with the pattern, not the stock.
