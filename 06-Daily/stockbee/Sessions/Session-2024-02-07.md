# Where Is the Party? — The Barcelona Pickpocket Rule and Four-Factor Troubleshooting

Day three of choppy market. Breakouts still not working. Pradeep delivers two powerful metaphors: **the Barcelona pickpocket** (go where the tourists are) and **the four-factor framework** (setup, process, SA, trader — diagnose your trading problem). Capitalization matters — small-cap biotechs working, large-cap Moderna not. Non-gap 9M breakouts working, gap breakouts fading. Palo Alto Network = the cleanest breakout of the day. He also bought Chipotle (sold 80%, holding 20%) and is actively trading while managing a new house.

## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### SA Framework: Ask Series of Questions

1. **Are breakouts working?** No
2. **What should I do?** Sit out OR trade what IS working (SIPs, one-day moves)
3. **Third option:** Get chopped

### What's Working: One-Day Overreaction to Earnings

- The only thing working = one-day overreaction to earnings (SIPs)
- Non-gap 9M breakouts working; gap breakouts have no follow-through
- Gapped stocks just sit there the next day — Meta, Amazon, IBM all gave up gains
- For every SMCI/NVDA, 100 other stocks doing nothing after catalyst

### Breakdowns Working Better Than Breakouts

- 9M breakdowns having better follow-through (2-3 days) than 9M breakouts
- Primary breakdowns (stocks breaking down from consolidation) = the short setup working
- But not in large numbers — only selective shorts

### The Barcelona Pickpocket Rule

> "If you're a pickpocket in Barcelona, where do you want to operate? Where the tourists are."

- For SIPs, the only question: **Where is the biggest party today?**
- Roblox, Snap = where the day traders are (high volume, big move)
- Doesn't matter if it's all-time high, all-time low, good catalyst or bad
- Where is the volume? Where are the eyes? That's where you go
- Don't pick exotic stocks nobody's watching — go where the tourists are

### SA Question Flow (Every Morning)

1. Are breakouts working? → **No**
2. What's working? → **SIPs (one-day overreaction to earnings)**
3. Where is the biggest party? → **Stocks with highest volume and biggest premarket move**
4. How to trade it? → **Day trade only, sell into strength**

### Visualization: Controlled Risk-Taking

- Pradeep's visualization: "Controlled, controlled, risk-taking"
- In this market: NOT doing "find and enter early"
- Selectively trading SIPs and 9M, waiting for delayed reaction setups
- Many stocks setting up (consolidation breakouts everywhere) — but breakouts not working YET

### Why Is Minervini Bullish? Context Matters

- Consolidation breakout traders see lots of consolidations setting up → bullish
- First leg happened Nov/Dec, now consolidating → VCP patterns forming
- They're right to be bullish — BUT breakouts not working yet
- Need breadth thrust for consolidation breakouts to work
- "I'm also bullish. But the question is: are breakouts working TODAY?"

### TI-65 Indicator: Discrete Information, Not a Setup

- People going crazy on Twitter putting TI-65 on charts
- "What are they going to do with it? Eat it? Put it in a sandwich?"
- No methodology = no setup = no money
- VWAP: works for some, not required for everyone
- "I don't care how you make it work — feng shui, numerology, chicken bones, Zulu — just make it work"

### Four-Factor Framework: Diagnose Your Trading Problem

> "If you have a trading problem, immediately take out the Four-Factor Framework."

| Factor | Question | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **Setup** | Do you understand the setup? | Buying TSM on day 4 = setup problem |
| **Process** | Did you execute correctly? | Forgot stop, wrong entry, didn't take profit |
| **SA** | Were conditions right? | Buying breakouts when SA says they're not working |
| **Trader** | Are you the problem? | Sick, distracted, emotional, taking calls during trading |

- **Setup problem** = majority of issues (9 out of 9 emails Pradeep read were setup understanding problems)
- **FOMO** is NOT the root cause — FOMO comes from setup misunderstanding
- "FOMO goes away when you have setup understanding"
- "If you don't have setup understanding, FOMO is a daily problem"
- **Process problem** = forgot stop, wrong timing, didn't peel
- **SA problem** = trading when SA says don't trade
- **Trader problem** = personal issues affecting trading (sick, distracted, emotional)
- Fix setup understanding FIRST — everything else cascades from there

### "All Stocks Are Pump and Dump"

- When asked about Hello (pump stock): "All stocks are pump and dump. NVDA is being pumped like crazy right now."
- At some point, NVDA will be 70% down — that's the nature of markets
- For trading: doesn't matter if pump or dump — **was there a setup?** That's all that matters

### Low-Price Stock Warning (Repeated)

- Hello-type stocks: 400% move, 8-10% stop required, small position only
- "You will never be able to trade a low-price stock with a 2.5% stop"
- Unless you go intraday time frame and time the entry perfectly

### Liquid Lava = Best Setup in 9M

- Liquid lava shows up every day with massive volume = can size up
- "This game is about moving your account in 0.5% to 2% increments" (multiple trades)
- OR move it in 5-50% increments (EP, 9M with catalyst, delayed reaction)
- Liquid lava, FSP (high-priced stocks), EP, delayed reaction EP = the setups for large moves

### If You Can't Hold Winners: Increase Trade Frequency

- Some people can hold trades, some can't
- If you can't hold → increase number of trades, peel (sell in increments)
- Pradeep peels: sold 80% of Chipotle, holding 20%

### Weak Structure Short Criteria

- Waterfall decline: 10%+ decline over 3+ consecutive days, no bounce
- Member shows MTCH: only 7% decline over 2 days = NOT a weak structure short
- "First leg must be 10%+ with no bounce — that's the criteria"
