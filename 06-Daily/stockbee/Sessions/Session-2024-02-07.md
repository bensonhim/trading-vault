# One-Day Overreaction to Earnings, Barcelona Pickpockets, and Fix Your Setup Understanding

> "Where is the biggest party going to be today? That's the party you go to."

Breakouts still not working. Three options: sit out, trade what's working (SIPs), or get chopped. Pradeep delivers a masterclass on why earnings overreactions fade next day, how capitalization determines what moves, and the Four Factor Framework for diagnosing trading problems. Blunt takedowns of bad stock picks and a recurring theme: **setup understanding is the #1 problem for most traders.**

## AM Meeting (9:00 AM) — Three Options: Sit Out, SIPs, or Get Chopped

### The Three Options Framework

- Breakouts not working → three choices:
  1. Sit on the sideline for conditions to improve
  2. Trade the setup that IS working (SIPs / one-day overreaction)
  3. Get chopped
- "Only a fool would choose option three"

### What's Working: One-Day Overreaction to Earnings

> "What is working? One-day overreaction to earnings. That is what is working."

- Stocks gapping 20% on marginal earnings — then fading next day
- [[META]]: classic pattern — gap up, give up all gains
- [[AMZN]]: gap up, now consolidating (Bezos/ex-wife selling will cap it)
- SIPs = trade the overreaction for one day, don't expect follow-through

### SIPs: Go Where the Party Is (Barcelona Metaphor)

> "If you're a pickpocketer in Barcelona, where do you want to operate? Where the tourists are."

- Today's party stocks: [[RBLX]], [[SNAP]] — where all day traders are concentrated
- SIPs question: where is the biggest party? Go there
- Doesn't matter if ATH, ATL, good catalyst, bad catalyst — where's the volume?
- Low-volume exotic stocks = no volume = no party = no money
- SIPs is a day trading strategy — not a swing

### 9M Breakouts: Non-Gap Working, Gap Not Working

- Non-gap 9M breakouts: some follow-through
- Gap 9M breakouts: sitting there, no follow-through
- 9M breakdowns working better than breakouts
- Primary breakdowns (regional banks) having some follow-through

### Consolidation Breakouts: Many Setting Up, Waiting for Breadth Thrust

- Why Minervini is bullish: dozens of consolidation breakouts setting up
- First leg happened Nov-Dec, now consolidating
- **But breakouts not working YET** — need breadth thrust
- "When the party starts again, will I be alive to trade, or will I have chopped myself to death?"
- Use MDT/MDP displaced to find consolidation setups yourself

### Context Matters: Why Others Are Bullish

> "Why are these people bullish? Because they can see consolidation breakouts setting up. You can find the same thing."

- Minervini sees consolidation breakouts → says "bullish"
- But consolidation breakouts need breadth thrust to work
- "A lot of stocks are setting up" ≠ "breakouts are working today"
- TI65 indicator on TradingView = useless without a setup
- "What are you going to do with it? Eat it in a sandwich?"

### The Four Factor Framework for Trading Problems

> "If you have a trading problem, immediately take out the Four Factor Framework."

1. **Setup problem** — don't understand the setup (majority of people)
2. **Process problem** — forgot stop, wrong stop, didn't move stop, entry/exit timing
3. **SA problem** — trading when SA says don't trade
4. **Trader problem** — sick, distracted, emotional, not focused

- Majority of problems = setup understanding + SA
- "FOMO goes away when you have setup understanding"
- If you don't understand the setup, FOMO is a daily problem

### Capitalization Is Everything

> "Given a choice, you should always look at the lowest capitalization stock."

- [[MRNA]]: 38B cap → needs 4B investment for 8% move — wrong biotech
- Working biotechs: 200M–500M cap → few hundred million to move 25%
- "What's working? Small-cap biotechnology"
- Bill 10: look for capitalization below 10B
- 20% study shows exactly this — small caps dominate movers

### Bad Stock Takedown: Levi (LEVI)

- Up 3 days in a row, multi-year "shithole"
- "The only time this goes up is if aliens invade and order jeans from Levi"
- Focus on stocks likely to MOVE — not dead consolidation

### Setup Definitions Matter

- **Weak structure short**: waterfall decline 10%+ three days in a row, no bounce
- [[MTCH]]: only 7% over 2 days = NOT a weak structure short
- Consolidation breakout requires HIGH VOLUME — no volume = not qualified
- Bottom bounces have low win rate = not Pradeep's setup
- "Am I buying a 4%, or a qualified 4%?"

### Liquid Lava: The Best EP Setup

- Liquid lava = stock that shows up every day with massive volume
- Can trade with big size because liquidity is there
- The game is moving your account in 0.5–2% increments (300-500 trades)
- OR moving in 5–50% increments (EP/9M with catalyst)
- "This game is about moving your account in increments"

### Swing Trading: Where Does the Swing Start?

- Buying on the 4th day of a move = not a swing entry
- Swing starts at the base, not the top
- "God cannot help you if you buy things like this"
