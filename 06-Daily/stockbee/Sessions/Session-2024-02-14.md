# CPI Pullback Is Overreaction, Bull Market Intact, Be Selective in Hot Themes

Market sold off on CPI data. Pradeep's assessment: overreaction, not a market top. No 9M breakdowns near highs. Leaders (NVDA, SMCI) didn't even get scratched. Bull market doesn't top with one bad day. Be selective in hot themes: biotech, technology, AI, crypto.

---

## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Cautious, Not Bearish

> "Will there be a follow-through selling? That's the most important thing."

SA after CPI selloff:
- **Not a market top** — Bull markets don't end with one sudden day
- **No 9M breakdowns near highs** — Leaders intact, damaged stocks were already troubled
- **Overreaction** — Market was due for a pullback, CPI became the excuse
- **Still buying breakouts** — But selectively, in hot themes only

### Market Leaders Unscathed

> "What happened to Nvidia yesterday? Spent rest of the day recovering. What happened to SMCI? Actually going up."

The market leaders didn't get damaged. This is a pullback in a bull market, not a top.

### 20% Study Downside

The 20% downside study shows stocks that are down 15%+ are mostly already-troubled stocks (SIGMA, lithium, solar), not leaders breaking down near highs.

### Duration Bull Market

> "The duration bearish market is over, and we are in a new phase of a bull market which might last three to five years."

No change in long-term bullish stance. Yesterday's selling was overreaction.

### Hot Themes Unchanged

- **Biotechnology** — Best performing sector currently
- **Technology** — AI/semis still leading
- **Crypto-related** — Sugar babies flying
- **Consolidation setups** proliferating — first phase of bull over, now consolidating
