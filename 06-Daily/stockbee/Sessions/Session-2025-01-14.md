## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### SA: Cautiously Bullish for Short-Term Bounce

- T2108: 20.41 → 21.99 (slight improvement but still low)
- Market had 300+ selling day (3rd consecutive), primary and secondary indicators red
- 20% study: 4:1 bearish vs bullish
- TNA/IWM had green day, buying into close (small caps bouncing first)
- Dow turned positive, S&P had positive expansion, Nasdaq closed lower (high-price selling)
- **Plan: slow and deliberate risk-taking on A+ quality reversal bullish setups, sell into strength, peel**

### 20% Study Is an Oscillator — Critical Insight

- **Below 20 bullish**: extreme bearishness = bounce likely
- **Above 100 also bullish**: extreme bearish readings signal washout
- Currently at 20 → approaching bullish territory but not there yet
- **Below 20 would make Pradeep more bullish** (more selling = more opportunity)

### Beaten-Down Stocks Bounce First (Not Hold-Up Stocks)

- Common assumption: "stocks that held up best will lead the rally" — **WRONG**
- When T2108 jumps from 20 to 51+ (recovery), the beaten-down stocks move first
- CVS, MU, KLAC — stocks that were hammered bounce back first
- "Question every single assumption people make in this industry"
- **This is why bottom bounce and reversal bullish are the right setups when market turns**

### Only Two Sectors Matter: Biotech and Tech

- 20% study sorted by sector: **biotech and tech dominate every time**
- "Focus on sectors. Which sectors? Biotech and tech. All the time."
- Day after day, week after week, month after month — same story
- **If you want to simplify your trading, just focus on biotech and tech**

### Capitalization Matters — Below $10B Where Moves Happen

- 20% study sorted by market cap: stocks below $10B is where moves happen
- Small accounts should focus below $1B market cap
- "It's not the size which matters, it's the motion in the ocean you create with the size"
- If small account → laser focus on stocks below $1B
- Large cap stocks (AVGO, Google) → for big moves when market turns

### Small Cap Session Announced

- 5:30 PM session on "Profit from Small Caps"
- Inspired by talking to day traders in Las Vegas who make money shorting small caps
- Pradeep has been doing deep dives on small caps since returning from Vegas
- Plans to create small cap-specific setups with smaller size, more trades

### Logical Spot for Bounce — But Will It Stick?

- Market down 3%+ in <4 days = tendency to bounce (mean reversion)
- "Logical spot for a bounce" after 4 days of selling
- But if bounce doesn't develop or stick → market is really weak
- Bounce would be short-term (1-3 days max), not a new trend
