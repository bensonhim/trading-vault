### EP vs SIPs — Genuine EPs Are Rare, Set Stringent Criteria

> "You're lucky if in an entire year, you can find three or four genuine EPs. I see people having three EPs a day. That is a severe misunderstanding of what EP is."

> "If you start diluting your definition, then when the genuine one comes in, you don't even recognize it."

> "You want to find SIPs which have a 90% probability of going up or going down. Not something which is bullshit, like a Fox News reporter saying this is going to happen."

> "Set objective criteria. Five plus days of short interest. At least three plus analyst price target raises which is at least twenty percent above the current price. For biotech: five best biotech investors, at least three of them holding it."

> "Focus on institutional quality SIPs — 500+ funds or 1000+ funds. Life will become extremely simple."

### 80/20 Peel Tactic — Oversize and Fill

> "One tactic I perfected is to oversize the trade, then take 80/20. I still have my original position in CHWY which is a swing position, but I took 80% off."

> "Oversize and fill. That changes the game."

> "Quantitative traders buy a position, the moment it goes up 2.5% in their favor, they start selling 5%. After that, for every half a percent move, they sell another 5%. This is a tactic used by all professionals."
