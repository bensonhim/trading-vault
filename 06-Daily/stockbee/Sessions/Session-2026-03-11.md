### HIMSS — Don't Pyramid on Broken Growth

> "Once a growth story is broken, it's very very difficult for that growth story to make a second inning. It's like you and me — all the things happen in the first inning of your life by the age of 40. After that, nothing happens. Only thing which is going to grow after this is your weight."

Growth decelerating: 46%, 52%, 77%, 95%, 111%, 73%, 46% → 28%, 6%, 18%, 19%. Short-term rallies possible, but not a growth story anymore. Buying on 3rd day = kissing your cousin.

### Three Rules That Free Your Mind

1. **Don't buy anything up 3 days in a row** — "It's a rule. Whether you agree or not, I'm not going to drink soy milk. That's my rule."
2. **Stop placement** — Move stop to low of day or half of day once in profit. Decide before the trade.
3. **Anticipation setups in the morning, start-of-swing at 3:30** — Adjust timing based on setup type.

> "When you make rules like this, it frees up your mind for 100 other things to do. If you're constantly changing rules, it just doesn't work."
