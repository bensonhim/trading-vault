# Bonkers Earnings Season, Size Matters Below 10B, Hit and Run, Marry a Method

One of the best earnings seasons for one-day overreactions in two years. Stocks gapping 20% then making another 20%, leaving nothing in the tank. Breakouts are not following through. The key insight: 90% of 20%+ moves come from stocks below $10B market cap. Focus on small caps, hit and run, and marry your method.

Stocks like CFLT gapping 26-30%, NXT making big moves then stalling. The EP strategy has changed because overreactions consume the move in one day.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Size is the #1 filter** — Below $10B cap, especially below $1B, is where explosive moves happen
2. **Earnings overreaction** — One-day gaps of 20%+ consume the move, leaving no follow-through
3. **Hit and run environment** — Tight stops, quick exits, highly selective
4. **Breakouts not following through** — Good setups stalling, confirmation scan shows reality
5. **Marry your method** — Commit to SIPs or another systematic approach; stop flirting between methods
6. **Biotech and tech are the only sectors worth tracking** — Consumer discretionary occasional
7. **Short side has no money** — Bears not following through despite big catalysts
8. **AI bubble** — Pradeep predicts 3-5 year AI bubble, early stage
