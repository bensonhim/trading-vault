# Bearish — Get Me Out Market, Economy Slowing, Fed Behind the Curve, Shorts and Delayed Reactions Work

SA: bearish. "Get me out at any cost" market. Early innings of selling. Fed narrative has changed — economy is slowing down, Fed is behind the curve. Broadly, breakouts won't work. ## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Bearish, Get Me Out

> "What you're looking at is a market which is 'get me out at any cost' — that will eventually lead to a lot of selling. We are in early innings of selling."

SA assessment:
- **Bearish** — Get me out at any cost mentality
- **Early innings of selling** — Just started
- **Fed narrative changed** — Economy slowing, Fed behind the curve
- **Broadly breakouts won't work** — Select breakouts only
- **9M EP works independent** — Earnings catalyst is market-neutral

### Strategies for This Environment

| Strategy | Works? | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Delayed reaction longs | Yes | Stocks with good earnings holding gains |
| Shorts | Yes | Many stocks had V1 reaction down |
| 9M EP | Yes | Works independent of market |
| Broad breakouts | No | Too much selling pressure |
| Select breakouts | Maybe | Interest-rate-sensitive sectors only |

### Key Takeaways
