### Fix — The DEP Masterclass

**How to identify a proper DEP:**
1. Stock has a catalyst (earnings, insider buy, clinical trial)
2. Stock makes a move on the catalyst
3. Stock has an orderly pullback/tightness
4. Entry within 5-15% of the high
5. Breakout from tightness = DEP entry

**What is NOT a DEP:**
- Stock already up 40-208% (BW, CRCL) — catalyst is priced in
- Stock up 3+ days in a row (NBIS, Kava) — three-day rule
- No catalyst at all — just a chart pattern

> "After three years of marriage, you want to announce your engagement." — on calling BW a DEP when it's already up 208%.
