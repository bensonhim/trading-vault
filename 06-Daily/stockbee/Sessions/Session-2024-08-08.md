# Choppy Bearish, SA Is Critical — Without It You Blow Up, Day Trade Market, Gaps and Fades Are Typical

SA: choppy bearish. As expected, gap up after yesterday's selling. Highly volatile bearish markets have day-to-day gaps and fades — typical. SA is critical — most people who blow up do so because they have no SA. Swing traders get chopped by a thousand cuts. Want VIX to drop and 3-4 down days before breakouts work well again.

---

## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Choppy Bearish, SA Critical

> "As expected, I said yesterday you might get a gap up. This is the thing about bearish markets — from day to day, you can have these kinds of moves. You can get disoriented."

SA assessment:
- **Choppy bearish** — Gap up after selling, typical volatile bear behavior
- **SA is the filter** — Without it, you go heavy at the wrong time and blow up
- **Day trade market** — Only select breakouts likely to work
- **Gaps and fades** — Typical of volatile bear markets
- **Want VIX to crash** — Then breakouts will work again

### Situational Awareness: Why It Matters

> "Most people who blow up do so because they have no situational awareness. They go heavy at the wrong time, either on the wrong side or heavily invested at the wrong time."

SA is the starting point that guides whether to play aggressively or not. Without it, you're trading blind in a volatile market.

### Choppy by a Thousand Cuts

> "If you are a swing trader here, you may get chopped by a thousand cuts."

Swing trading in this environment is death by a thousand small losses. Day trading is the right approach.

### Key Takeaways

- Choppy bearish — gap ups after selling are typical, don't get disoriented
- SA is critical — without it, you blow up
- Swing traders: chopped by a thousand cuts
- Day trading is the right approach in this environment
- Want 3-4 down days then VIX crash before breakouts work well
