### AI Training Methodology — Detailed

Pradeep shared his complete AI training workflow:
1. Every evening, review 9M EP stocks
2. Create 3-4 examples per day of EPs with catalysts
3. Feed chart + news + catalyst description into Gemini
4. Add "insider buying" as a factor: "Whenever you're doing episodic analysis, look for insider buying which is beyond a certain proportion"
5. The AI now identifies delayed reaction EPs on its own
6. Give feedback when AI calls something an EP that isn't: "This is not an EP. You are calling everything EP."
7. Over time, the AI refines its understanding

> "If you need 2.5cm of toothpaste on your toothbrush, the butler learns that. If somebody else needs 3cm, he puts 3cm."
