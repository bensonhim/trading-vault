### Key Teaching: Get Rid of EMA

> "The day you get rid of EMA on your chart is when you'll start making money. The worm has no EMA. That's why the worm works. The moment you put EMA on it, it will die."

The worm tells you per-second whether buying or selling is happening. It's an absolute number. EMA is too slow for 3-5 day swing trades.

### Breakouts Fading — Don't Chase

> "If something is fading, they're telling you there is no momentum in it. Why are you buying something which doesn't have momentum?"
