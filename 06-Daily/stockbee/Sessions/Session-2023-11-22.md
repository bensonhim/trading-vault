# Pre-Thanksgiving Chop: NVDA Earnings Disappointment, Quick Exits Rule

> "I'm bullish but cautious. Breadth has deteriorated. After the 1,000+ day, no real buying panic — stop and go. Doing 80/20 even more aggressively. Had a very good month — not going to give it back now." NVDA gapped down after earnings, everything got stopped out. Only one position left.

## AM Meeting (9:00 AM)

### Market Regime: Bullish but Cautious — Stop and Go

> "This is a stop-and-go rally. After the 1,000+ day, there has been no real buying panic. Two up days followed by a down day — not the thrust you want to see at the start of a bull market."

**Focus**: 9M EP, continuation setups, SIPs. Not much in terms of stocks in play today.

### NVDA Earnings — Red Flag

> "Most are reacting negatively to earnings. I'll keep an eye on NVDA."

NVDA rallied 23% into earnings, then didn't react in premarket — a big red flag.

### 80/20 Even More Aggressive

> "I was thinking I'd do 80/20 on third day, but now I'm doing it more aggressively. Sell into strength. The game is controlled risk taking."

Very good month so far — not going to get complacent and give back gains.

### Black Friday Risk

> "After Thanksgiving, Black Friday sales figures will start coming in. If they're not good, the market might correct. The market is overextended."

### Story Stocks Not Lighting Up

> "I don't see many story stocks really taking off. Most are breaking out and then getting hammered back into range. The only story that really moved was safety stock (+156%), but that's a real catalyst, not our kind of story."

### Sugar Babies Quiet

> "Sugar babies are not really willing to go out currently. There should be some action in sugar babies, but I don't see any doing anything."
