Stocks beating earnings by small margins (Chipotle, AutoZone, Axon) are better plays than the 20-40% gap-ups. Smaller moves have more follow-through potential.

### Biotech Winter Over

Chipotle: after one-day move, gave up most gains, then broke out again. Delayed reactions are the nature of this market.
