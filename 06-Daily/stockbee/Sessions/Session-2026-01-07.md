## PM Session (3 PM)

### Not Much Actionable: Fading Momentum

> "Not much actionable today. Things break out and things fade."

The morning's breakouts were fading by afternoon. Many 3-day setups losing steam.

### The 20% Study Warning Confirmed

Stocks that broke out on Day 1 were giving back gains:

> "If you don't take profit on the second day, you almost end up giving up all the gains. A lot of these stocks which made two or three day moves, they're actually having a reversal today."

But Pradeep noted the volume was low — the market wasn't taking it seriously:

> "If it was so serious, I would see highest volume. Volume is hardly there."

### Don't Chase Dirty Setups — Again

The PM session reinforced the morning's message forcefully:

> "Select better setups if you want to increase your win rate. That's under your control — not taking shitty setups."

> "Every day you come and ask about some shitty stock — 'is it a good start of a swing?' Is there any learning happening? Or are you just doing the same thing again and again?"

### Second Day of SOS = Don't Buy

> "What is so difficult to understand about this being the second day of a start of a swing? If this is what you're trying to get into after joining and attending meetings, you should just give up trading."

### Google: Will It Hold?

Pradeep was watching Google closely:

> "If Google fades again, I'll close that position and take a day off. Showing signs that it is likely to fade again."

### When to Short: Wait for Range Expansion

> "If you want to go short, you want to see a range expansion like this on SPY. That day, you'll see a lot of 9M breakdowns and sugar babies going the other way. That day, you go short. Not before."

### Key Takeaways

1. **20% study over 100 = pullback warning.** Expect sideways or pullback in next couple days.
2. **Sell into strength.** Don't wait — stocks that ran 2-3 days were reversing today.
3. **Train your procedural memory on clean setups.** TTD-style start-of-swing with volatility decrease, not dirty 3rd-day breakouts.
4. **Google broke out but may fade again.** The pattern has been: breakout → fade. Watch closely.
5. **Don't buy second or third day of a swing.** Members kept asking about late entries — Pradeep was emphatic.
6. **Defense stocks selling on Trump news, but volume is low.** Market isn't taking it seriously yet.
7. **When to short: wait for SPY range expansion day** with 9M breakdowns, not before.
8. **Fix your setup understanding first.** That's the #1 key to profitability.
