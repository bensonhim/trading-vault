# Shift to Caution — 20% Study Screaming Pullback, Reduce Exposure, Lock Profits, Lion and Fox

April 20 marked a tonal shift. After 13+ straight up days, Pradeep turned cautious. The 20% study at 200+ bullish vs ~20 bearish is screaming that a pullback is imminent. He locked profits, reduced overnight risk to under 0.5%, and performed "surgery" on positions not moving. The Machiavelli quote: be both lion and fox. The time to be a lion (aggressive) was the last two weeks. Now = fox mode (cautious). Continuation and anticipation setups still valid, but size should be reduced. EP 9M shorts might work briefly. Extended stocks are most vulnerable. The Instagram husband story continued — people buying 3rd/4th leg moves and shorting CAR. CRDO stood out from anticipation list. Rigetti and AstroLab remain A-category. Irene with 3 tight days. "Some profit is better than no profits."

## PM Meeting (3 PM)

### Cautious — Reducing Exposure, Locking Profits

> "If you're going to be buying anything very extended, or sitting in very extended, probability of getting shaken out or losing money."

> "I'm really, really careful here. Thirteen, fourteen days the market has gone up. Today might be another positive day, but it's too much."

> "The time to be aggressive was for the last two weeks. Now, I'd rather be timid."

Complete tonal shift from previous sessions. Pradeep is locking profits. Reducing overnight risk to less than 0.5%. Performing "surgery" on positions not moving.

### Machiavelli — Be Both Lion and Fox

> "Niccolò Machiavelli said you should be like a lion and a fox. The lion is very courageous, but he cannot avoid traps. The fox can avoid all the traps, but doesn't have courage. You should be a combination of both."

> "At this stage, I would rather be timid. The time to be aggressive was for last two weeks."

Lion mode = last two weeks (aggressive buying). Fox mode = now (cautious, protect profits).

### Extended Stocks Are Most Vulnerable

> "Most important: the kind of stocks which we trade are the ones which are going to get hit the most in a pullback."

> "If you believe there is a train coming at 500 miles per hour, should you stand in front of it and say 'I'm strong, I'll stop it?'"

Livermore quote. Even A-category setups — Pradeep is reducing size. RVMD (A-category anticipation) got stopped out for profit. Some profit > no profits.
