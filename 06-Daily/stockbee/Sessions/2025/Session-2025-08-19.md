### Self-Efficacy Belief — Albert Bandura Framework

> "Albert Bandura studied human achievement his entire life. Success is a function of self-efficacy belief and luck — what he calls fortuitous events or chance encounters."

> "Self-efficacy beliefs are enhanced through five mechanisms: mastery experience (40%), vicarious experience (10-12%), social persuasion, visualization, and psychosomatic state."

> "Mastery experience is the number one mechanism. If you do the deep dive, build your own delayed reaction playbook, write your own guide — that changes your belief system."

> "If people are telling you 'delayed reaction doesn't work, this doesn't work' — that's negative social persuasion. It prevents you from even trying."

### Three Types of Swing Traders

> "There are only three basic types of swing traders: breakout trader, pullback trader, or reversal. That's it."

> "Stop chasing wild ideas. Make one setup work. Whichever one appeals to you — breakout, pullback, or reversal."
