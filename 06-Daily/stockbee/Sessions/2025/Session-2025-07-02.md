# Wait and Watch — Rotation Sustainability Question, High Flyers Hit, and Shortened Holiday Week

Pradeep opened with a clear "wait and watch" posture. The prior day's rotation into beaten-down names raised the key question: would the selling in high flyers accelerate, or was it a one-day rebalance? He noted many third- and fourth-leg stocks had been hit, and if selling continued, the rally was entering pullback mode. The session was brief — he had personal business and would return at 2 PM.
