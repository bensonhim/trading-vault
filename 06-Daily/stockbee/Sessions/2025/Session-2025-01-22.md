# Netflix Earnings, Gap-Up Fades, Singles vs Home Runs, Abhimanyu Lesson

> "Abhimanyu knew how to enter a formation but didn't know how to exit — and he got killed. Same thing with traders. They know where to enter but dream about Oklo going to 100, bikini-clad women waxing their ears. You must have a strategy to harvest profit or exit."

## 11 AM Meeting — Singles vs Home Runs, Meta Can't Get Going

### Two Types of Moves: Singles vs Home Runs

| Type | Account Move | Examples | Method |
|------|-------------|----------|--------|
| **Singles** | 0.5-5% | SIPs, delayed reactions, quick hits | Momentum burst, day trades |
| **Home Runs** | 20-100%+ | EP9M, delayed EP, sugar babies | Swing positions |

- EP9M, delayed EP, sugar babies all fall in home run category
- Current market = singles market — hit and run

- EIX: delayed reaction short

### Sugar Babies: Too Choppy, Wait

### Two Presidents Problem

> "Can you put two swords in the same case? You have a real president Trump and also president Musk. Trump stands with CEOs saying Stargate is game-changing. Musk goes and says that's all bullshit. That leads to whipsaws."

- Policy not settled yet — just showmanship (signing orders)
- Real policy = when bills get signed → that's when real moves happen
- Until then: volatility trades (SIPs) and wait for delayed reactions
- "Multi-day, multi-week moves will happen based on policy changes"
