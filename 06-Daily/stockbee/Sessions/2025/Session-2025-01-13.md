# Distribution Phase, Must-Watch List, Breakouts Have 5% Probability

> "Are breakouts likely to work for the next three days? No. If the answer is no, what is the probability of an anticipation setup working? 5%. Why would I go into an anticipation setup? Make a framework for yourself that allows you to be clear about what you're going to do today."

### Two Phases of Selling: Fast and Slow

> "There's fast selling (institutions say 'get me out at any cost') and slow selling (asset allocation, sector-specific). We are in slow selling right now."

- **Fast selling**: 500-900+ selling days, everything goes down, no buyers at all
- **Slow selling**: institutions reduce exposure by 30%, but sector-specific, not everything
- Currently in slow selling phase — not "get me out at any cost" level
- Slow selling = stocks with specific business reasons decline (MDT, Moderna, chemical companies)
- QBTS, IONQ, RGTI: entered long slow death phase — will go down 50-90%
- Small caps most vulnerable to interest rates and tariffs

### Bounce Probability After 4-Day Decline

- Market down 3-4 days + gapping down = bounce probability high
- But bounces in bearish conditions are **tradable rallies**, not trend changes
- "Every time the market goes down, it bounces back" — but these bounces fade
- SIPs are the only strategy that works in all market conditions
