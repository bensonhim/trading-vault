## 11 AM Meeting — Setup Framework Deep Dive

### Three Types of Bullish Momentum Burst

1. **Continuation Setup** — first leg + <10 days consolidation + breakout
2. **Consolidation Setup** — long base + breakout (needs C+V: catalyst + volume)
3. **Bottom Bounce** — range expansion from bottom (best in market corrections)

### Continuation Setup: 2 Lynch Checklist

> "The stock should NOT be up 2 days in a row. If it's up 2 days already, the move started 2 days ago."

- **2**: Not up 2 days in a row (not literal 0.01% — no range expansion in last 2 days)
- **L** (Linearity): First leg should be linear, orderly buying
- **Y** (Yield/First Leg Size): First leg ≥15%, or ≥$10 for high-priced stocks
- **N** (Narrow/negative day): Pre-breakout day should be narrow or negative — confirms today is range expansion
- **C** (Consolidation Quality): Orderly, low volume, low volatility
- **H** (Closing near high): Stock should close near high at time of buying

### Consolidation Setup: C+V Required

- **Catalyst** required — stocks consolidate for a reason (institutions selling)
- **Volume** required on breakout — new buyers must come in
- Without C+V, only 25% of consolidation breakouts work
- "Institutions sell into breakouts because it gives them liquidity"
- DAL had catalyst AND consolidation breakout — still failed

### Bottom Bounce Setup

- Huge range expansion from bottom required
- Most common during market corrections
- Best in biotech and tech sectors

### Short Side Framework: Three Types

1. **Weak Structure Short** — 3 consecutive down days, down 10%, weak bounce <3 days <2.5% each, not near support. Best on 1st or 2nd leg.
2. **Momentum Burst Short** — short at start of a swing when primary indicator is bearish. Can take in any condition.
3. **Strong Structure Short (Parabolic Short)** — stocks up 200-300%+ in short time. "Either you'll be a hero or a zero." Pradeep doesn't trade these — too risky.

### "Be Like the Chinese" — Reverse Engineer Setups

- Chinese looked at American cars, reverse-engineered them, now make better cars
- Chinese looked at Tesla, now make better EVs (NIO, battery swapping)
- "Reverse engineer some setup idea. Process, process, process."
