# Flexibility Is Your Superpower — Three Trading Time Zones, Continuation Setup Lesson, SOXL Short, Punjab 3-Day Rule

**Last trading day before Labor Day weekend. Market started cautiously bullish (T2108 61, 20% study 8:1 bullish) but sold off hard during the session — semiconductors led the selling.** Pradeep's core framework: **three distinct trading time zones** — morning (NTRT/MTRT/SIPs/EP), 2PM (research breakouts/momentum burst), 3-4PM (reversal bullish/anticipation/delayed reaction watchlists/buckets). **"Flexibility is your superpower — ability to go fully invested to zero with one button."** All morning positions closed for profit or small hits; only SOXL short held overnight. **Continuation setup lesson**: AC is NOT a continuation setup. Real continuation = first leg 15%+/$10+, 3-7 day consolidation, then breakout. Victoria's Secret = perfect template. **Punjab 3-day rule**: everything moves in 3-5 day bursts — "after two pegs in Punjab, you can't walk." **Social sentiment indicator**: "I'm fully invested, just keep buying" = caution. **Market correction more likely than top** — small catalyst can cause 5-6% shakeout when everyone is fully committed to the narrative.

### Three Trading Time Zones — Framework for Working People

> "There are three distinct time frames at which you can make money. Morning — NTRT, MTRT, SIPs, EP. 2PM — research breakouts, momentum burst. 3-4PM — reversal bullish, anticipation, delayed reaction watchlists, buckets."

> "At 3PM, where are you? How many people show up for 3PM? Hardly 40-50. If you're a fisherman and 300 people are at the lake, you're fighting with each other."

> "For working people who cannot do morning trading — you can develop the last one hour. Buckets at 3PM. Delayed reaction EP watchlists. Reversal bullish."

> "If you think AC is a continuation setup, the person on your screen is Brad Pitt. Only difference is the tan."

### Punjab 3-Day Rule — Momentum Burst Pattern

> "Everything works in three to five days. It goes up only for three days. It goes down only for three days."

> "After two pegs in Punjab, you cannot even walk. After two legs, stocks can't continue."

> "Third leg is also rare. Two legs and after two legs, they can't."

> "This is the power of observation. When you start looking at chart after chart, you see this went up only for four days. Then it went down only for three days."

### Social Sentiment Indicator — Caution Signal

> "On Twitter, people are saying 'this is the greatest technology era, I'm fully invested, I'm on margin, I wish I had more money.' When do you see messages like this? Not at bottom. At bottom they say 'this is a Ponzi scheme.'"

> "It takes a very small catalyst for a market to correct 5-6%."
