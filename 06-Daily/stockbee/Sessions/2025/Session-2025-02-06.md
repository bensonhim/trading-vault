## 10 AM Meeting — Palantir, LAES Exit, and Start of Swing Mastery

### Two Types of Trades: Home Runs and Singles

- Home runs (EP, EP 9M, delayed reaction EP, sugar babies) — infrequent but 10-100%+ moves
- Singles (combination bullish/bearish, reversal) — frequent but 0.5-5% moves
- Singles finance the home runs

### LAES: The Sugar Baby That Called

- LAES gapped up 20% — Pradeep sold into strength at ~80% then remaining at ~65%
- > "I sold mine at 50-80%, moved my stop to 65%. Easy peasy"
- This is what sugar babies do: they call, you answer, you sell into strength
- [[SOUN]] — "third day sitting there, no action. What do you do? Just run"
- Don't add to a momentum burst on day 2 — put full position at the beginning

### Palantir: The Delayed Reaction

- Palantir is the primary trade of the day — was on the watch list, broke out from the gate
- It's a **cat** (clear catalyst, 9M volume, immediate move)
- Also qualifies as delayed reaction from prior earnings

### Setup Clarity Teaching: 4% Breakout is the Engine

- Pradeep shows example after example: every stock that made 20%+ in the last week had a 4% breakout first
- LAES had 4% breakout → up 317% after
- Stock had 4% breakout → up 45%, then another 16%
- > "Whether it is 2000 or 2024, there are only two things which are traded: momentum burst and EP"
- The 4% breakout scan + sector filter (tech + healthcare) captures 80%+ of all big moves
