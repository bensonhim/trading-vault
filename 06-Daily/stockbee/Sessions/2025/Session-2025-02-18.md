### FFM: Find Free Money

## Key Takeaways

1. **Evidence-based trading** — 20% study proves every big move starts with a 4% breakout. This is not opinion, it's evidence
2. **Catalyst strength prioritization** — UPST (real earnings surprise) vs. Expedia (normal earnings). Not all EPs are equal
3. **Ralph Lauren vs. Walmart** — if you can't see the difference between a genuine EP catalyst and a normal earnings setup, "everything is an EP" and nothing is
4. **4% breakout is the common characteristic** — if reality changes, adjust. But evidence says: focus on 4% breakout at start of swing
5. **Process over everything** — "If you don't have a process, nobody can help you"
6. **FFM: Find Free Money** — trade the setup, don't think about opinions
7. **Sell into strength** — after third-day exit (worst), selling into strength is the second best strategy
8. **Best reversal bullish entry: before market close** — buy late, sell to morning buyers
9. **Gaps without catalyst fade** — CRDO example: gapped up premarket, faded intraday
10. **UPST is a genuine EP** — holding through volatility, real catalyst, adjusted size for wider stops
