# Back from Holiday, Mind Clarity Required — Distribution Starting, Breakouts Failing, Setup Understanding Is Everything

Back from Turks and Caicos, Pradeep found a changed market: three consecutive selling days, T2108 dropped from 67.59 to 57.73, 20% study at 1:1 (equal bullish/bearish), and more breakdowns after earnings than breakouts. The dominant message: **mind clarity is the biggest challenge in trading**. "If you don't have mind clarity about setup and process, it just doesn't work." Stocks rallied 50-100% into earnings and need something extraordinary to keep going. Very few actionable setups found — SSNC (tech start of swing) the only standout long, with Texas Instruments (delayed reaction short) and Slumberger/Murphy Oil (short side) the cleanest shorts. FOMC day — Pradeep's view: "Fed comes, Fed goes. If there is a setup, take it."

---

## AM Meeting (9:15 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Cautious, Deteriorating Breadth

Judy's essay: cautious, 400+ selling day, T2108 dropped from 59.45 to 57.73, 5-day ratio below 1 (weak buying), 20% study equal weighted (1:1 bullish/bearish), small caps pulled back with negative range expansion.

Pradeep's assessment:

> "The observation I have after coming back and going through things: I see more breakdowns after earnings than breakouts. There is a distribution happening. A lot of these stocks rallied into earnings — they rallied 50%. They need something completely crazy to justify that."

> "Story stocks which are making some moves, they're all giving up their gains. QuantumScape is over. Kohl's is in the toilet."

> "Three consecutive days of selling. The 20% study is almost equal now — 31 stocks bullish, 30 bearish."

### Mind Clarity = The Million Dollar Difference

> "Mind clarity requires insane amount of effort. That is where you will see traders fail — they just don't have mind clarity."

> "If your account goes up, your behavior is right. If you're losing money every day, that's a behavioral problem."

> "Conviction comes from mind clarity. Mind clarity builds conviction. Otherwise, you're not going to have conviction."

> "The million dollar difference between whether you'll make it or not make it is: can you fix your understanding of setup?"

### Not Everything That Gaps Up Is a Delayed Reaction

> "Just because a stock gaps up on earnings day doesn't make it a good delayed reaction. It has to have something which will make you look at this thing."

> "Look at HIMS x-ray versus Amcor x-ray. Does this look like a good earnings? Their probability is minus 13%. They're growing at 4%."

> "You have to understand the difference between a good x-ray and a marginal thing."

### Fed Day = Setup Day

> "Fed comes, Fed goes. Very few times, Fed makes a move. If there is a setup, I take a setup. If there is no setup, I don't take a setup."

> "The insane amount of work required is to convince yourself to trade setups and not random things."

## PM Meeting (2:00 PM)

### Shorts Are Working, Breakouts Are Not

> "Most of the money which I have made in the last couple of days has been on the short side. Shorts are working very well, especially semiconductor shorts."

> "Breakouts are not really sticking. I'm not really interested in long setups."

### SOXL Short = No-Brainer Trade

> "SOXL — range expansion, 9M breakdown after five legs. This is such a clear range expansion. It's telling you: look at me, I might go down for the next three to five days."

> "My entry is $25.53, stop at $26. Risk is $0.47-$1. For that small risk, when SOXL gets going, it can be a 25-50% swing."

> "9M works independent of the market. 30 million shares sold, one of the highest volume ever. You don't even need situational awareness."

### TXN Short = Clean Delayed Reaction

> "Yesterday's TXN short — this was a perfect delayed reaction. Your risk was 0.97%. How much can you put in? A lot more with that kind of risk."

> "TXN is not a stock retail investors go near. Who is selling? This is institutional. Owned by 2,500 funds."

### Find a Catalyst or Don't Trade

> "You have to find a stock which has a reason to go up. If you cannot articulate why this stock should go up, don't buy it."

> "If you're trading something which has a reason — catalyst — then it works. If you don't have a catalyst and you're just buying pullbacks or support/resistance, it's not working."

### Rubrik = Third Day of Swing (Don't Buy)

> "Rubrik is a third day of a swing. I really can't understand why it is so difficult to understand start of a swing."

> "If you buy on the third day, I would rather retire from this business."
