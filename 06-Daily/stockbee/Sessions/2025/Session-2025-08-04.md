### The 20% Study: A Consistent Warning Signal

Pradeep emphasized the 20% study as a key leading indicator:

> "When the indicator goes extreme, within a few days, there is a likelihood of correction. And when it goes 200 plus, there is a deeper shakeout — that's almost guaranteed."

> "This readings also completely correlate with the kind of emails which I get or the number of people attending the meeting."

The study hit extreme readings before every recent correction. Current reading: 59 stocks in 20% bearish (vs normal <10). The 50%+ bullish study also showed stocks that made big moves **starting to break down and slowly turn around**.

### Framework Thinking: Three Questions

> "Are breakouts likely to work? No. Follow through will be a problem."

> "Are breakdowns likely to work? Breakdowns are working."

> "My number one focus is delayed reaction EP."

This is the critical tactical shift: in a deteriorating market, **breakdowns work, breakouts don't**. Focus on delayed reaction EPs.

### Who Is Selling? Institutions.

> "Do you know of any retail trader or your mother-in-law selling? No. So who's selling? Institutions."

> "An institution slowly — this was a very slow selling. The number of stocks 13%+ in 30 days is also turning negative."
