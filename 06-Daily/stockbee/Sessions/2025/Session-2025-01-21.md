- "Should I trade consolidation breakouts at all?" — the real question
- If you did NTRT/MTRT, you'd know the catalyst before the stock shows up
- Multiple failed breakout attempts on same stock = "glutton for punishment" — avoid
- Some will work, but opportunity cost of failing ones is high
