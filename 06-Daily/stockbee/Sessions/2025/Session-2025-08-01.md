# Correction Confirmed, Shorts Are the Play — TXN/SOXL Working, Breakouts Failing, Fix Your Setup Problem

T2108 plunged from 49.79 to 45.31, VIX spiked, 4th consecutive selling day, 5-day ratio below 1, and 690 stocks in bearish combo vs 46 in bullish. Pradeep's verdict: **"Are breakouts likely to work? The answer is no."** The correction has started. Shorts are working far better than longs — SOXL and TXN shorts from yesterday are profitable, and semiconductor stocks are rolling over one by one. The dominant message: **fix your setup problem**. Most traders don't have a setup, or they have one but don't use it. "If you don't have a setup, even God cannot help you." Very few actionable setups — delayed reaction shorts are the primary play. Pradeep took profit on TXN and SOXL shorts, and is waiting for the next opportunity.

> "Whenever I see a stock that has rallied crazily into earnings, why do you think there is a surprise? There is no room for surprise. Your best bet is always when there is neglect and surprise."

### 40%+ Gap Ups = Short Candidates

> "They might have a one-day move, but almost all of them end up becoming shorts."
