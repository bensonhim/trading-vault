### SIP Cyclicity: Every Sheet Flies, Then Every Sheet Fades

> "Sometimes every single one of the catalyst kind of stock is going to work, and sometimes every single one of the catalyst stocks is going to get played."

> "Lot of the SIPs are not really making the kind of explosive moves or continuation moves which they were making earlier, just a couple of days ago."

This is the **cyclicity of SIPs** — there's a period where everything flies, then a period where gaps up and fade. Subtle hints to stay vigilant.
