# Dominoes Falling, Slow Deterioration — Kohl's Joke, Cipher Mining Shines, Find a Reason to Go Up

The melt-up is cracking. One by one, speculative stocks are breaking down — QuantumScape, Archer Aviation, Joby, Red Cat, Sarcos — the dominoes are falling. Pradeep's key observation: "Nothing has reacted positively to earnings." Stocks that rallied into earnings need "beyond extraordinary" results, and most aren't delivering. The best setup of the day was **Cipher Mining (CIPHER)** — a start-of-swing in the crypto/mining theme with clear catalyst. The PM session featured a memorable rant about Kohl's (up 75% on short squeeze) — "guaranteed to go bankrupt." Pradeep reinforced: "Find a reason why your stock should go up. Kava next to my house is not a reason."
