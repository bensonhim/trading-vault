# Bounce Day, Bank SIPs Fade, Lizard Mindset, Singles Finance Home Runs

> "Be like a lizard. Waiting on the rock for your setup to show up. When the big bug comes along, make your move from the gut without thinking." — Ed Seykota

Market bounced after 5-day decline. T2108 jumped to 25.51 from 21.99. Bank SIPs (JPM, BLK, GS, WFC) all gapped up then **faded** — textbook gap-up behavior. Pradeep introduces the **singles finance home runs** framework and the lizard metaphor for patience. Key lesson: SIPs is intraday, EP is game-changing. LLY is a SIPs, not an EP — minor catalyst, not catastrophic. Consolidation breakouts continue failing without catalyst.

### Gap Up Strategy: Only Enter for EP or Clear SIPs Catalyst

- Only two reasons to enter a gap up: EP (game-changing catalyst) or SIPs with clear catalyst
- LLY earnings miss = **SIPs, not EP** — still growing 49%/47%/25%, not catastrophic
- If LLY had said Ozempic not selling, sales growth dropping to 10% — that would be EP
- "SIPs is a day trading strategy. EP is holding for longer period. Is there a difference between a Victoria's Secret model and a Walmart model?"

### The 20% Study Oscillator

- Below 20 bullish = oversold = bounce signal
- Bearish approaching 100 = extreme pessimism = bounce signal
- It's an oscillator — readings at extremes signal reversals
- 20% study consistently shows: **biotech and tech are the only game in town**

### No FOMO Necessary

- "Is it going to be the end of the road? Are there going to be no opportunities?"
- "Nothing is going to run away" — patience is the play
- Wait for low-risk setups, don't chase gap ups
- Ed Seykota's lizard metaphor: "The lizard just hangs around on the rock and waits for his pattern to show up"

## 11 AM Meeting — Singles Finance Home Runs, Conviction Development

### The Trade Factory Framework: Singles, Mini Home Runs, Home Runs

> "Singles allow you to finance your home runs. If you're just going to be trading home runs, you will have an equity curve which looks like this." (draws choppy line)

- **Singles**: 0.5-5% account move per trade, 2.5-5% stops, frequent (3-5 day swings)
- **Mini home runs**: 10-15% account move, wider stops
- **Home runs**: 20-100% account move, 20%+ stops, rare (3-5 per year)
- Singles smooth the equity curve between home runs
- "I'm not a day trader. I'm looking at swings of 3-5 days, 8-20% magnitude"

### EP 9 Million: Turbocharged EP

- EP at best gives 3-5 opportunities per year (5-10 very rare after correction)
- EP9M gives many more opportunities — delayed EP9M, sugar babies expand universe
- Three types of 9M: cats, dogs, and liquid lava (none currently compelling)

### Market Scans: No Confidence

- Combination bullish: 87 stocks. Combination bearish: 99 stocks
- "Do these scans inspire any confidence that the market will run away? No."
- "There is no real range expansion. Just reluctantly forced bounces"
- HOOD: stopped out with 25¢ stop, then it went up — "either wide stop or don't trade"

### How Markets Turn: Breadth Expansion

> "Every time the market has turned, it turns with breadth expansion — 1,000+ day, 1,500+ day. Elephants attacking."

- 2009 bottom: 1,700 stocks up, followed by 1,300, 1,500, 900+
- COVID turn: 1,300+ day
- "When institutions bring buying power, can they hide it? No."
- Current bounce: only 200+ day — not enough for sustainable turn
- "Never say never, but probability seems lower"

### Conviction Development

> "Secondhand conviction is extremely difficult. You have to develop your own conviction by doing the work."

- Trust comes from building and owning your model
- "Why do I trust my model? Because I created it"
- Write down your guide every morning — clarify and reclarify
- Boot camp helps because social learning + repetition creates "eureka" moments

### Specific Setups

- **SoundHound**: bounced but no fresh catalyst — "sugar baby? No, not really compelling"
- **Intuitive Surgical (ISRG)**: consolidation setup with catalyst, but wide bid-ask spread
- **CRDO**: consolidation breakout with catalyst, but needs high volume
- **SMTC**: breaking out from longer range, has catalyst
