### Setup Template Deep Dive (Continued from Yesterday)

- **Continuation setup**: first leg 15%+, <10 days consolidation, not up 2 days in a row, narrow/negative pre-breakout day, orderly consolidation, closing near high
- **Consolidation setup**: long base + breakout, but NEEDS catalyst + volume (C+V), only 25% work without
- **Bottom bounce**: huge range expansion from bottom, best during market corrections, biotech/tech preferred
- "Close your computer. Take a notebook. Draw each setup from memory. Template it."
- "If you can draw it from memory without looking at a chart, you have clarity"

### Short Side Framework (Continued)

1. **Weak structure short**: 3 consecutive down days, 10% decline, weak bounce <3 days <2.5%, not near support — institutional selling footprint
2. **Momentum burst short**: short at start of swing when primary indicator bearish
3. **Strong structure short (parabolic)**: stocks up 200-300%+ — "either hero or zero"
