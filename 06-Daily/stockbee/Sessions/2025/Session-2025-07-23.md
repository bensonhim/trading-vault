### Just Trade the Setup — The Key Message

> "If the market is going to go into a melt up, what do you do? Trade a setup. 9M breakout, story stocks, that's what you trade."

> "If the market is going to crash, what do you do? Trade a setup."

> "The answer is always: trade the setup. Put that time and energy into learning setups. Once you learn the setup, you are completely worry-free."

> "Any retard can learn a setup, but how many people have a setup and actually use it? That is the question."

> "If he reduces the rate, what do you think is going to happen? It's going to be the mother of all bubbles. You're pouring oil on top of a raging fire and then watching."

> "You'll have to bring a wheelbarrow to buy a packet of condoms."
