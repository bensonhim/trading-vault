# Distribution Signs, Speculative Stocks Lead, and Build Your Edge: Setup Clarity is Everything

> "If you don't have a setup, you're just buying random things. If you buy random things, you get run out. Build your edge through probability qualifiers — cap below 10B, IPO within 10 years, first/second leg, catalyst. Each one stacks the odds."

### Four Reasons Trades Fail (Framework)

1. **Setup wrong** — buying the wrong pattern or second/third day entry
2. **Process wrong** — wrong position size, fat-fingered stops, wrong order type
3. **Situational awareness** — right stock, right setup, wrong market environment (e.g., Rubrik)
4. **Trader problem** — revenge trading, ignoring SA, oversizing against plan

- A random momentum burst: 50/50. Add qualifiers: probability increases substantially
- **Most traders' #1 problem: no defined setup** — just buying random things
