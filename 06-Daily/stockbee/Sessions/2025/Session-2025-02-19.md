# Fade City — No Follow-Through, Watch List Only, and the Sugar Baby Short

> "Fee only from watch list. No fee unless it is on watch list. If you chase a breakout in this market, you get into trouble."

Day after the bullish session. Market near ATH but underneath the surface: **fade city**. Breakouts not following through. 4% breakout characteristic unchanged — but execution must be from watch list only, no chasing. The only actionable idea at close: [[QBTS]] sugar baby short. Anticipation setups failing in this market.

### Market: Approaching ATH but Fades Dominating

- T2108 increased from 48.88 to 51.55
- 20% study: 2:1 bullish vs bearish ratio
- But intraday: fades happening more frequently
- > "Something which looks good can fade intraday, and then again, like in a refit"
- Mega caps selling yesterday — rotation happening
- Stocks missing earnings getting "taken to the cleaners"
- Both sides of market active — characteristic of range-bound market

### Must-Watch List: Slim Pickings

1. **DOXOS** (Doximity) — 9M breakout recently, small pullback, nothing great in earnings
2. **VST** — permanent watch list member, setting up nicely, not making a move yet
3. **Rocket Lab** — same story, watching
4. **AVGO** — if clarity on Intel news, could shoot up
5. **Wing** — gapping down, high priced, worth watching intraday only

### 4% Breakout: Still the Only Game in Town

- > "There is no change which has happened. It is still 4% breakout. Stocks are still breaking out with 4% breakout"
- 20% study shows buying coming into market (62 bullish readings)
- But many stocks coming out of lows, not consolidation breakouts
- Higher volatility intraday — Goodyear Tire example: traded then followed through, but fading is constant

### Fee Only From Watch List

- Transitioning more to operating from watch list for better entries
- > "No fee unless it is on watch list. Fee only from watch list"
- Chasing breakouts in this market = trouble
- Tighter stops because you don't want bigger losses in choppy conditions

### Delayed Reaction: Urgency Required

- Good delayed reactions happen within 5-10 days
- > "If it keeps going sideways, sideways, sideways, there is no delayed reaction. It's just a normal setup"
- There should be an **urgency** to get into a delayed reaction
- WGS setting up from yesterday
- SWTX and HIMS had good moves last week — could set up for delayed reaction

### SIPs: Not Much Actionable

- SINT — biotech, US patent for surgical advancements, huge float rotation
- Serve — first green day, autonomous advancements for Shake Shack, but earnings tomorrow (warning)
- Most SIPs = day trade only

5. **Anticipation failing** — skip anticipation setups entirely in current market
