# Choppy Markets Favor Day Traders — Netflix Is the One

> "I 100% believe at this stage that the best is to do day trading here. Or put such a wide stop that volatility won't take you out — but then what size can you do? Very small."

Day two of tariff chaos. Markets choppy — bounce, correct, bounce, correct. Pradeep's conviction: **day trading is the game now**. Swing traders getting stopped out left and right. The one standout swing: [[NFLX]], which Pradeep had flagged since before earnings. Everything else: reduced size, delayed reactions, or pure intraday.

---

## AM Meeting (Situational Awareness) — Volatility Is the Trade

### Choppy Range-Bound Action

- 20% study bounced back after reaching lower levels — still volatile
- Market likely to stay range-bound for an extended period until Trump unpredictability is priced in
- > "The market will get used to unpredictability. After some days, volatility will die down. This dog barks a lot but there is no bite"
- Same pattern as Trump's first term — initial volatility, then markets discount the noise

### Delayed Reactions: The Primary Swing Strategy

- Stocks with catalysts will set up delayed reactions — but not immediately
- [[SPOT]] — extended, then delayed reaction 4-5 days later
- [[NFLX]] — classic delayed reaction pattern on large stocks
- [[LULU]] — earnings, then delayed reaction much later
- Problem: some delayed reactions never give a proper entry — "they just keep going without giving you a chance"
- > "I'm focused on delayed reactions, more low-risk opportunities"

### Day Trading Is the Game Now

- Day traders reporting success — "they can't believe their luck"
- 16+ emails from members saying "I'm giving up swing trading, I'm going to become a day trader"
- > "This has been happening for a couple weeks now. A lot of people have given up on swing trading"
- Intraday moves of $36 (MSTR), $21 (META) — massive volatility
- Pradeep: day trading is "the fastest way to make money if you have the skills"

### Palantir: 22-25% Gap-Up After Earnings

- [[PLTR]] gapped 22-25% — too risky for Pradeep
- > "Where are you going to put a stop on a move like that?"
- Only accessible via after-hours/pre-market — game is shifting there
- Small cap stocks with 4% breakout (sugar baby setup) are different from liquid lava

## Key Takeaways

1. **Day trading is the current game** — choppy, volatile markets favor intraday. Swing positions get stopped out
2. **Netflix is the one** — genuine catalyst, Pradeep's #1 idea for days, capable of $100-200 move
3. **Delayed reactions require patience** — they may come days or weeks later, especially on large stocks
4. **Position sizing matters** — 10% in choppy conditions vs 70% in clear markets
5. **Don't trade broken charts** — if your setup is bouncing on 200M selling volume, change your tribe
6. **Sugar baby ≠ EP 9M** — they are different setups. 4% breakout is sugar baby; 9M EP is for home runs
7. **Volatility will compress** — Trump's first term saw the same pattern: initial chaos, then markets discount the noise
