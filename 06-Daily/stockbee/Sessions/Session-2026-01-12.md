## PM Session (3 PM)

### Trade Factory: Two Conveyor Belts

Pradeep explained his trade factory framework:

**Home Run Factory:**
- EP 9M, delayed reaction EP, DP 9M
- 20-100%+ moves, 3-5 day hold
- Happens 100-300 times per year
- Requires catalyst conviction

**Singles Factory:**
- Momentum burst, anticipation, reversal bullish
- 5%+ account moves per trade
- Happens 1,000-3,000 times per year
- Much easier to find

> "It's much easier to find a move of three to five days. It's much easier to find home runs. Singles happen more frequently."

### NBS: The Best Setup Today

NBS (Nabors Industries) — breakout with earnings, sector working. Best setup of the day. Clear catalyst, proper breakout pattern.

### Chinese Stocks: Skip Them

> "Chinese stocks are so volatile nowadays that I don't really get involved. Wider stops required = smaller position = unhappy sugar baby. I just don't go there."

### Trump Stopped Out Positions — Respect the Process

Multiple positions stopped out in the morning due to Trump volatility, then recovered. Pradeep's response:

> "That is the nature of the beast. You have to respect the process. Every time you don't sell into strength, it comes to bite you."

### The 6% Uncle Point

> "Before I go to any day, I make sure that if all my stops are hit, I'm not going to lose more than 6% of my overall capital. That's my uncle point. I know if I get a 6% loss, I will recover."

### Controlling Yourself: The Real Edge

> "Setups and process are all fine. Can you control yourself? The very fact that you're contacting me in panic means you're overleveraged. You don't have control over your trading. The solution is reflection."

### Bearish Side: Low Priority but Still Scan

### Key Takeaways

1. **Two conveyor belts: home runs vs singles.** Home runs (EP 9M, delayed reaction) happen 100-300x/year. Singles (momentum burst, anticipation) happen 1,000-3,000x/year.
2. **20% study elevated = pause likely.** Bullish >100, bearish <20 signals short-term pullback.
3. **Trump volatility = manage overnight risk.** Smaller positions, tighter stops, sell into strength.
4. **6% uncle point.** Never risk more than 6% of total capital in one day.
5. **Respect the process.** Stops getting hit then recovering is the nature of the beast — don't chase.
6. **SIPs process: 90% from earnings/sales page.** Briefing.com → 39%+ sales growth → catalyst confirmation.
7. **Chinese stocks: too volatile.** Wider stops required, smaller positions, skip them.
8. **NBS = best setup of the day.** Breakout with catalyst, sector working.
9. **ANF = textbook delayed reaction setup.** After 90%+ drop, watch for the bounce-then-fail pattern.
10. **Control yourself > control the market.** Process over panic, reflection over reaction.
