### The Abhimanyu Lesson — Know How to Exit

- **Abhimanyu from Indian mythology: knew how to enter a formation, but not how to exit — got killed**
- Same with traders: "know where to enter, but start dreaming about Oklo building nuclear plants on every island"
- **Must have a harvest/exit strategy before entering**
- "Don't dream about bikini-clad women waxing your ears while the coconut falls"

### If You Don't Have an X-Ray Machine, Don't Do the Setup

- "If you don't have an X-ray machine, don't do the setup that requires an X-ray machine"
- Netflix gap: 29% in premarket, then 13-15% at open — most of move already priced in
- Don't buy premarket just because a stock is mentioned
- Wait for proper buy point or delayed reaction

- **Home runs / mini home runs**: EP 9M, delayed EP, sugar babies — 20-100%+ account moves

- Current market: more singles than home run opportunities
