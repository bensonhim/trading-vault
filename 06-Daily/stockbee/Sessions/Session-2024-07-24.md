## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Bullish, Choppy, Rotating

> "The market is undergoing a rotation. When large caps start selling off, everything gets affected because large caps dominate the index. Then small caps reassert themselves."

SA assessment:
- **Bullish, choppy** — More intraday volatility from rotation
- **Large cap sell-off drags index** — Then small caps reassert
- **499 stocks up 15%+ in 10 days** — Money keeps coming in
- **Back-to-back follow-through** — Small cap move confirmed

### TNA: Volatile but Valid

> "If you get your panty in a twist every time TNA is down 90 cents in premarket, you'll be panty-less soon."

TNA is a 3x ETF — inherently volatile. Don't get shaken out by normal premarket gyrations. Same with SOXX.

### Buying Stocks Up 3 Days in a Row

> "Where is the biggest money? Finding stocks that are up 3, 4, 5 days in a row."

The biggest money is in stocks showing sustained momentum — 3+ days of consecutive gains.

### Key Takeaways

- Rotation causes intraday volatility — not a top, just sector rotation
- 499 stocks up 15%+ in 10 days — massive breadth
- TNA and SOXX are volatile — use wider stops
- Back-to-back follow-through confirms small cap move
- Look for stocks up 3+ consecutive days
