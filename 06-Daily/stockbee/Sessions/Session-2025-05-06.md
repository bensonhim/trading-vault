### 4% Scan Modification Teaching

- For finding start-of-swing stocks, modify 4% scan:
  - Drop V by V1 condition
  - Keep 4% breakout
  - Keep C by C1 > 1.05
  - Keep volume > 100,000 (minimum liquidity)
- "Just 4% alone is enough"
- "Without V by V1, you'll capture stocks that have legitimate start-of-swing moves"
- Example: BioCryst Pharma — 4% move, 3M+ volume, V by V1 not met but legitimate start of swing
- For lower price stocks, volume is absolutely required — "without liquidity, you'll get stuck in dead moves"

### 20% Study — News Drives Moves

- Most stocks in 20% study are driven by news or earnings
- "News is what drives stocks" — catalyst matters
- "The study is designed for home runs and mini home runs, not singles"
- "If the study starts showing consolidation breakouts, I'll start doing consolidation breakouts"
- But currently: most are start-of-swing from weakness, NOT consolidation breakouts
- Study the best performers (like top 10 salespeople) — replicate what works

### Apple Short — Catalyst-Based

### Capitalization Teaching (Continued)

- Small account traders: volume > 100K is minimum, otherwise you're "trading with yourself"
- "If you put 25K shares into a stock with 100K volume, you're going to move the stock yourself"
- Different issues for $30-40K accounts vs $5-10M accounts
