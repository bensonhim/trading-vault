# 83% of Big Moves Start From the Bottom: Question Every Assumption

> Pradeep's definitive proof: 83% of 50%+ moves started from 52-week lows, not highs. Books say buy near highs — the data says the opposite. The third day of a momentum burst is the best day to buy. Setup understanding remains the #1 problem. Not much actionable — market is choppy, brain is foggy from boot camp cold.

## AM Meeting (9:00 AM)

### Market Regime: Bullish, Short-Term Pullback Possible

20% study dropped — when it drops, that's bullish. Breakouts are working but choppy with intraday fades.

### The 50% Study: Definitive Proof

> "83% of the stocks started their move from their lows."

Out of 96 stocks up 50%+ in the last 40 days, roughly 80 out of 96 started from 52-week lows. Not from highs, not from consolidations near highs — from the bottom.

Every single one also had a 4% breakout at the start.

> "Thereby, I rest my case that the only way to become a good trader is to question everything. Question every assumption. Every single assumption."

### Why Books Are Wrong

> "Why is it that it's such a popular thing to focus on stocks which are holding up well? I'm talking to you people. You keep feeding those ducks and believe in those people."

Books say: buy stocks near 52-week highs, buy leaders, buy above 200-day MA. Reality: most big moves start from lows. The majority of traders repeat conventional wisdom without checking the data.

### Sector Rotation: Only Three Sectors Matter

> "The best way to find sector rotation is to focus on three sectors. Healthcare, technology, and consumer discretionary. How difficult is that?"

By the time sector rotation is apparent, the moves have already happened.

### Momentum Burst: Buy the Third Day

Key concept: momentum burst starts with a 4% breakout. The third day is the best day to buy. Sometimes day 5 or 6 is even better because it's confirmed.

> "Buy the swing at the start of a swing. As simple as that."

### HOD: Don't Buy Big Legs

On HOD (Robinhood): it's one continuous leg, 11-16 bars straight up. That's not a setup — that's a short squeeze that will fade. Ideal first leg is 3-5 days.

### Visualization Script

ABC — Always Be in Control. Find and enter early. Sell into strength. Think T+3, T+30, T+90. BTFD. IPO <10, sugar babies, built-in size, FSS, OLCs, stocks in play, FHP, anticipation, 80/20, pill.
