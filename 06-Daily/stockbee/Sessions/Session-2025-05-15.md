### Key AM Teaching: Extended Stocks = Danger Zone

- Market rotational correction happening — money flowing from extended stocks to laggards
- Extended stock definition: stocks that have put in 2-3+ legs (first leg, second leg, third leg = extended)
- "Avoid extended stocks" — that's the biggest thing right now
- Williams Alligator: when mouth is wide open = extended, when mouth closed = sideways
- Similar to Bollinger Bands: stock outside upper band = extended
- Extended stocks getting sold (Netflix, CRWV, etc.)
- Select breakouts and delayed reaction = focus areas
- Wider stops needed in this environment — adjust size down accordingly
- Close stops not working well in choppy market

### Market Condition Assessment
