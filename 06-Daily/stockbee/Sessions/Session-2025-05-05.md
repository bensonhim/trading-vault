### Delayed Reaction = THE Pattern This Earnings Season

- "Almost invariably, they sell off on earnings day, and after the sell-off is when next day they start going up"
- Microsoft: sold off on earnings day, rallied next day
- Meta: same pattern — sold off, then rallied
- AVGO: good earnings, did nothing, second day = real move
- Boston Scientific: sold off earnings day, rallied next day
- Amphenol: same pattern
- HIMS: sold off on Novo Nordisk catalyst day, then started rallying
- "This is a very common thing happening in this particular earnings season"
- "The first reaction on the earnings day is not predictive"
- Key takeaway: don't panic-sell on earnings day sell-off — look for delayed reaction entry
