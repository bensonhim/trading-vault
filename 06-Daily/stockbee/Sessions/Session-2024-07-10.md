# Selective Bull Market Like 2012, Range Expansion Signals, Healthcare/Technology/Consumer Cyclical Dominate

SA reminds of 2012 — up year but choppy with periods of selling. Selective market: not everything works. Be very quick taking profits, lower profit expectancy. Focus on 9M EP, delayed reactions. Range expansion at start of swing is the signal. Healthcare, technology, consumer cyclical dominate.

---

## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Selective, Like 2012

> "This year reminds me of what happened in 2012 — ultimately it was an up year, but it was choppy with periods of selling."

SA assessment:
- **Selective market** — Not broad-based, some things work
- **Be very quick taking profits** — Lower profit expectancy
- **Be very selective** — Not everything works
- **Focus**: 9M EP, delayed reactions

### 20% Study: Range Expansion Is the Signal

> "Stocks continue to move in the direction of a range expansion. At the beginning of the move, you have a range expansion."

Range expansion at the start of a swing is the signal — not on day 3 or 4.

### Three Sectors Dominate

1. **Healthcare** (biotech)
2. **Technology** (AI, semi)
3. **Consumer cyclical**

These three sectors consistently produce the biggest moves.

### Key Takeaways

- 2012-style market: up but choppy — be quick, be selective
- Range expansion at start of swing = entry signal
- Focus on 9M EP and delayed reactions
- Healthcare, technology, consumer cyclical = the only sectors worth focusing on
- Lower profit expectancy in this environment
