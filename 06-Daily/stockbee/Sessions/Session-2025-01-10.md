### "Death by Thousand Cuts" — The Core Problem

- In choppy markets, breakouts look perfect but fade intraday
- Institutional selling detected → buyers disappear → your breakout pulls back
- Each small stop = death by thousand cuts → kills confidence
- Professional traders stay out of volatile markets for this reason
- "I wish I knew this many years ago — I would have avoided so many drawdowns"

### Style Drift Warning — Most Important Teaching

- **Don't switch to day trading** in choppy markets
- Once you adapt to taking profits in 15-20 minutes, you can't hold for 3-5 days anymore
- 98% of traders cannot successfully switch between styles
- Short sellers who get good during bear markets get wiped out when market turns — same problem
- "Remain loyal to your style and accept there are periods when you won't make money"
- Other people on timeline posting gains = temptation = danger
- "Market is always right" — but you must know your own personality
- If you can't switch, don't try — focus on one style and become an expert

### Breakout Trap Explained

- Breakout looks perfect in bull AND bear market — same chart pattern
- In bull market: continuous buying supports the breakout → works for 3-5 days
- In choppy/bear market: institutional selling hits → buyers disappear → breakout fades
- RH, Lululemon, Reddit — all looked like perfect breakouts, all failed last week
- "The same setup will work differently in bull vs bear market — took me two market cycles to figure this out"
- Algorithmic traders detect institutional selling and fade breakouts deliberately
