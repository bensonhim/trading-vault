# Setup Is All You Need — Shorts Squeezed, Breakouts Still Skeptical, Day Trading Stock Selection Matters

AM: Pradeep says **"Are breakouts likely to work? No."** Shorts had been working but many got stopped out on the gap up. Lemonade gave up all gains. Circle IPO is classic "go to the toilet" pattern. 20% study at extreme again. Focus on delayed reaction EP with catalyst. PM: Market held — **800+ stocks in combination bullish**, "much better action." Goldman Sachs and Meta breaking out. TNA first 9M breakout in a long time. Small caps rotating. But Pradeep cautious — not going guns blazing, just taking some exposure. Key lesson: **"Focus on setup — Powell, no Powell, Trump, no Trump. What we need is setup. If there is a setup, we make money."** Day trading stock selection: trade liquid stocks with $3+ range, not sub-$1 junk.

---

## AM Meeting (9:15 AM)

### Breakouts NOT Likely to Work — Again

> "Are breakouts likely to work? The answer is no. What is likely to work? Delayed reaction EP or delayed reaction EP 9,000,000."

> "Just because the stock is extended, it doesn't make it a good short. You need a catalyst. Always look for a catalyst."

### Breakdowns Still Having Follow-Through

> "You see all these stocks are breaking down and also having follow-through. TWLO follow-through. A lot of stocks after breakdown, they're actually having follow-through."

> "60 stocks in your bearish study. If you find even 10, that's really news."

### 20% Study at Extreme — Pullback Signal

> "20% study is telling you that extended a day ago, this happened, results in a pullback. Whenever you see the study gets very extended, there is a period at some stage, there is a pullback."

### Circle IPO — Not Interested

> "Circle is likely to go to the toilet. Most IPOs make a little bit of a move in the beginning, and then they go down 40, 50, 60, 70, 80%."

> "I didn't say anything which was grabbing me by the neck and saying, 'Circle, I should go and buy it right away.'"

### Lemonade Gave Up All Gains

> "Lemonade ended up giving up all the gains. I didn't do 80/20. I entered it yesterday."

> "At some stage, on the total position which I held across accounts, at almost $30,000 profit. And now, I let it come back."

### Shorts Getting Stopped Out

> "All my shorts are getting stopped out today. That's also a signal that the short side which was working maybe has transitioned."

### Setup Is All You Need

> "What we want is setup. Powell, no Powell, Trump, no Trump — doesn't matter. At the end of the day, you're not going to buy Fed. You are going to buy a setup."

> "If there is a setup, we make money. If there is no setup, we don't make money. Focus on setup."

### Day Trading Stock Selection — Range and Liquidity

> "If you're day trading, trade where the volume is, trade where the action is. Don't trade something which is a $1-2 range. I would not trade anything which has a range of less than $3."

> "Good day traders were all over Circle today, or BMRN, or Meta. Those are liquid stocks with $8-10 range."

> "Lower the price, higher is the volatility. You are trading something which is extremely volatile and then complaining that you are getting chopped."

### Airlines — Making Money on Credit Cards, Not Flying

> "Do you know where the airline business makes money? Only on the credit card and the frequent flyer. They are making losses in their operation. Without the credit card revenue, they'd all be making losses."

### Zena Technology — Neglected Story Stock

> "These are the kind of crazy companies which can make a move. Those are the ones you should buy."

### Credo — 9th Leg, Don't Buy

> "This will be the ninth leg of this move. The stock has gone up 2300%. Can this work? Yes, possible. But we discuss this every day."
