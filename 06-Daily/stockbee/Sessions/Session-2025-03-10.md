### Three Catalysts for the Decline

> "A big 45% rally from bottom was due for correction."

1. **45% rally was extended**. Market was already losing steam before correction started
2. **DeepSeek** changed the AI narrative. NVDA gapped down 16% and never recovered. 30+ AI models released since. AI becoming a commodity game
3. **Walmart** signaled consumer slowdown. "Walmart is the pulse of USA"
4. **FAANG capex pivot**. Companies announced $50-100B AI infrastructure spending instead of buybacks. Market saw this as negative

**FAANG Narrative Change**

> "Before, they were buying their own stock, which was positive. Now they're putting $70-100B into infrastructure which may or may not be profitable."

- FAANG stocks never recovered after their earnings + capex announcements
- Zuckerberg's new "dancing style" moment didn't help Meta either
