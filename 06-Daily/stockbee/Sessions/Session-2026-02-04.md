## AM Session (9:15 AM)

### SA: Cautious — 500+ Selling Day, 20% Study Below 20

Market pulled back with 500+ selling day. But 20% study dropped below 20, which signals **high probability of bounce**. Breadth was 326 bullish vs 537 bearish — not one-sided panic selling.

### DeepSeek Moment for Software

> "A Deep Seek moment for software stocks due to launch of Deep Seek. One company launches a product and a bunch of stocks get sold off."

Parallel to past tech disruptions. Software names accelerating to the downside — [[ADBE]], [[CRM]], [[RUB]], [[HUBS]], [[DDOG]] all down 40-50% over multiple legs.

### Missing Earnings = Cockroaches

> "Missing earnings is like cockroaches. If there is one, there are many."

Adobe missed earnings, then selling accelerated. Same pattern across all software names — negative catalyst, bounce, gap down, bounce, gap down again.

### 9M Volume Is the Catalyst

> "Volume itself is a catalyst. On an institutional quality stock, 9M selling = institution selling. That's a clear footprint of elephants."

If you just focus on 9M EP and delayed reaction, you can find these stocks without understanding anything else.

### Rotation: Mega-Cap Tech Stalling, New Sectors Rising

Mega-cap tech (except [[LLY]] and maybe [[GOOG]]) going nowhere. Money rotating into aerospace, electronics, defense, industrial. The party in large tech names is over — money is going somewhere else.

### SNDK: 30 Emails Asking How

### Today's DEP Candidates

From earnings released overnight: [[LLY]] (Eli Lilly), [[SMCI]], [[WCC]] (Wesco), [[CMG]] (Chipotle) — all have potential for 20-40% moves in next 1-2 months.
