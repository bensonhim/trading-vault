# Liquidation Phase, OPM Exodus, and Polish Your Shoelaces

> "This is a liquidation phase. Fast liquidation. Unless you want to get chopped, sit on your hands. Go on vacation. Polish your dancing skills. Iron your pajamas. When the time is right, the bell will ring."

---

## AM Session (9:15 AM)

### SA: Bearish — Multiple Days of Selling, Narrative Change

500+ selling day yesterday. 20% study elevated (bearish >200), signaling potential short-term bounce. But narrative has changed: **AI investment will not pay off** is the new story, similar to DeepSeek moment.

> "Every market move starts with a narrative. AI investment will not be profitable — that's the new narrative. Software is being killed."

### OPM Exodus: Other People's Money

> "If you're doing other people's money (OPM), your first consideration is to not lose money. You only get paid if there's profit. If you're down 20% for the year, you get nothing. So the incentive is to sell first, think later."

Hedge funds and OPM managers are liquidating simultaneously. 10,000+ funds trying to close positions — exits are jammed. Liquidation spiral feeding on itself.

### 20% Study Below 20 = Bounce Likely

Despite the selling, 20% study is bullish. Gap down = third down day in a row. Typically triggers a counter-trend bounce. But washout-style selling has been rare since COVID — quants game the bounce.

### Delayed Reaction EPs Will Emerge After Selling Stops

> "Once selling stops, delayed reaction EPs will start emerging. That's where the money will be. Catalysts will re-emerge once the dust settles."

### Too Late to Short

> "It's too late to short. If you had to short, you should have shorted way early. MicroStrategy — it started going down way early. Now it's an exhaustion move, not a short."
