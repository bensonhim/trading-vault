### Third Day of a Swing — Don't Buy

> "Is this a start of a swing? It's a third day of a swing. What stops you from buying on the first day?"

Same answer every day. You missed it on day 1. Day 2 and day 3 = the move is gone. Buy on day 1 (EP 9M) or don't buy at all.

### Stop Placement — 50% of Day's Range

> "Put it at half of the day. Always."

Clarify, reclarify, reclarify. Same question every day. Stop = 50% of day's range. If all stops get hit, total risk should be manageable. 1% overnight risk maximum.

### Risk Management — Total Portfolio Risk

> "If all stops are hit, what is your risk tolerance? That's how you manage risk."

Add up all stops. If all get hit, what's the total loss? Keep it at 1-6% of portfolio. That's it. Don't overcomplicate.

### Body Count — Track Your Trades

Every trade = one body count. Keep track. SIPs and day trades are Tinder dates — over quickly, move on.
