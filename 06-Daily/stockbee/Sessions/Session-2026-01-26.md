> "Most breakouts are not having traction. They break out and then don't really make much of a move. IWM had an 8% range expansion after a good rally — that should ring caution. Hit singles. If it's not going, we go for singles."

## AM Session (9:15 AM)

### SA: Cautious Ahead of Fed, Big Tech Earnings, Canada Tariffs

Slight red day Friday. Primary/secondary indicators green but with weak breadth — slower buying. Small caps ([[TNA]], [[IWM]]) had sharp pullback, underperforming. Crypto mixed. No aggressive SIPs — taking profits quickly.

### IWM Range Expansion: Caution Signal

> "IWM had an 8% range expansion after a good rally. If this starts having follow-through, then the small caps which were leading this whole thing are in trouble."

Breakouts are not having traction. Many breaking out, then chopping. No follow-through.

### Intel: The Mythical Turnaround

> "Intel went up on mythical belief of a turnaround. Nothing in this shows there is really anything. They're projecting 1% sales growth. Negative sales growth means something doesn't add up."

Intel short is a valid DEP if it gets highest volume. Otherwise, it's a story stock propped up by hype.

### USR: Already Front-Run

> "USR has rallied almost 100% into this catalyst. It's not coming out of a neglected stock. People already knew. Wait for delayed reaction."

### Insider Selling: The Rats Desert First

> "The insider rats are the first ones to leave the ship. 74 people selling in one company. 25 insiders selling in another. They don't know something? They know."

On CoreWeave: "CoreWeave insiders are selling as if there's no tomorrow. Like the ICE people are going to come take away their house."

### NBIS vs CoreWeave

> "NBIS is a better play in that space than CoreWeave. CoreWeave insiders don't really see any hope there."

### Key Takeaways

1. **IWM range expansion = caution.** After 8% rally, range expansion signals potential trouble for small-cap leadership.
2. **Breakouts not getting traction.** Breaking out, then chopping. Hit singles, not home runs.
3. **Intel = mythical turnaround story.** Negative growth, hype-driven. Short candidate on volume confirmation.
