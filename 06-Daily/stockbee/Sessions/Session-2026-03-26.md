> "Unless you have a process, you cannot keep saying I want to find EP this and that."

Use AI tools (Gemini) with specific instructions: restrict to premarket data, find catalysts, parse the data. Don't give it blue-sky prompts. "Garbage in, garbage out."

### No EP 9M Setups Visible

Nothing compelling. Pattern-based setups struggling in this market. Even stocks with catalyst are struggling.

### Keep Looking — One Day the Market Will Start Going Up
