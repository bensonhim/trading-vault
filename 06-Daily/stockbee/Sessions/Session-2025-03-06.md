## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Bearish, Both Sides Whipped

- Essay remains bearish

- T2108 decreased from 28.43 to 25.70
- 20% study leans bearish (1:1+ ratio)
- VIX pulled back. Major indices had range expansion to upside (bounce) then selling resumed
- Crypto stocks bounced with Bitcoin over $90K

> "Both sides are going to get whipped currently."

### Key Themes

**Slow Grind = Chinese Torture**

> "This is Chinese torture. It's too slow selling. Not enough buying. You want a gap down that keeps selling till 2-3 PM. Not this minor gap down followed by reversal."

- Need T2108 to go below 20 for real bottom
- Slow, grinding decline. Not a capitulation washout
- Institutions haven't thrown in the towel yet

**SIPs Always Work**

> "Day traders who actually make money, their number one setup is SIPs. Stocks in play."

- SIPs work regardless of market condition because they have a specific catalyst
- Burlington (BURL) up $32 premarket. MongoDB (MDB) down $51
- AVGO (Broadcom) earnings winner, bucket one stock, high short interest, AI revenue up 77% YoY
- SIPs = day trade setup, independent of market direction

**Day Trading Takes 6-12 Months to Learn**

> "I can't dance, so I'm going to become a ballet dancer. Possible. But your ballet dance will look like nothing close to ballet dance."

- Don't become a day trader just because the market is bearish
- Day trading requires speed, watching charts in real-time, quick entries/exits
- Swing traders have hours to make decisions. Day traders have seconds

**Wider Stops or Smaller Size**

- Good short setups exist (weak structure charts) but volatility shakes out close stops
- Apple: good signal, took out entry, hit stop, then went down. Whipsaw
- Walmart short: gapped down, recovered more than half, then gapped down again. Wider stop needed

**Breadth Is Real, Not a Lie**

> "Breadth is market-derived information. Can breadth lie? If money was really coming in, it would be 800-day, 1000-day, not 200-day."
