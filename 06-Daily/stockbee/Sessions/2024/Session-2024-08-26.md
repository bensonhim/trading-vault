## 3 PM Meeting

### Key Teaching: Slow and Deliberate When Chopped

> "If you are really struggling and getting chopped, slow down significantly. Don't put a trade unless you are 100% sure this is an A-class setup."

Buying early not working? Stop buying early. Context matters. If the worm shows red (bearish), don't force longs.

### Key Teaching: Anticipation Setup Definition

> "My definition of anticipation: NC < 0.25%, 3 days sideways."

MNDY was suggested as anticipation — Pradeep rejected it. NC was 2%, not < 0.25%. 12 days sideways, not 3. Choppy consolidation, not tight.

**If it doesn't meet the definition, not interested.**

### Key Teaching: First Leg Length — 3-5 Days Ideal

> "Three to five days is best. If it becomes a long leg, it doesn't really work. Everything is priced in the first leg itself. Most of the time, stocks with long first legs don't have a second leg."

Long first leg (8-12 days) = everything priced in. Short first leg (3-5 days) = more room for second leg.
