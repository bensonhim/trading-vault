## 11 AM Meeting — SIPs Is a Hookup, Meta Was the Party

### Meta: The Only Game in Town

> "Where was the biggest party going to be today? Meta. That's it."

- [[Meta]]: massive earnings catalyst, 20%+ gap up
- Phone "blowing up" with messages about Meta since last night
- Lollipop opportunity — get in, take profit, done
- [[Deckers]]: also good SIPs but less liquid, wider spread
- [[Amazon]]: catalyst but barely moved — not the best party

### SIPs vs EP — The Hookup Metaphor

> "SIPs is a hookup. You don't ask about values or soy milk in coffee. All that matters is: is there going to be action tonight?"

- **SIPs** = one-day phenomenon, sometimes 1-2 days
- **EP** = long-term relationship (hold for weeks/months, like [[SMCI]])
- SIPs don't need neglect — just need a **powerful catalyst**
- Neglect is an added advantage for SIPs, not a requirement
- Don't confuse SIPs with EP — they are fundamentally different setups
- Knowing the difference = mind clarity about setups

### Where's the Party? The Only SIPs Question

> "Is there going to be a party today, and where is that party going to be?"

- Three questions for SIPs:
  1. Is this going to go up today? (catalyst check)
  2. Where is the day-trade party? (which stock)
  3. Which party has the most energy? (ranking)
- [[Clorox]] = 55+ community bingo party, not the day-trade party
- Technology and biotech = where the real parties are
- Every day there is a SIPs party somewhere — the question is **which one**

### NYSE OPG Order Problems

- [[Deckers]]: OPG order not filled on NYSE
- Market makers won't fill orders they'd lose money on
- NYSE = manual open, market maker controls fills
- Solution: for less-liquid NYSE stocks, use market orders after open, not OPG

### Catalyst Strength Determines SIPs Quality

- [[Meta]]: analysts upgrades, massive catalyst = biggest party
- [[IBM]]: one-day move, then fade = hookup only
- [[SoFi]]: looked like a relationship, turned into a hookup — faded immediately
- "You can put SIPs on Fox News, Instagram, TikTok — people still won't get it"
- Context comes from daily note-taking: which catalysts worked, which didn't
