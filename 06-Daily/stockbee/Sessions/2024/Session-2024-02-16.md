### Classical EP vs 9M Breakout

> "The real money is in 9M. The real money is in finding those dogs. Classical EPs happen 3-4 times a year. 9M breakouts happen every day."

- Every earnings gap is NOT an EP — the term has been bastardized
- EP = find something that will move your account 25-50%+
- Earnings gap = might move your account 2-5%
- People call every gap an EP now — "let them continue to believe that"
- 4% breakout / range expansion has an edge — the question is can you size it?
- "The edge is in execution. The edge is in putting size. The edge is in 80/20 and peeling."

- "Every trader who's successful is constantly creating new edges"
- When everyone starts doing delayed reaction, Pradeep will find something else
- "If you worry about the world, you will never make progress"

### The Edge Is in Execution

- Range expansion has an edge — but can you convert a small edge into a big edge?

- "Ability to tolerate low win rate IS an edge — provided you recover from drawdowns"
- New traders don't care about win rate; experienced traders reengineer their approach
- The whole game: observe, iterate, innovate
