## 11 AM Meeting

### The Core Teaching: Qualified Range Expansion

This was the defining teaching of the session:

> "I'm not trading a range expansion. I'm trading a **qualified range expansion**. A plain range expansion has a probability of working fifty-fifty. Is that enough for you to take a trade?"

Qualification criteria:
1. **Continuation setup** — range expansion qualified by prior trend + two links
2. **Consolidation breakout** — range expansion qualified by CV (catalyst + volume)

> "What setup am I going to buy? Continuation setup. Are there continuation setups today in the scan? Do you see continuation setups?"

Answer: **No.**

### FOMO Warning

> "Why are people wanting to buy something today? FOMO. It's FOMO. When you don't have a setup, FOMO is a beginning trader's problem."

> "If you have trained yourself to think in terms of setup, I have no FOMO because I am going through this and I don't see my setup. So I'm not worried the market is going to go up without me."

### Overhead Resistance Myth — Debunked

> "Every single stock which made the biggest move in November and December — all had the highest overhead resistance. The stocks which didn't have overhead resistance didn't even show up in this list. Either that theory is right or it's wrong — which is likely."
