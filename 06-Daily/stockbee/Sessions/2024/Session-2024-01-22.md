# KLP Day: Setup Understanding Is #1 Problem, Don't Chase Gap-Ups Without Catalyst

> "If you don't have a setup understanding, you believe that you're doing something, but that something which you're doing is — somebody's going to eat your lunch. Even the gods will not be able to help you."

Monday after the Friday bounce. SA flipped bullish (20% study at single digits), but the market faded hard — classic KLP day that sucked everyone in and trapped them. ADM was the only real SIP (CFO fraud, front-page story). Pradeep's #1 message: **poor setup understanding is the biggest problem**, and **don't buy gap-ups without catalyst**.

### Trading Problems Discussion

**Problem #1: Poor Setup Understanding**

- People buying MARA (waterfall decline), PLTR (no catalyst), RIVN (terminal decline)
- "This is a piss poor setup understanding. That is the first fundamental thing to fix."
- If you don't understand setups, nothing else will help

**Problem #2: Not Selling Into Strength**

- Most common problem: enter right stock, it goes up, don't sell, then it comes back
- Solution: **80/20 — peel your position**
- Every professional trader (PCB, Monis, KQ, even Mark Minervini) sells into strength
- "The advice they're getting from 'experts' is exactly opposite: let winners run, don't take small profits"

> "If you want to make money regularly, you should be in stocks like ADM, SMCI. Day trading is the kind of thing which you need to do."

**Problem #3: Buying Gap-Ups Without Catalyst**

- Affirm gapped up 12% with no news → Pradeep sold into strength
- "If there's no reason for a gap-up, you take the money and run"
- Some people bought because they saw Pradeep's boot camp video about Affirm → FOMO, not setup

**Problem #4: Small Account Excuses**

- "I have a small account, I can't 80/20" — wrong
- PCB makes $25-70K with a $35K account by peeling
- "What does 80/20 have to do with account size? Tell me."

### Three Ways to Make Money

> "There are three ways to make money in this market: **Be first, be smart, or cheat.** When you are first, you're going to make money. You're smart, and you also cheat because the other people are going to buy it here."

1. **Find and enter early** (SIPs, NTRT/MTRT)
2. **Oversize the trade** (put large size on high-conviction setups)
3. **Sell into strength** (80/20, peel at every pop)
