## 10 AM Meeting

### Key Teaching: No Thinking — Seconds to Execute

> "If you are thinking, that means you are dead in this business."

Between seeing TGTX in the scan and buying it: **seconds.** No analysis paralysis. No "what does this company do?" No double-bottom-check, no alligator indicator, no moving average scan. Pattern recognition embedded through repetition. See setup → click button.

> "All thinking you do before you decide to trade a setup. After market opens, it's all execution."

### Key Teaching: IAG — Why Did I Buy It?

> "What was I thinking when I bought IAG? Nothing. That's exactly what they don't understand."

IAG showed up in 9M EP scan on Friday. It met the definition. Bought it. No thinking required. It's a 9M EP + sugar baby. Now up 6.54%+. Same scan, same setup, same process.

### Key Teaching: Profit Is Outcome of Process

> "Profit is the outcome of following a good process."

Process loop: run scan → find setup → buy → put stop → manage → sell into strength → repeat. Not sexy. Not what people want to hear. But it's what works.

### Key Teaching: Process Solutions for Everything

> "Every single problem in your life has a process solution."

Want to grow 401k? Process: max out, lemonade strategy, avoid drawdowns. Want to lose weight? Process: workout, diet, cardio, daily. Want to trade profitably? Process: setup scans, execute, manage. Your first process will be inefficient. Your hundredth iteration will work.

### Key Teaching: Don't Over-Analyze

Member asked "is CNK end of a second leg?" — wrong framing. CNK is a start-of-swing continuation setup. That's all. We're trading probability of 2-3 day follow-through. Not predicting legs.

### Key Teaching: Sell Into Strength — IBD Gets It Wrong

IBD's swing trader service buys stocks, they go up 8-10%, then IBD says "add to it, this is a market leader." Then they sell at a loss. If they'd sold into strength at 8-12%, they'd be profitable. Changing that mindset takes years.
