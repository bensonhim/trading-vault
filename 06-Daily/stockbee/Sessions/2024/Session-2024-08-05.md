## 10 AM Meeting

### Key Myth-Busting: Stocks That Held Up Best DON'T Rally Most

> "Stocks which held up best during the correction — do they make the biggest move when the market rallies? No. They don't."

Proven with data across multiple correction periods:
- When SPY corrected 16%, then rallied 6%, the stocks that rallied the most were the ones **beaten down the most** (IQE +144%, GME, etc.)
- The "hold up well" stocks were defensive (healthcare, etc.) — they actually underperformed when the market rallied
- Across 10 different correction periods, the same pattern holds

> "The best trade coming out of this correction will be buying SOXS or TNA at the low. Those will be the trades."

**Bottom bounce setup definition**: Must take out 3-5 days of weakness. Not just a gap-down-and-reverse (that's short covering, not a bottom bounce).

### Key Teaching: BTFD = Short Covering + Day Traders

> "The initial buying is just short covering. People who were short from 2-3 days ago are covering. Like I covered my Tesla short."

The initial bounce is NOT institutional buying. It's short covering and day traders. Real bottom requires:
1. VIX collapsing (not just dipping)
2. Pain, capitulation, margin calls
3. Nobody wanting to buy — opposite of current sentiment

> "I see no one crying currently. Everybody is gloating on Twitter about buying the dip. When nobody wants to buy, that's when you get the real bottom."

### Key Teaching: Don't Chase Tools, Freeze Your Setup

> "16 emails from people pitching me their tools. Do I even respond? No. My trade factory works like a charm. Why would I break something that's not broken?"

Same message as yesterday. Process orientation means frozen tools. Don't change the formula every month.

### Key Teaching: Question Every Assumption

> "First rule of making money in the market: test to see if it is true. There is a lot of idol worship in this industry."

Examples of common myths that are FALSE:
- "Put a stop at 8%" (William O'Neil) — Pradeep uses half of day's range
- "Stocks that hold up best rally the most" — disproven with data
- "Stocks with earnings power rally the most" — ZAP and other no-earnings stocks make the biggest moves
- "Buy on the second or third day" — 97% probability of capital wipeout

### Key Teaching: T2108 Signal

> "The 410(k) signal — when T2108 goes below 20 and you get a breadth thrust confirmation — has never failed. That signal always works."

Wait for T2108 to go to single digits + breadth thrust confirmation. Don't buy early — you'll lose 22% before the real bottom.
