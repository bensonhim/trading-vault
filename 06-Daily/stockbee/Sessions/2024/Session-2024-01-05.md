> "Profit is an outcome of following a good process. Method turns market." After three days of selling, the BTD setup arrived — and the trade factory delivered [[KRUS]], [[MRNA]], [[BAC]], and [[WYNN]] with textbook 80/20 execution.

## AM Meeting (9:00 AM)

### SA Is Setup-Specific, Not Universal

Pradeep delivered a masterclass on situational awareness framework:

> "There is no universal SA. The only question: are breakouts likely to work? Are anticipation setups likely to work? Are shorts likely to work? You want to only trade when the overwhelming answer is yes."

### Setup-SA Matrix

| Setup | Works When | Doesn't Work When |
|---|---|---|
| Momentum Burst | All 4 Market Monitor columns green | Any column red |
| Breakout / Continuation | Bull market + green columns | Pullback/chop (now) |
| Bottom Bounce | T2108 below 20, extreme oversold | T2108 at 60 (like now) |
| Weak Structure Short | Any market condition | N/A — always valid |
| 9,000,000 EP | Any market condition | Catalyst-dependent, magnitude varies |
| SIPs (Stocks in Play) | Any market condition | Day-trade only |
| Shorts near 52-week high | Near turns (currently) | Deep bull market |
| Consolidation Breakout | Later phase of bull market | Beginning of bull (now forming) |

> "WSS works in any market condition. 9,000,000 EP works in any market condition. These are your anchors."

### The Phase Sequence (Critical Teaching)

1. **Bottom bounce** → works at T2108 below 20
2. **Continuation setup** → works in second phase
3. **Consolidation breakout** → works after pullback/chop period (starting to form now)
4. **Consolidation breakout** has terrible win rate because everyone sees them in books

> "Continuation breakout is the low hanging fruit. Consolidation breakout — most tempting, terrible win rate."

### Current Context

> "This particular market has now entered a pullback mode. Something which is near high is the stock which is likely to pull back. That's the kind of setup which is likely to work."

> "I would start on the weekend looking at consolidation breakouts. The party is over as far as I'm concerned."

> "Belief drives behavior. Unless you change your belief, your execution will not change. All this bullshit about Atomic Habits — do you think your habits will change by reading a book, or will your habits change if your belief changes?"

On conviction and 80/20:

> "I didn't even think twice before putting in 5,000 shares of Moderna. My stop was $1. The stock went up $4+. Because I oversized, it changed my overall returns."

### 80/20 and Stop Management Evolution

> "Every year, I keep upgrading my system to put stops which are even closer and closer. Half of the day, or above half of the day — that's my typical stop. My typical stops are 40¢ to $1."
