> "Range expansion is the most powerful signal we have in our arsenal. Stocks move in the direction of range expansion. That's the fundamental principle behind all the trading I do."

News → range expansion → stock moves. Range expansion by itself IS a signal. You don't need an established uptrend.

## 10 AM Meeting

### Singles Factory: The Core Strategy

> "Singles move your account 0.5-5% per trade. Home runs move 5-300%. But singles are findable every day. Home runs are rare. Singles finance the home runs."

Stop types:
- **Singles**: 2.5% or less (sometimes <1%)
- **Home runs**: 8-20% stops

> "If you focus only on home runs, your equity curve will look like this [draws volatile zigzag]. If you focus on singles, your equity curve is smooth. You keep crawling until a home run happens."

### Trade Factory: Nothing Actionable Pre-Fed

- 9M EP: nothing actionable
- Combo bullish: 54 stocks, combo bearish: 116 stocks (far more bearish)
- Red worm dominating green worm — breakouts will fade

> "I don't see anything which is really standing out as something I would go and buy, based on situational awareness and based on what I see in my work."

### Adobe: The Best Setup

> "Adobe is the only thing I would get excited about. It's owned by 5,720 funds. It's a high-priced stock. Last time it did something like this, it went up $29-30. Half a day's risk of $6, potential $25 profit. That's a probability play."

But he didn't touch it because of the Fed decision. Controlled risk taking.

### Key Teaching: Buy Setups, Not Stocks

> "If you want to be a trader, buy setups, not stocks. Is there a setup there? I don't see a setup. But he also doesn't have a setup. He just bought some stock which goes down. Once in a while it works."

Story of a member who bought CrowdStrike, Nike, Starbucks — all broken stocks. Every stock in his portfolio was down. "Buy setups, not stocks."

### Key Teaching: Get Rid of EMA

> "The day you get rid of EMA on your chart is when you'll start making money. The worm has no EMA. That's why the worm works. The moment you put EMA on it, it will die."

EMA is too slow for 3-5 day swing trades. The worm (new highs/new lows per second) is real-time.

### Key Teaching: Start of Swing

> "How difficult is it to understand to buy a swing at the START of a swing? If you buy on the second or third day, you're late to the party. Every single one of those trades was bought on second or third day."

Solution: "If you have a wife or girlfriend, every time you buy a stock on the second or third day, tell her she can go shopping for $500. Guaranteed it will be solved."

## 2 PM Meeting (Includes Fed Press Conference)

### Fed Decision: No Rate Cut, But September Signaled

Powell holds rates at 5.25-5.50%. Key quote:

> "If inflation moves down broadly in line with expectations, growth remains reasonably strong, and the labor market remains consistent with current conditions, then a rate cut could be on the table as soon as the September meeting."

**Translation: September rate cut is almost certain.** The Fed is no longer "data dependent" in a restrictive sense — they're looking for confirmation to cut, not reasons to hold.

### The Trade: TNA 9M Breakout
