## AM Meeting (Situational Awareness) — Best Market in Years

### This Is the Best Earnings Season in Years

- Small earnings gaps (1-3%) are producing better follow-through than 20-40% gap-ups
- [[CMG]] (Chipotle) — small earnings beat, then delayed reaction breakout
- [[AZO]] — same pattern, small gap, big eventual move
- "If you're in the right kind of thing, that's where the money is — semiconductor, biotechnology"

### What's Working

- 9M EP: still the primary screen
- Breakouts: working across sectors
- Themes: crypto, AI, semi, biotech — "after a long biotech winter, everything is basically done"
- SIPs (stocks in play) working very well
- Delayed reactions: many stocks making big moves, then setting up for delayed reaction entries
- Money rotating into smaller stocks — large caps going nowhere

### Short Side: Still Not Worth It

- "Short side is really, really a struggle"
- Even when some shorts work, not worth the time
- "So much money on the long side"

### Seasonality Critics Are Wrong

- "All these people who are crying seasonality — you just had to look at what is happening in the market"
- Don't get too cute — breakouts are working, trade them

### Organization Rant

- Can't find scans in the morning — "how are you going to be geared up to trade if you are not organized?"
- FHP (focus on high-price stocks), TTT (two/three tight days for anticipation), 80/20 (swing tactics), peeling (exit tactics), FFM (find and enter early, sell into strength)
- "I think three days ahead. Think thirty days ahead."
- "Everything which had to happen happened before the age of 30"

### Key Sectors for Big Moves

- Healthcare, technology, consumer discretionary
- Stocks with stories make bigger moves than stocks with earnings and sales
- IPO-10: biggest moves happen in first 10 years of a stock's life
