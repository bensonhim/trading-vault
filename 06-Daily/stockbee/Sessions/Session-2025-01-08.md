# Choppy Market — Day Trading Territory, FuboTV Magnitude Play, Story Stocks Cracking

Day 4 of 2025. Markets in full chop mode — buying, selling, buying all day. No follow-through on either side. Quantum stocks crashing (QBTS -41%), story stocks finally cracking after parabolic runs. FuboTV remains the only conviction position: ~10,000 shares at ~$5.22 avg, treated as a "call option" for magnitude (potential double/triple), not micromanaged. Pradeep's core teaching: **"Treat it like a call option"** for high-conviction catalyst plays with small position sizing. Breakouts failing, consolidation breakouts especially failing. Best environment: day trading. Tomorrow (Thursday) market closed, Monday MLK Day.
