# The Tango Dance: Reevaluating When Buying Fades — Cautious but Bullish

> "It's a tango dance. You have to react to what the market is doing." After 2 days of aggressive buying, Pradeep shifted to wait-and-watch — 80% of stocks making new lows by afternoon. Still bullish, but holding back horses.

---

## AM Meeting (9:00 AM)

### Market Regime: Bullish but Cautious — Need More Buying

Pradeep remained bullish but flagged a key concern: after 2 days of powerful buying, the buying momentum has slowed.

> "You need to see 'get me in at any cost' kind of buying coming in. We had two days of buying and after that, it slowed down."

Historical context: at true bottoms, you see 500-800+ breadth days with complete buying domination. The current rally needs more of that confirmation.

### The FEE Dilemma: To FEE or Not to FEE

> "To FEE or not to FEE. If you don't FEE, the day you don't FEE, it just goes crazy. The day you FEE, it fades after the first 15 minutes."

Pradeep bought [[NVDA]] yesterday via FEE — it went $3-4 from entry, he did 80/20, but the 20% got stopped out. Then NVDA went $6 more later. He also gave [[INTC]] room and got stopped at breakeven.

**Adjusted tactic**: Still doing FEE on quality anticipation setups, but taking 80% much quicker — same-day profit taking rather than giving 3 days.

### Continuation Setup Quality Is Excellent

> "The quality of setups — underlying quality of setups which are forming first leg and consolidating — is very good. That's why I still remain bullish."

Anticipation watchlist has many stocks where buyers are not giving up.

### Leg Quality: Focus on Linearity

> "The leg has to be linear. And there has to be persistent buying. That is what matters. What happened before that doesn't really matter."

---

## 11 AM Meeting

### Trade Factory: Caution Mode — Breakouts Fading

Buying not getting traction. The worm showed selling all morning.

> "I'm not really getting the love which I want to see from the market. I'm holding back my horses."

Key observations:
- Many breakouts are chopping around and fading
- 80/20 strategy: taking 80% faster because breakouts aren't following through
- [[DIS]] — stock in play with earnings, but "I'd like to see a lot more volume"
- [[COIN]] — good breakout, but tempting to take smaller size due to choppy market

### Market Reassessment: Tango Dance

> "It's a tango dance. You have to react to what the market is doing. Three days ago I came in with a bang and bought so much. Here, I don't have the vibe that breakouts are likely to follow through."

### Position Management

- [[AMZN]] — sold (stopped out on profit protection after 3rd day)
- [[NVDA]] — did 80/20, 20% got stopped out, then it went higher
- [[INTC]] — gave room, stopped out at breakeven

## 2 PM Meeting

### SA Validation: The Right Call

> "Was the situational awareness which I worked on in the morning the right thing for today? As a result of that — no damage was done. Money was kept."

80% of stocks making new lows by afternoon. Pradeep did NOT do anticipation entries because of this.

> "That's how you adjust your SA on the fly, constantly. There is no fixed SA. I'm still bullish, but it's a constantly evolving picture."

### [[TSLA]] Shot Trade

Despite the cautious stance, Pradeep put on a [[TSLA]] short at $213.74 with a $1 stop — currently at $207.76, about $6 profit. Process-driven: "Run a scan and find a setup and take a trade."

### [[DIS]] — Delayed Earnings Reaction

Disney potentially a turnaround play with 100M+ volume, but Pradeep won't chase it. Wants to see it develop as a delayed reaction to earnings.

### Key Takeaways

1. **SA is a constantly evolving picture** — no fixed SA; adjust on the fly
2. **The FEE dilemma** — when breakouts fade after 15 minutes, take 80% faster, same-day
3. **Buying momentum needs confirmation** — want to see 500+ breadth days, not just 2 days
4. **Quality of underlying setups is still excellent** — first legs are forming well
5. **Tango dance** — react to what the market gives you; aggressive 3 days ago, cautious today
6. **No damage done** — proper SA preserved capital on a choppy day
7. **Leg quality = linearity + persistent buying** — past choppiness irrelevant
8. **Process over narrative** — "Did I know anything about what was happening? No. All I said was run a scan, find a setup, take a trade."
