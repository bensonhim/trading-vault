### M20: Anchored Momentum for Bottom-Bounce Stocks

M20 (20% momentum from bottom) is the key scan for this market phase:

> "Stocks which were near low are bouncing back — and that is what you need to focus on for continuation setups."

**Debunking the "relative strength leader" myth**: Stocks that held up well are NOT the ones bouncing back. The ones bouncing from the bottom are making the biggest moves. RS ratings are in the toilet for the best setups.

### The Leg Matters, Not the Past

> "What matters is not what happened before. Was the leg very linear? And was there persistent buying? That is what matters."

**Past choppiness is irrelevant** — focus only on the current leg: is it linear? Is there persistent buying?

### Single Setup Focus

> "If you're doing 20 things, following me, following somebody on Twitter, following somebody else — you're jack of all trades, master of none. Invest in one simple setup idea."

### Position Sizing
