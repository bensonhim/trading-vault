### Size Matters — The Key Lesson

Pradeep spent significant time on the most important concept:

> "When it comes to making money in the market, the most important thing is SIZE. Size matters. Concentrated positions. High turnover. Find and enter early. Sell into strength."

The formula: Put 60-80% of your account in ONE swing position. Take 80% profit. Keep 20%. Move to the next trade. This is how you move the account.

### Apple Entry: Missed Opportunity

> "I saw Apple on trade ideas first thing. I should have loaded up 10,000 more shares. Such an idiot."

With a good early entry on Apple, the stop would have been less than 1%. That's how size works with tight stops.

### Don't Re-enter Stopped-Out Stocks

On Microsoft (stopped out, wanting to re-enter):

> "You went on a date. She kicked you in the balls. She threw your underwear down the window. You barely escaped with your private organ intact. And you want to go back on another date? End of story."

### Anticipation Method: Front-Running the Crowd

Pradeep showed an anticipation setup (likely CRWD) trading at ~$90 with the breakout level at $92. He bought at ~$88 with a 40-cent stop (0.44%). The point: enter BEFORE the breakout, not after confirmation.

> "This stock will make it to 300 newsletters tonight. And you know this at 3 PM. It's like knowing which lottery ticket is going to win tomorrow."

### Key Takeaways

1. **SIZE is the secret to explosive returns** — concentrated positions, not 40 tiny ones
2. **Find and enter early** — front-run the newsletter crowd
3. **Don't re-enter stopped-out stocks** — move on, "she kicked you out"
4. **Delayed reaction ONLY after positive earnings reaction** — GitLab has no positive reaction, don't trade it
5. **Self-leadership and conviction** — the million-dollar secret, not indicators
6. **4% breakout at the start of a move** — simple math, not rocket science
7. **Working people need 20% stops** — not 3% or 5%
8. **Anticipation setups = front-running** — buy before confirmation, sell to the crowd
9. **Sell into strength (80/20)** — don't let profits turn into losses
10. **Simple methodology wins** — 9M EP + combo bullish is all you need
