# Breadth Thrust Confirmed: Continuation Setups Are the Low-Hanging Fruit

> "T2108 reached extreme levels below 20, then bounced powerfully to 63%. Every time it goes below 20, it bounces back very strongly — that's the market's nature for 100 years. Now: continuation setups are the lowest-hanging fruit in the market."

---

## AM Meeting (9:00 AM)

### Market Regime: Bullish — Breadth Thrust Confirmed

> "From a severely oversold condition, the market has had a breadth thrust. T2108 went below 20, and whenever that happens, it bounces back very strongly. That's how the market has operated for 100 years."

T2108 progression: 12-13% at lows → now 63% above 40-day moving average. The initial thrust wasn't convincing (700-800 day), then choppiness, then the 1,400 day confirmed.

### Key Framework: First Leg → Consolidation → Second Leg

> "You get a first leg move, then it consolidates. Some — not all — have another leg move. This setup has a name. This is the lowest-hanging fruit in the market: the continuation setup."

300 stocks up 20%+ in 10 trading bars. Now they'll consolidate, and some will form second legs. Focus on those where the **first leg was very good** — not choppy or weak first legs.

### Large Caps Led — Now Story Stocks Emerge

> "Technology stocks and index components bounce first — that's where big money goes. But large caps won't double or triple. Now story stocks will start showing up."

### 4% Breakout → 20%+ Move

Reiterated: every 20%+ mover starts with a 4% breakout (momentum burst). The 4% breakout is the genesis of all big moves.

## 11 AM Meeting

### The "Edge": Red Dress Woman

> "When the red dress woman is on top, you don't put in that breakout trade unless there is a reason."

Pradeep's SA indicator: when the selling pressure indicator (red dress woman) dominates, don't buy breakouts. Today the red was on top all morning — no buying.

### [[MARA]] Stopped Out — and the Abhimanyu Problem

> "I got stopped out first thing in the morning on MARA. I had a stop at breakeven. It happens sometimes."

32 emails asking "what do I do with MARA?" — people married to one trade without an exit strategy.

> "In Indian mythology, Abhimanyu knew how to break into a war formation but not how to get out. Same thing — people know how to get in but have no exit strategy."

### Chinese Stocks: Amazement at the Dysfunction

> "I'm amazed that a country in such trouble, the stocks are not working. That's the problem when you don't have a democracy."

### Don't Chase Gaps

> "That's why I didn't chase all those gaps on Monday. You need to know when to chase and which one to chase. You just don't go and do random shit."
