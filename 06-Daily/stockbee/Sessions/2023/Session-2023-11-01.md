### The Milestone Payment Lesson

A member asked about TGTX as an EP (Episodic Pivot) candidate. Pradeep challenged:

> "Does he understand what is a milestone payment? [...] Is it genuine sales, or is it like a milestone payment from some company which they are in partnership with?"

**Key distinction**: Not all revenue is created equal. Milestone payments (one-time payments from partnership deals) are NOT the same as genuine sales growth. A genuine EP needs **sustainable catalyst**, not a one-time payment.

### Cardinal Health: The Better Entry Was Yesterday

> "The better entry for Cardinal Health was yesterday, before the close. Today it's already gapped up."

**Lesson**: Reversal bullish setups are **anticipatory entries** — you buy before the close, not the next day after the gap.

> "The idea behind reversal bullish is to buy it before the close. And then you don't buy it next day."

A member tried to link NVIDIA and AMD as correlated plays. Pradeep shut it down:

> "Nvidia and AMD — they are not related. Nvidia is a category by itself. It is doing completely different thing from AMD. AMD is trying to get into Nvidia's market. So it's a different story."

**Lesson**: Don't force correlations between stocks. Each stock has its own thesis.
