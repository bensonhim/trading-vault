- **Restoration Hardware**: -32% in premarket

- **Shopify**: drop-shipping from China

### SIPs — Focus on Delayed Reaction Shorts

- EP 9M breakdowns will set up today, then delayed reaction short entries
- Lululemon, RH, Nike = high-priced shorts with room to fall

### Sit in Cash

- Even if a rally starts, there will be many opportunities

### Key Quote
