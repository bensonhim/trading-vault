### Key Lesson: Slow Moves Persist, Fast Moves Mean-Revert

> "Slow deliberate moves tend to persist. Fast-paced moves tend to mean-revert."

- This applies both to upside and downside
- Persistency ranking: stocks that crawl up steadily tend to keep going
- Fast parabolic moves tend to snap back
- Texas Pacific Land kept going up steadily, not spectacularly

### Key Quote

> "Fast moves tend to mean-revert. Something which goes up fast tends to come back. Something which crawls up steadily keeps going."
