# Hit Singles Till 20% — Cougars Over Sugar Babies, Question All Assumptions

First trading day of 2025. Market in slow deterioration — low-price stocks dominating, breadth weak, no real selling but no follow-through either. Pradeep's theme: slow and deliberate, hit singles until 20%, then start swinging for home runs. Two big lessons: (1) consolidation breakouts have very high failure rate without volume + catalyst, and (2) question all assumptions — stocks that "hold up well" do NOT dominate the 50%+ study; big moves start from the bottom. Marvel looked perfect as delayed reaction, went up $2, then faded to breakeven stop. Zim = continuation breakout (NOT consolidation), holding with breakeven stop.

### Consolidation Breakouts: Very High Failure Rate

- **Challenge**: do a deep dive on consolidation breakouts for 3-6 months
- Most consolidation breakouts go back into range after breakout day
- They look beautiful on breakout day, then fade
- Consolidation breakouts work ONLY when: (1) significant volume AND (2) catalyst present
- Without both conditions, they're traps
- VCP / long base / Darvas box — most fail in real market conditions

### Question All Assumptions

> "The most important skill in this business is to question all assumptions."

- Common belief: "stocks that hold up well will lead the next move"
- **Reality check**: do a 50%+ study — it's NOT dominated by stocks that held up well
- Big moves start from the bottom, from stocks in the gutter
- "I also believed that nonsense 25 years ago"
- Books teach consolidation breakout theory — reality shows continuation dominates
- 20% study is dominated by continuation setups, not consolidation breakouts
