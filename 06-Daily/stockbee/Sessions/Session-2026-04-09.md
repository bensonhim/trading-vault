The 800+ breadth thrust was strength, but the 20% study at 100+ says the short-term is overbought. Expect pullback/sideways.

### Strategy: EP 9M, DEP, Sugar Babies — Nothing Else

> "EP 9M, DEP, DEP 9, sugar babies. And if there is nothing there, then I will look at intraday."

> "Just pattern-based trade may or may not work. ... The only pattern which I am interested is in a SOS with tight consolidation or a SOS with reversal."

The tight pants theme continued. Only SOS (Start of Swing) with tight action prior to breakout qualifies. Random breakouts without tightness are "bell bottom pants" — flared, not qualified.

### EP 9M Is the Tiger Balm

> "You got headache? Put some tiger balm over it. Your stomach ache, put tiger balm. ... EP 9M is the solution to a lot of your troubles in life."

The universal answer: EP 9M with catalyst. Palantir had catalyst (war over + retail favorite). Calendar had catalyst. Every good trade traces back to EP 9M. Don't complicate it with VWAP, Fibonacci, relative volume — just run the scan and qualify.

### Context Matters — Not Just Setup

> "Tightness is a context. Story is a context. Catalyst is a context."

UAC showed up in the scan but guided down — twice. Scanning is not setup. You qualify after the scan. Don't buy blindly because something appears in a scan.
