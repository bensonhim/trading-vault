# Catalyst or Nothing — Breakouts Failing, Shorts Thriving, 20% Study at Extreme

Market rallied Friday into Monday, SPY near record highs, Nasdaq surging — but breadth tells a different story. **128 combination bullish vs 449 combination bearish.** 20% study hit 100+ again, signaling exhaustion. Pradeep's stance: **"Unless there is a clear catalyst, don't buy a random pattern-based breakout."** Breakdowns continue to have follow-through. Shorts are working far better than longs. The market is driven by 10 mega-cap stocks propping up the index — "this is like the Nifty 50 era." Lemonade is the cleanest long setup; Whirlpool, Otis, Align, Airbnb, Carrier the best shorts. **"Don't cry — find opportunities."**

---

## AM Meeting (9:15 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Cautiously Bullish but Selective

> "Are breakouts likely to work? Maybe select breakouts might work. I would like to look at something which has a reason to go up or down. Something which has a clear catalyst and not take just a random pattern-based kind of trade."

> "Breakout breakdowns are still working. We have around 40 breakdowns working. A lot of stocks which had missed earnings — they are having follow-through."

### 20% Study at Extreme — Pullback Coming

> "We are reaching extreme in the 20% study. What happens when we reach an extreme in 20% study? You get a pullback."

> "It's always very tempting when the 20% study reaches extreme because a lot of things are going up. But then on the corner, there'll be some sort of a bandit standing there to rob you of the profit."

### Only Buy With Catalyst — No Random Breakouts

> "Unless there is a clear breakout, I'm not going to get involved. Because it has so much drama."

> "Find something which has a reason to go up, short sell something which has a reason to go down."

### Setup Mind Clarity — The Fundamental

> "Setup mind clarity is the fundamental to becoming profitable in trading. If you don't have setup mind clarity, if you're calling this a weak structure chart, you don't understand the setup."

### SIPs Ideas — Cannabis, MU, Lithium

- Cannabis stocks on Trump reclassification news (policy catalyst)
- MU (Micron) — raised guidance, decent earnings
- Lithium stocks (PLL) — CATL closing mines, supply constraint catalyst
- Tesla — robotaxi rideshare license news (lowest priority)

### IMXI Acquisition — No Edge

> "If something is getting acquired at twice its current market cap — why would a stock go up beyond this acquisition price? It will trade at that price till the transaction is done."

### Small Caps Dominate 20% Study

> "Only 8 stocks out of 125 are $10B+ capitalization. The most popular stocks are on everybody's radar, while the stocks which make the biggest moves tend to be very low capitalization."

### No FOMO

> "No FOMO. Wait for setups. They will come in. Patience, my friend. Patience."

> "Short side is working better, at least in my experience, than the long side."

> "Many breakouts are not working. It's not a bad market at all. I don't know why you people are crying so much because you are just long-only traders. You are not even looking at the short side of the equation."

> "Nobody puts any shorts in the SIPs list. If you are a long-only pattern-based trader, and you don't have a reason for a stock to go up or down, then you are going to cry."

## Key Takeaways

1. **Catalyst or nothing** — don't buy random pattern breakouts; only trade stocks with a clear reason to move
2. **20% study at 100+** — extreme reading, pullback likely; "there'll be a bandit standing to rob you"
3. **Shorts thriving** — 449 bearish vs 128 bullish combinations; shorts have better follow-through
4. **Breakouts NOT working** — "many breakouts are not working," only select ones with catalysts
5. **Nifty 50 parallel** — 10 stocks propping up the entire index; breadth is deteriorating underneath
6. **Lemonade = best long** — clean delayed reaction setup with catalyst
7. **Best shorts: Airbnb, Carrier, Otis, Align, Whirlpool** — all delayed reaction with clear earnings miss catalysts
8. **Setup mind clarity is fundamental** — "If you don't understand the setup, you don't understand the trade"
9. **Don't cry — find opportunities** — "Shorts are working. Many shorts are working. That's all you need."
10. **Nobody shorting in SIPs** — long bias is leaving money on the table
11. **Anticipation setups unlikely to work** — market conditions reduce probability
12. **Delayed reaction EP is the focus** — both long and short, with short side having more opportunities
13. **Slow, deliberate, calculated trading** — "Unless there is real reason to buy, I'm not going in guns blazing"
