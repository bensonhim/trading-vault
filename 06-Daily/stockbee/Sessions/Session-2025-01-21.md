- SIPs are the game until dust settles from inauguration volatility

### The Fundamental Question: Start of a Swing?

- **Before every trade, ask: "Is it a start of a swing?"**
- If not a start of a swing → most setups not worth your time
- Then classify: continuation, consolidation, or bottom bounce?
- **If it takes too long to classify → should you trade breakouts at all?**

### Consolidation Breakouts = Sucker's Bet

- **"Consolidation breakout, nothing happened"** — pattern repeated multiple times
- Credo = "damp squib" — tried to break out, failed again
- **Multiple failed breakout attempts** = avoid ("like dating someone who keeps standing you up")
- Popularized by books (CAN SLIM etc.) — that's why they fail
- **Deprioritized in Pradeep's framework** — too many other opportunities
- If you do take one: must have C+V (catalyst + volume)
- Volume requirement: **minimum 2x the 20-day average volume**
- But if you wait for that volume, stock often runs up too much
- **If you did NTRT/MTRT, you'd know the catalyst before the scan shows it**
