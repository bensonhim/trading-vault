- SOXL: +36% then stalled at $8, gave back gains
- EP 9 million: went from 600+ reversal bullish to <100 in 15 minutes, then back to 600
- Market is oversold — 2-3 day pause likely, but beyond that uncertain

### Day Trading Lessons

- Most action happens in first hour — momentum bursts of 3-5 candles
- After first hour, volume dries up, moves become smaller
- Common day trader mistake: make money before 11 AM, give it all back rest of day
- SOXL went up 34-36% intraday — illustrates extreme volatility
- Pradeep's scalps: SOXL in at $7.50 out $7.60, in at $7.88 out at $8.00
