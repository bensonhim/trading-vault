# Bounce Day, Don't Chase — Breakouts Still Won't Work, Wait for Delayed Reaction Shorts

T2108 dropped to 36.95 (5th consecutive decline), 20% study at 59 bearish vs 33 bullish, 675 stocks in bullish combo, 147 bearish. Market bounced after 4 red days but Pradeep's verdict is unchanged: **"Are breakouts likely to work? No. Follow-through will be a problem."** This is a reflex bounce, not a bottom. Shorts still working — TXN and SOXL continued from prior days, ExxonMobil short held with tight stop. The dominant theme: **patience on the short side, don't chase the bounce**. APLD is the only long setup with clear delayed reaction potential. Affirm and ILPT are maybes.

## AM Meeting (9:15 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Cautious, Anticipating Short-Term Bounce

Judy's essay: cautious, anticipating short-term bounce after multiple bearish days. Market monitor had 500+ selling day Friday, T2108 at 36.95 (5th day of decline from 45.31), 5-day ratio below 0.5, 20% study lean bearish 2:1. VIX ascending. New 40% tariff on trans-shipped goods + soft economic data adding pressure. Dow and small cap gapped down with reversal bullish candles — wait for bounce or follow-through.

Pradeep's assessment:

> "Are breakouts likely to work? No. Follow-through will be a problem. This has been the case for the last couple of weeks."

> "The breadth is negative. Things are deteriorating. You can see that in all kinds of indicators, no matter what you track."

> "Institutional selling. Do you know of any retail trader or your mother-in-law selling? No. Who's selling? Institutions."

### 20% Study = Most Reliable Indicator

> "When the reading goes extreme — 200+ — within a few days, there is a likelihood of a correction. Almost guaranteed."

> "The 20% study is at 59 stocks today. Normal is less than 10. This tells you things are slowly deteriorating."

> "If this study goes below 20, I'll start getting bullish. At 33, there's still some buying."

### 50%+ Bullish Study = Short Idea Goldmine

> "If you look at the 50%+ bullish study — stocks that made 40%+ moves — you'll see many have started breaking down. They are slowly turning around. This is a very good place to find short ideas."

### Don't Short Early — Deep Buyers Will Shake You Out

> "There is a structural tendency in the market. Anything which is down, there are deep buyers who try to buy it. So you don't want to be caught shorting something early and the deep buyer comes in and takes out your stop."

> "It bounces and then fades. That's the reason why shorting early is different from the long side."

### Three Ways to Make Money

> "There are three ways to make money in the market: be first, be smart, or cheat."

### Delayed Reaction = Primary Play

> "My number one focus is delayed reaction EP. I see a number of stocks have reacted negatively or positively to earnings."

> "The game has shifted to pre-market. People who trade catalysts, they're sitting there at 4 AM waiting. If you want your lifestyle, you do delayed reaction — you sacrifice the first day move."

### Opening Range Breakout Warning

> "Half of my HEE meeting people are buying opening range breakouts and getting stopped out. Don't buy opening range breakout in this market environment."

> "COVID-19 time was a different time. This is a different time. The market is not the same."

> "Evaluate everything in the context of current market conditions. Don't do things blindly."
