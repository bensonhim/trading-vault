## 10 AM Meeting — Trade Factory Framework

### Trade Factory: Home Runs, Mini Home Runs, and Singles

- **Home run**: moves account 20-50%+ in one trade (rare, 3-5 per year from correction, 5-10 very rare)
- **Mini home run**: moves account 10-15% in one trade
- **Singles**: moves account 0.5-5% per trade (close stops 2.5-5%, frequent)
- **Singles finance home runs** — they build the equity cushion that allows taking big swings
- SA (Situational Awareness) determines how aggressively or defensively to trade
- Without SA, equity curve becomes a random walk — with SA, it smooths into an upward slope
- Pradeep: "I'm primarily looking at swings of 3-5 days, 8-20% magnitude"

### EP 9 Million — Expanded Universe

- EP 9 million = "turbocharged" version of EP
- Regular EP: at best 3-5 opportunities per year (5-10 very rare)
