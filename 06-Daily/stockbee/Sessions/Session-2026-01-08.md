> "The best part of the rally is done. Range stop is reached. We likely transition back into range-bound action."

### The Story Stock Phase Is Coming

This was the key call of the session — after the initial rally driven by real catalysts (AI, uranium, nuclear), the next phase brings **story stocks and lottery tickets**:

> "Spectacular moves in low-price stocks may be the next phase. You're going to get depth-defying stunts — 900%, 1900% moves on story stocks with $300K revenue. Look for stocks with less than 30% mutual fund ownership."

> "DFDV made a 1900% move last year on a stablecoin story — it had revenue of $300,000. Not even $33 million. $300,000."

### Defense Stocks: Most Likely to Fade

Defense stocks gapping up on Trump news (caps on CEO pay, Venezuela threats):

> "Defense stocks gapping up — they are most likely to fade. Same thing that happened to the Venezuela trade."

### Top 100 Stocks: Only 5-6 Participating

> "When I look at the top 100 stocks by capitalization, only five or six are meaningfully participating. All the others are in the toilet or gave up gains after one or two days."

### Google: Upgrade, But Will It Stick?

> "Google has been upgraded. Let's see if it sticks. I don't think it will stick. It will also be one of the stocks which is more likely to fade today."
