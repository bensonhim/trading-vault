# Choppy Selective Market, 4% Breakout Remains the Unit, Earnings Season Starting, Sub-$10B and Sub-$20 Focus

SA unchanged — choppy environment where selective breakouts work. Focus on 9M EP and delayed reactions. Earnings season starting will produce volatility. 4% breakout continues to be the unit that signals the start of moves. Sub-$10B market cap and sub-$20 stock price is where big moves happen. Biotech and technology dominate.

---

## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Choppy, Selective

> "We continue to be in a choppy kind of environment where selective breakouts are working."

SA assessment:
- **Choppy, selective** — Not much changed since last meeting
- **Earnings season starting** — Will produce news-driven volatility up or down
- **Focus**: 9M EP, delayed reactions
- **20% study at 20** — Not many breakouts happening broadly

### 4% Breakout: Still the Unit

> "The one unit of 4% breakout continues to be the unit which tends to show up at the beginning of every breakout miraculously."

Things don't change as much as people say. 4% breakout + range expansion = stock likely to continue in that direction.

### Sub-$10B Cap, Sub-$20 Price, Starts from Lows

Three persistent observations from the 20% study:
1. **Market cap below $10B** — Most big moves are in smaller stocks
2. **Stock price below $20** — Most big moves happen in lower-priced stocks
3. **Moves start from lows** — Not from stocks already going up, but from stocks at their lows

### Sectors: Healthcare and Technology Dominate

> "Healthcare, technology — that tends to dominate the most. And it tends to happen every time."

Biotech/healthcare and information technology consistently produce the most 20%+ moves.

### Key Takeaways

- 4% breakout remains the primary entry signal
- Earnings season = volatility opportunity
- Sub-$10B cap, sub-$20 price = sweet spot for big moves
- Healthcare and technology dominate 20%+ study consistently
