### Short Squeeze vs Sustainable EP: Critical Distinction

**DNUT** and **RRGB** — both pre-market with high short interest. DNUT short interest was 9+ days to cover yesterday.

> "Understand WHY a stock is moving. DNUT was up 38-39% because of 10-day short interest. That's a short squeeze, not a sustainable EP. SIPs one-day moves are different from EP sustainable moves."

Short squeezes: great for day trading, questionable for follow-through. High short interest creates one-day spikes that often fade.
