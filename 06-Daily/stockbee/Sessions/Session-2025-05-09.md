### Two Types of Trades — Home Runs vs Singles

- **Home runs/mini home runs**: EP 9M, delayed EP, sugar babies
  - Stop: 8-20%, profit potential: 50-300%+
  - Move account 10-20%+ in one trade
  - Example: 25% position × 60% move = 15% account move
- **Singles**: swing trades, FHP, delayed reaction
  - Stop: <5%, profit potential: 8-20%
  - Move account 0.5-5% per trade
  - Higher volume, lower drawdown
- Singles finance home runs — "totality of mind clarity"
- Even Kelvin Wong did both EP and swing trades, not just EP
