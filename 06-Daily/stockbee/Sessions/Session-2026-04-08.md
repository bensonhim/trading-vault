# Methods Trump Markets — Trade Factory Empty, Walmart Short the One Play, Tight Pants Still the Theme

April 8 opened with cautious bullishness on ceasefire headlines, but Pradeep immediately framed it: the market never cared about the Iran war in the first place, so ceasefire "optimism" changes nothing. The easy money on the bounce is done — now it's a stock-picker's market where only EP 9M with a real catalyst works. The trade factory was empty in both sessions. Walmart short was the one play. MAG7 money is rotating to small caps. Triple ETF topping signals broad-based derisking. The PM session reinforced: tight action prior to breakout (PCG, MARK) is the only thing worth watching, pattern-based setups without catalyst continue to fail, and "methods trump markets" — your process must work regardless of market mood.

---

## AM Meeting (9:15 AM)

### Ceasefire Optimism — But Market Never Cared

> "The market never cared about the Iran war. ... The market was going up while all that was happening. So what changed? Nothing changed."

Ceasefire headlines created a gap up, but Pradeep was clear: the market was rising throughout the geopolitical noise. The "cautiously bullish" essay was about the setup landscape, not the news.

### Easy Money on the Bounce Is Done

> "The easy money on the bounce is done. Now you have to work harder."

The V-shaped recovery off the lows produced simple profits. That phase is over. What remains requires selectivity and catalysts.

### MAG7 Flat, Small Caps Rotating In

> "MAG7 is flat for the year. Money is rotating to small caps."

The mega-cap names have stalled. The rotation into smaller names is the structural shift to watch — it's where EP 9M and DEP setups with catalysts will appear.

### Trade Factory: Empty Except Walmart Short

### Triple ETF Topping = Broad-Based Derisking

> "Triple ETF topping means broad-based derisking."

When TQQQ/SQQQ/SPXL all show topping patterns simultaneously, it's not a sector story — it's the entire market pulling back. This is structural derisking, not rotation.

### T2108 at 30 — No Signal

> "T2108 at 30 — that's no signal."

The breadth indicator was neutral. Neither oversold nor overbought. No edge from breadth.

### Methods Trump Markets

> "Methods trump markets."

The recurring theme: your process must work regardless of whether the market is bullish or bearish. Don't change your method based on the news cycle.
