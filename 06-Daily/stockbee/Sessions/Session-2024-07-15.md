## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Bullish, Breadth Thrust

> "There's a big breadth thrust. Unless there is a negation of that thrust, it is likely to last."

SA assessment:
- **Bullish** — Big breadth thrust confirmed
- **Money flowing into small caps** — Rotation from large-cap tech
- **Continuation setups will work** — First legs established
- **Earnings season started** — 6-7 weeks of catalyst-driven moves

### Rotation Warning

Semiconductor stocks and large-cap technology stocks might rotate out. Small caps are the new leadership.

### Factor Analysis: What Drives 20% Moves

> "What factors drive 20% move, week after week? It's called factor analysis."

Study the most successful moves (like studying Victoria's Secret models) to find common factors: range expansion, 4% breakout, sector, market cap, price level. Find the factors, then scan for them.

### Key Takeaways

- Big breadth thrust — likely to last unless negated
- Continuation setups will be the primary setup going forward
- Earnings season = 6-7 weeks of opportunity
- Semiconductor/large-cap rotation risk
- Factor analysis: identify what drives 20%+ moves
