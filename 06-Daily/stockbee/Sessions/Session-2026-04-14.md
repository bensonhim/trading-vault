# EP 9M Is Evergreen — Coke in Different Bottles, Theme Over Everything, Don't Chase Salsa Dancing

April 14 was a digestion day after yesterday's explosion. Market up 10 days in a row — a TQQQ record — and most setups already triggered yesterday. The AM session was lean: gap-ups everywhere but fading, no clean entries. Pradeep's singular message: EP 9M is the only setup that matters. It's evergreen. It's Coke in different bottles. It tells you where money is flowing, so the themes reveal themselves. Stop chasing exotic setups you don't understand. The PM reinforced: theme is everything. Only four themes working (Semi, Max7, AI, Oil short). Linearity is as important as EP 9M. And don't be a sucker — the obvious setup is the one most people ignore.

## Key Takeaways

1. **EP 9M is evergreen** — The fountain that keeps giving. Coke in different bottles (DP, Sugar Baby are variations). It tells you where money flows, so themes reveal themselves.
2. **Theme over everything** — Only four themes work: Semi, Max7, AI, Oil short. Expose money to theme. Don't trade non-thematic stocks.
3. **Linearity is as important as EP 9M** — JetBlue EP 9M = craps. Not linear, not predictable. Filter for orderly, predictable action.
4. **Don't be a sucker** — EP 9M is public, obvious, and most people ignore it. They chase exotic setups with no process clarity. Run the scan, find the same stocks.
5. **TQQQ up 10 days in a row — a record** — Market is melting up. Extended. Digestion day. New setups thin.
6. **Peel aggressively on gap-ups** — Standard operating procedure. Oracle up 7% = lock profits. No debate.
7. **Controlled risk taking** — Locked ~50% in 4-5 days on some positions. Size up, then peel. Singles + sizing.
8. **Oil short is the thematic short** — USO, OXY, APA, COP. Iran war over, oil declining. Only short side with clear catalyst.
9. **20% study at 100+ can persist 5-7 days** — Not an immediate sell signal. Can stay extreme. Don't panic.
10. **Story stocks phase begins** — After quality rallies, story stocks fly. AI, nuclear, space, data centers. But story stocks are dangerous without catalyst and linearity.
11. **Beaten-down software = no setup** — No catalyst, no volume, no tightness. "What setup is this?" If it's not EP 9M, continuation, consolidation (with catalyst+volume), or reversal — don't trade it.
12. **Intervention continued** — You must create your own interventions when trading solo. Physical barriers, position sizing, watch lists, qualifying criteria.
