# Bull Market Overshoots, Stay Bullish, Scared Traders Sit Out While Veterans Make Money

SA bullish — pent-up demand wiping out $9.42 drop in two days. Every dip bought. Scared traders bearish while veterans making money. Bull markets overshoot, especially after 1.5-year bearish period. SMCI parabolic move 90% probability done. Google lost the plot. Trade Factory is process-driven discretionary.

## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Bullish, Pent-Up Demand

> "There is such a pent up demand that $9.42 got wiped out in just two days."

SA assessment:
- **Bullish** — Every dip getting bought
- **Pent-up demand** — Massive buying under the surface
- **Scared traders bearish** — Sitting out, then regretting
- **Veteran traders making money** — Staying bullish in bull market is hard but necessary

### Duration Bull Market

> "Bull markets tend to overshoot. When you have a long duration bearish market of almost one and a half years, when it is over, you tend to get bull markets which last three to five years."

Key insight: Anchored thinking from the bear phase keeps traders scared. Data history shows bearish markets are followed by 3-5 year bull markets.

### Staying Bullish Is Hard

> "Staying bullish in a bullish market is a very difficult thing."

Traders who are scared always find reasons to justify their fear (double tops, Fibonacci extensions, etc.). The market doesn't care about those excuses.
