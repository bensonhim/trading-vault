### Biotech Scam Warning

- 98-99% of biotech companies will NEVER get their drug approved
- Biotech "earnings" = someone gave them money for testing/joint development
- Not sustainable, not real earnings
- "Dilute, dilute, dilute, dilute" — the biotech business model
- Insiders get stock options, immediately sell, rinse and repeat
- Only 4-5 biotech companies ever truly succeeded: Amgen, BIIB, Gilead, Genentech
- "Buying biotech in anticipation of earnings is not my idea — I have no suicidal tendencies"
- Valeant/Vanda example: same schizophrenia drug story recycled for years
