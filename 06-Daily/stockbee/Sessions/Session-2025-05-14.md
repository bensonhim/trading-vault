### Key AM Teaching: Trade Factory Framework (Home Runs vs Singles)

- **Two conveyor belts in trade factory:**
  1. **Home runs / mini home runs** — move account 10-50-100% in one trade
  2. **Singles** — move account 0.5-5% in one trade, 3-5 day holding period
- Home runs use: EP 9M, delayed reaction EP 9M, sugar babies, bucket 0/1/2
- Singles use: start of swing, continuation, consolidation, bottom bounce patterns
- **Singles finance home runs** — if you take 8-20% stops on home runs, singles cash flow allows recovery
- **All successful traders do combination approach** — hit singles, occasionally hit home runs
- "Magnitude moves vs duration moves" — do you want 100% in one year or 100% in few days?
- HIMS = mini home run (60% in few days)
- **Common element of ALL single strategies: buy on day of breakout** — never buy 2nd, 3rd, 4th day
- **Start of swing is the most important concept** — without understanding it, "better options than trading exist"
- If you can't understand start of swing, trading may not be for you
