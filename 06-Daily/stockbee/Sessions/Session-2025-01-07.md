### 50% Study — What Actually Makes Big Moves

- Examined stocks with biggest moves over last 10 days, 1 month, 1 year, 3 years, 5 years
- 1-month movers: dominated by **story stocks** (AI, quantum, robotaxis, robotics) — NOT biotech
- 1-year movers: stories still dominate, but some real sales (Nvidia, AMD, Shopify, Axon)
- 5-year movers: dominated by real sales growth — Nvidia, MercadoLibre, Axon
- **Key insight**: most 20% movers are NOT consolidation breakouts — they start from **bottom bounces** or **continuations**

### Why Consolidation Breakouts Fail

- "Stocks go into consolidation for a reason" — institutional distribution
- When buyers come in, institutions sell into the rally
- 75% of consolidation breakouts fail — only 20-25% work
- Yet everyone is "sold on consolidation breakout" — that's the trap
- The reason: no buying power, no catalyst, just sideways drifting
- Only works with **tower of volume** AND **fresh catalyst**

### Dating Analogy for Qualifying Setups

- 5 million women on Tinder — you don't date randomly
- You set criteria: age, height, education, language, weight — that's **qualification**
- Same with stocks: every setup type has its own qualifying criteria
- Continuation: first leg 15%+, pullback 3-10 days
- EP: Magna 53, Cap 10×10
- Cat/Dog: start of swing + plausible story
- **More qualifying criteria = higher probability**

### Key Concepts Repeated

- **Linearity**: stocks that make big moves tend to have **higher linearity** (orderly price action)
- Choppy stocks = no aggressive buying, avoid
- **Cougar** ($60+): focus on these, not sugar babies
- **IPO 10**: IPO'd in last 10 years — #1 focus
- **Cap below $10B**: size matters
- **FSS** (Sales Stars): for longer-term holds, real sales matter
- **BBT**: big bang volume at start of move
- **SIPs** (stocks in play): for day traders, good daily income strategy
- **80/20**: sell 80% into strength, keep 20%
- **Objective**: find free money, swing 3-5 days, 8-20% moves
