### Major Teaching: Start of a Swing = THE Fundamental Entry

- **"Start of a swing is the fundamental concept of trading — no matter the timeframe"**
- Best entry = anticipation (if you can predict the move)
- Second best = start of a swing (SOS)
- Entering second day = poor trade, "you should not be even thinking about trading"
- "The stock that gives you entry on second day at a lower price is a poor stock anyway"
- Stop placement: at the low of the start-of-swing day
- "If start of swing is negated, the trade is no longer valid"
- UVIX example: time to buy was at start of swing, not third day of move
- "This is not my invention — this is basic to all trading"
- CRWV as day trade: enter at start of swing, stop at low of day
