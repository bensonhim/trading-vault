### The First Question: Will Deep Buyers Show Up?

> "Will the buyers show up? Let's see if the buyers show up."

Answer: **YES.** Within minutes of open, deep buyers raced in. Gap down → immediate recovery. No panic selling. No one-sided selling.

### Market Refuses to Break Down — Again

> "There is no real selling which will clear the way for a tradable bounce. This has been a range-bound market still rewarding stocks with catalyst."

Went through 160+ stocks owned by 2,500+ funds. Vast majority holding up. Only 10-15% in correction — and those have **specific stock catalysts** (mergers, slow sales, high mortgage rates for homebuilders). Not broad-based selling.

### OPM Selling = Algorithmic Buying

> "This is why the market behaves differently on the downside. The downside is dominated by people handling other people's money (OPM). When panic sets in, OPMs sell first."

But algorithms keep buying weakness. Gap downs get bought immediately. The deep buyers are not giving up.

### Google Stopped Out

> "I gave Google a little bit more room to run, and today had to take it at a loss. If I had been very ruthless and taken it at half the day, that would have been less."

Lesson: **in choppy markets, don't give extra room.** Ruthless stops save money.
