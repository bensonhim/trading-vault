Same as yesterday — Japan carry trade unwinding + Trump/Greenland/trade war escalation. But Pradeep's take:

> "The Europeans will come growling, bring some Irish butter, butter him, and this will get resolved."

### One Job: Find Dollars

> "I only define my job as one thing: no matter what the circumstances, to find dollars. Trump is not giving us $100K a year free healthcare. We are not Greenlanders. We have to make our own fortune."

### Tesla: First Stock on Trade Ideas

> "Whenever I look at something, I look at history. RIG has had many 9M EPs, but every time it just doesn't have the history of following through. Just because it shows up in a scan, you don't get involved."

### Under Armour: No Catalyst, No Story

> "Is there a catalyst? It's not a story stock. Is there a hedge fund manager pumping this? Do you see anything in earnings or sales? No reason to get excited. It might work, but it doesn't make sense."

### Don't Short Gold and Silver

> "Gold and silver might work as shorts, but I don't have those suicidal tendencies."

### Key Takeaways

1. **Setup clarity is 98% of the challenge.** If you don't understand the setup, you're guessing.
2. **EP requires neglect.** A stock up 300% is not neglected — it's not an EP.
3. **Trump is noise. Setups are signal.** Buy the setup, ignore the drama.
4. **400+ red day is a shakeout, not panic.** No triple ETFs dominating = no real fear.
5. **Breakdowns working on follow-through.** DEP shorts on credit card cap names are valid.
6. **Know the history.** RIG shows up in scans but never follows through — skip it.
7. **No catalyst = no trade.** Under Armour has no story — pass even if scan shows it.
8. **Selective setups will work.** ULTA, continuation plays, government policy DEP — focus on these.
