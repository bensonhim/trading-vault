# Greenland Drama, Japan Carry Trade, and Respect Your Stops

> "Respect stops and look for opportunities. The Japanese carry trade unwinding is much bigger than Greenland drama. But ultimately, we're just useless people looking for dollars."

---

## AM Session (9:15 AM)

### SA: Cautious — Headline Risk Escalates

Another green day Friday but with deteriorating margins. 20% study at 3:1 bullish/bearish. Dow, Nasdaq, S&P all pulled back red. Small caps marginally higher. Breakouts unlikely to work.

### Two Forces Driving Markets Down

1. **Greenland/trade drama** — Trump threatening to buy Greenland, tariff escalation
2. **Japan carry trade unwinding** — "Every single hedge fund has money in Japan. That trade is unwinding. If both collide, real fireworks."

> "If you're any other country, you sell US dollars and put money back into your own country. You can get 4% yield in Japan versus uncertainty in the US."

VIX already spiking. Market was due for a pullback regardless.

### Respect Stops — This Can Spiral

> "Respect stops. You don't know whether this resolves in ten days or fifteen. These macro events can quickly spiral out of control. You want to be solvent."

### What to Look For

- **DEP (delayed reaction) on the downside** — companies deeply affected by policy
- **9M EP on the short side** — breakdowns working
- **Government policy change catalysts** — credit card cap, tariffs
- **Earnings season** — will take a backseat to drama

### Don't Day Trade Unless You Have the Skill

> "If you don't have day trading skill, just sit out a period like this. This will resolve. On a day like this, why would I do SIPs unless I have a clear short with a catalyst? That's suicidal tendencies."

## PM Session (3 PM)

### Not "Get Me Out at Any Cost" Selling

> "When there is real panic selling, you see triple ETFs dominating EP 9M. Do you see triple ETFs dominating anywhere? No. The market is not taking this seriously."

Market sees the Greenland/drama as just that — drama. Most participants think nothing will come of it.

### Tesla: The One Clear Short

> "Tesla is the one which hit the trade idea screen first. It's a weak stock with a catalyst, and Elon Musk cannot control his testosterone."

### Credit Card Cap Starts Today

The 10% interest rate cap on credit cards takes effect. Most affected:
- **Synchrony Financial** — subprime lending, store cards
- **Capital One** — subprime lending
- **Delta Airlines** — makes $4B profit from credit cards; airline is loss-making

> "I don't see major sell-off in the banks though. They'll probably go to court immediately."

### CRDO: Late to the Party — Wrong DEP Candidate

Member asked about CRDO as a DEP. Pradeep rejected it:

> "What catalyst? The earnings reaction was negative. It's been inert. The idea of DEP is to look for something that had a positive reaction, then look at it. CRDO's catalyst was a year ago — it's late to the party. In this market condition, it won't work."

### Don't Pay Attention to Drama — Pay Attention to Setups

> "Don't pay attention to the drama. Pay attention to the setups. You have setups there. Setup, setup, setup."

Key setups spotted:
- **SNDK** — multiple immediate catalysts (but extended)
- **ULTA** — nice tooling setup
- **Tesla short** — clear catalyst, weak stock
- **Bath & Body Works** — retail, government policy catalyst
- **MGM** — discretionary spending

### Seasonality Warning for Retail

> "Seasonally weak period for retail stocks might be starting. Positive seasonality for them is over."

### Key Takeaways

1. **Respect stops.** Macro events can spiral. Be solvent first.
2. **Japan carry trade > Greenland drama.** Hedge fund unwinding is the bigger systemic risk.
3. **Market not panicking — no triple ETF dominance.** Participants see drama, not real risk.
4. **Credit card cap starts today.** Synchrony and Capital One most exposed; banks not selling off aggressively (will go to court).
5. **Don't day trade without skill.** Sit out volatile periods if you can't manage intraday.
6. **Pay attention to setups, not drama.** Tesla short, ULTA, government policy DEP shorts.
7. **Retail seasonality turning negative.** Be careful with retail longs.
8. **CRDO is a bad DEP candidate.** No immediate catalyst, late to the party, negative earnings reaction.
