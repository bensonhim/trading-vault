## AM Meeting (9:15 AM)

### Essay: Bearish — Expecting Counter-Trend Rally Before Further Downside

> "I remain bearish as uncertainty and volatility continues. Expecting a short-term counter-trend rally before potential follow-through to the downside."

Market monitor: 600+ selling day Friday. Both indicators red with deteriorating margin. 20% study slightly bearish (1:1 ratio). VIX surged. Indices broke range to downside. Crypto sold off with broader market.

**Key insight:** Market approaching extreme oversold levels (T2122), but we haven't seen real panic selling yet — no 800-1000+ day. Better conditions for a bounce would require **extreme selling** (readings below 200 on T2122). Current readings not there yet.

### No Panic = No Convincing Bottom

> "You just don't see panic selling. The kind of environment which was there for the April bounce. We haven't even seen real selling like an 800,000+ day."

Best turns happen after extreme oversold conditions. Current conditions: not extreme. Just choppy selling with buying dips. **More panic selling would create better setups.**

### Gap Up Faded — Again

> "Morning strength, everybody started buying. Then it faded."

### Are Breakouts Likely to Work?

The answer hasn't changed. Only breakouts with **clear catalyst** have any chance. Everything else fades.

### The EP 9M Signal — When Real Turns Happen

> "When there is a genuine turn in the market, what setup is going to show up? EP 9,000,000 will light up like crazy. You'll have 200 EP 9,000,000 viable stocks. Do you see any EP 9,000,000 viable here? No."

Reference: April 2025 bottom. On that day, every single stock had EP 9M or DEP 9M. That's what a real bottom looks like. Current: hardly any. **When it's real, you'll know.**

### Control Yourself — Don't Trade Because Food Is Available

> "You don't have to eat just because food is available. Control."

FOMO is the enemy. People want action. But situational awareness says: no setups, no catalysts, breakouts don't work. Wait for the market to show its hand.

> "If you have all this information, and then you can control yourself, how can it be that you lose money?"

### Stagflation Doesn't Matter

> "Even if there is stagflation, there will be setups. Stocks with news will go up 20-30-100%. Some guy is going to smuggle chips into China and his stock will go down 50-60%. How does it really bother me?"

Setups exist in every environment. Focus on what you control: setup quality and risk management.
