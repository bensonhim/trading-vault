# Narrative Power, AMD Catalyst, Sugar Babies, and Think Setups Not News

> "When the narrative is bullish, any shit flies. When the narrative turns bearish, nothing is good enough. Understand the power of narrative."

---

## AM Session (9:15 AM)

### SA: Cautious — 500+ Selling Day, Margin Deterioration

500+ selling day yesterday. Secondary indicators fully red. 20% study bullish (2:1 ratio). VIX pushed higher. Indices sharp pullback — tariff uncertainty. Small caps (TNA/IWM) underperformed, closing red. Bitcoin below $70K.

> "More selling at some stage this range will break and produce big moves. More likely to the downside. The tape is very heavy except for non-tech."

### Chinese Torture Selling Pattern

> "Large cap tech stocks are showing the same size seen before big corrections. Slow gradual Chinese torture kind of selling. Drip, drip, drip, drip, and then one day the scope and the real selling shows up."

### Narrative Has Changed

The bullish themes have evaporated:
- **AI stocks** → "AI is bad for many businesses"
- **Crypto stocks** → "kiss of death"
- **Gold/Silver** → crashed after parabolic
- **Quantum** → "nobody is even talking about quantum now"
- **Space/Cybersecurity/Drones** → all grounded

> "When the narrative is bullish, any shit flies. When the narrative turns bearish, nothing is good enough. That's how markets work. In the long run, based on earnings. In the short run, it's all about narratives."

### AMD: The Real Catalyst Day

Meta-AMD AI chip deal worth $100B+ — front page of every business outlet.

- First target: $250-260
- Optimistic: $300
- Most optimistic: Could become trillion-dollar stock by 2028-2030
- Or wait for delayed reaction setup
- $325B market cap — longer-term catalyst, not immediate revenue

> "This is the kind of stock where the catalyst is a longer-term catalyst. The real revenue from Meta is going to come in probably next year. But I will definitely take a position in AMD, or build a position over next couple of days if it sets up right."

### Volume Confusion Explained

9M pre-market shows LRMR with 27M shares. AMD 10M+ pre-market. If genuine news, stocks trade heavy volume in pre-market.

> "If you want real volume, you need consolidated tape service — $15/month. Without it, you get relay level volume."

### Think Setups, Not News

> "Think setups. Think setups. Not in news."

## PM Session (3 PM)

### Sugar Babies Everywhere

> "The sugar babies are all over the place trying to make a move."

Sugar baby list method: Top 25 by each of 5 different criteria, filter for institutional quality.

### Best Long Setups

**INTC** — institutional quality 9M breakout, sugar baby. Looks better than AMD.
**LRC** — nice breakout, looks very nice.
**FTEI** — decent earnings, looks good.
**AMD** — range-bound, will remain so for a while. Catalyst day but volume didn't come.
**FRME** — delayed reaction EP, 9M setup.
**LACK** — sugar baby biotech. "When the sugar baby calls, you always answer."
**IROD** — biotech, can move from any level.
**SNDK** — momentum burst EP short.

### SNDK Short: Why?

> "It's a momentum burst to the downside. Stocks move in the direction of momentum burst, and they've done that for hundreds of years. Great earnings by itself is not a reason not to short a stock."

### Intel vs AMD

> "Intel looks better than AMD right now."

Intel: institutional quality, 9M breakout, sugar baby. AMD: range-bound, needs higher volume.

### UNH: Continuing Short

Keeping UNH short. Willing to give $2 more for a possible 3rd-4th day move. The delayed reaction is playing out.

### ARM: Fade Pattern

> "Something which comes out of the gate and looks very good — you can short it on an intraday basis and it will probably fade half its gain or more. This is a setup idea you can almost take to the bank nowadays."

### Reversal Bearish Picks

AMT, ZBH, VTRS, CAG (Conagra — at best).

### Breakouts Not Working

> "Even good earnings don't move the market. The narrative has become negative for the entire sector. AMD news didn't move the stock at all. This is why you wait for delayed reaction — the volume just didn't come."

> "There are just no buyers in this market. In a different environment, same AMD news would send the stock flying 10%+. The tape is very heavy. Looks similar to before corrections."

### Don't Buy on Day 2 or 3

> "People cannot stop buying on a second or third day. No matter what nationality, people cannot zip up their zipper and get into trouble."

### Key Takeaways

1. **Narrative power rules markets.** Bullish narrative = anything flies. Bearish narrative = nothing works.
2. **Tape is very heavy.** Like before corrections. Slow drip selling then real selling shows up.
3. **AMD $100B Meta deal** — real catalyst but longer-term. Wait for delayed reaction. Volume didn't come.
4. **Intel > AMD** right now. Institutional quality 9M breakout, sugar baby.
5. **SNDK short** — momentum burst to downside. Great earnings ≠ reason not to short.
6. **Sugar babies everywhere.** LACK, IROD, INTC — answer the call.
7. **ARM fade pattern** — stocks that gap up big, short intraday, they fade.
8. **Breakouts unlikely to work.** Selectively only. Even good earnings get ignored.
9. **Think setups, not news.** If SNDK breaks down, it comes to you via setup.
10. **UNH short continuing.** Willing to give $2 more for 3rd-4th day move.
