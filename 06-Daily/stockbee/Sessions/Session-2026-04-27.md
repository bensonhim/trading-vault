# NVIDIA Free Trade Financed by NVDX — SOX Pullback Expected, SNAP Only EP 9M, Extended Stocks Still Working, Nothing Actionable

April 27 was a transition day. After Friday's stops, Pradeep was cautious — expecting consolidation/pullback in semiconductors after the parabolic move. The keyword: "consolidation and pullback." Extended stocks still worked, but that teaches the wrong lessons. The big play: NVIDIA. Pradeep bought 5,000 shares and used 25,000 shares of NVDX (2x NVIDIA ETF) to finance the trade, selling NVDX in premarket for ~$9K profit, making the NVIDIA core position essentially free. ### NVIDIA — The Free Trade
