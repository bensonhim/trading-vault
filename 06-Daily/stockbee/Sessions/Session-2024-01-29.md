# The Eureka Moment and Why Hard Work Alone Won't Make You a Trader

Monday reinforced the choppy, selective market. Breakouts fade, shorts don't work, only one-day moves and SIPs are paying. But the real teaching was philosophical: **the eureka moment — the flash of insight — is worth more than 10,000 hours of hard work.** You can do 10 deep dives and still not make money. But one insight, one "aha" moment, and your trading changes forever. This is what separates traders who make it from those who don't. Pradeep announced a special 6PM session on the Eureka Moment after a 3-hour conversation with a psychologist.

### Affirm: The Main Position

### Algorithmic Trading: Why Moves Compress Into One Day (Continued)

- Quant models project target (e.g., "this catalyst = 33% move")
- They buy continuously from open until target reached, then sell everything
- **This compresses what used to be a 2-week move into 1 day**
- EP used to gap up 4-5%, then grind up 25-30% over 2 weeks
- Now EPs gap up 15-30% in one day because algos front-run the entire move
- After algos finish buying, the stock does nothing → **delayed reaction becomes the better setup**
- Also: **fading extraordinary moves** (IBM +12% in one day) is a new edge
- **As strategies become popular, the edge fades** — everyone knows EP now, so the traditional edge is gone
- Need to create NEW edges: delayed reaction, fading moves, institutional quality SIPs

### LTV Returns

- Long-term value setup being reintroduced
- Had disappeared during COVID
- "LTV is back, and it's back with a bang"
