### Credit Card Industry Under Threat

Pradeep's morning watchlist: **USR, Tempus, JPMorgan Chase, Capital One Financial, Synchrony Financial** — just five stocks.

> "Synchrony Financial is the largest issuer of store cards. If this 10% interest rate cap becomes law, what happens? Business is over. How are they going to survive? I have no idea."

> "The person at the counter says 'do you want to put this on a store card today?' — they get $25 for every card signed up. That's their model. At 10% cap, this industry is dead."
