## AM Meeting (9 AM)

### Situational Awareness: Bearish, Maximum Volatility

- Essay: Bearish. Uncertainty and volatility at all-time highs
- 1900+ selling day yesterday. Both 5 and 10-day ratios turned red = aggressive selling
- T2108 cracked below 20 (from 30.89 to 18.03)
- 20% study: bullish approaching 20, bearish approaching 100
- VIX surged to 30 initially, then 45 by open
- Dow -1000, Nasdaq -4.5%, S&P -5.3% yesterday. All at lowest levels of the year
- TNA/IWM waterfall decline. Small caps hardest hit by inflation
- Crypto sold off. Bitcoin just above $80K
- **China retaliated with 34% tariff on US imports**

### This Is NOT COVID — No QE Fix

> "This bear market is not caused by recession. We have a roaring economy with full employment. If Fed does QE here, you'll have double-digit inflation."

- COVID: Fed pumped $30T, stimulated demand, averted recession
- This time: supply shock, not demand shock. QE makes inflation worse
- Trump tariffs are illogical (34% on Solomon Islands which exports $200K?)
- No resolution unless policy reverses or Trump is forced to back down

### Index Components = Worst Affected

> "Where do institutions normally hide during stress? Index component stocks. These are the MOST affected by tariffs."

- Apple, Nike, Meta — their business models depend on global supply chains
- Apple phone would cost $3,500 if manufactured in US
- Meta's business model = ads from China resellers on Amazon/Shopify
- FedEx = international shipments affected
- Starbucks = coffee imports affected
- These stocks will set up as delayed reaction shorts

### What Setup Will Form First?

> "Reversal bullish is more likely than bottom bounce. For a bottom bounce, you'd need a 6% up day on SPY. Probability is very low."

- First setup likely: reversal bullish
- Bottom bounce needs massive gap up — unlikely given tariff news
- Delayed reaction shorts = laser focus priority
- Bottom bounces on index components are also questionable (these stocks' business models are broken)

### Wait for Setups

> "Don't react emotionally. Wait for reversal bullish setups. Wait for bottom bounce setups. Wait for start-of-swing setups."

- Reversal bullish → 3-5 days, 15-20% → transitions to anticipation → continuation
- That's the chain. But nothing forming yet
- The signal will come: "When Bill Ackman comes crying on CNBC, that's the signal"
- NMAX and IPOs for day trading only. Not for swing trades

### SIPs for the Day

- **Nothing new from Pradeep** — sit in cash, wait
- **China stocks** (BABA, TAL) = potential longs if market bounces
- **Triple ETFs** = day trade opportunities only (TNA, SQQQ, etc.)
- **CXAI**: earnings, some premarket action
- **IPOs**: ICCT, BDRX (low float, no plausible story)
- **Minerals**: USAR, MP (tariff-related beneficiary)
- **ABBB**: earnings downside guidance

### Key Quote

> "Sitting in cash is a superpower at this stage. Don't pick up pennies in front of a bulldozer. Don't take weekend risk on Friday."
