# Nothing to Buy — Extended Markets, Exhaustion Everywhere, Calendar Short Explodes, Tight Pants Still Required

April 10 was a "nothing to buy" session. Everything is extended after the 6-day bounce. The market is in a secular bull that started in 2009 — the Iran skirmish was just an 8% cyclical dip, already recovered. But now setups are scarce: no tight pants, no clean EP 9M, no actionable breakouts. Calendar short continued to explode (moved stop, locked 80%+ profits). Palantir short got stopped out after trimming. UnitedHealth delayed reaction entry failed — stopped out, then recovered. The PM session was 6 minutes long: three names (Cisco short, Zoom short, Costco short) and one long (XNDU). Pradeep signed off early for a Broadway play.
