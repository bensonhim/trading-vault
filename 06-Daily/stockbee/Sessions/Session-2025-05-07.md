### SIPs Prioritization — SMB Capital Video Teaching

- Recommended SMB Capital video on SIPs prioritization
- Key metrics for prioritizing SIPs:
  1. **Relative volume**: 20%+ of 21-day average volume in pre-market = higher probability
  2. **Short interest**: 5+ days, especially 7+ days = higher probability of squeeze
  3. **Capitalization**: below 100M = where the biggest one-day moves happen
  4. **Float as % of short interest**: 20-25%+ = squeeze potential
  5. **4M+ average volume** for serious liquidity
- SIPs are primarily for day trading — "most of the time, the catalyst is not necessarily sustainable"
- "Can you do 10 SIPs equal to one EP? Depends on your style"
- Low price stocks attract most retail traders
- "These are one day moves — very profitable if you have a small account"

### 4% Scan Modification (Permanent)
