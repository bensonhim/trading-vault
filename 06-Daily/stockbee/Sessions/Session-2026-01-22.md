# Sloppy Action, Hit Singles, and Setup Clarity: No Setup, No Play

> "The pullback destroyed many setups. Everything in the top 200 looks sloppy — wide and loose. Hit singles. If there's a setup, we play. If there's no setup, we don't play."

---

## AM Session (9:15 AM)

### SA: Cautious — Choppy, Select Breakouts/Breakdowns Working

500+ green day yesterday. VIX retreated. Small caps ([[TNA]], [[IWM]]) outperformed. But the pullback destroyed many setups — everything looks sloppy.

### Volatility Will Continue

> "This is never over. Some new country we might decide to do this with. Volatility will continue. The tackle/tariff thing worked, but many pullbacks and consolidations are too choppy and sloppy."

### Sloppy Action: Wide and Loose Top 200

> "Anything you look at in the top 100 or 200 stocks is looking very sloppy currently. You need tight price action. Wide and loose action means you need wider stops or you get stopped out."

[[APLD]] — best earnings so far this season — even it has wide, choppy action with shakeouts.

### Best Strategy: Hit Singles

> "Best option is to hit singles — intraday action kind of thing. Otherwise you'll get shaken out multiple times. By the time you realize, you're in a drawdown."

> "My strategy is clearly to hit a lot of singles. And then occasionally, when you get a home run, go for it."

### Only IWM Standing Tall

> "The only thing which is standing tall is IWM. Technology has been down one, two, three, four, five days."

### SIPs or Day Trade Tesla/Nvidia

> "I would take something which is SIPs, or go into Tesla or Nvidia — good candidates for day trading because they tend to have clean moves intraday."

### Shorting Intel: Need a Signal

On shorting Intel:
> "Is there a signal? If I get a 9M EP kind of signal, yes. But there's no signal here. As long as you have a setup, we play. If there is no setup, we don't play."

### Don't Buy Sixth-Day Swings

> "If your idea is to buy something which is up six days in a row, give me your contact number — I'll send it to the next Market Wizards book."
