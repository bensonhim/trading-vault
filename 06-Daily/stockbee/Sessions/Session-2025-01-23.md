### Process Over Picks — THE Key Teaching

- **"Have a clear setup definition. Go by setup."**
- **"Money is an outcome of following a process"**
- **"Process, process, process"** — not picks
- "Refine and refine and refine your setup"
- People ask for tickers like asking about their kid — "want me to say good things about their ticker"

- **If you know the process, you don't need the 2 PM meeting** — you'd already be in the setups before the meeting starts
