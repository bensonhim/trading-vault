# Creeping Moves, Fade Dominated, Process Over Picks

> "Process, process, process. If you don't have a setup and don't have a process, you're just going from place to place. Money is an outcome of following a process. If breakouts aren't working for you, fine — find another process. But anything you want to make work, you have to have a process."

## 2 PM Meeting — Fade Problem, Process Over Picks, Day Trading Only

### The Fade Problem: Death by a Thousand Cuts

- **"So many fails"** — breakouts look good, you get in, they reverse
- eBay: perfect setup with catalyst → bought small → it failed
- Google: looked like breakout intraday → got hammered back
- Meta: same pattern again — looks like breakout → fades
- "Things look good, then they just fade. Same problem everywhere."
- Even high-priced stocks (EPM) doing pins — would stop you out then take off
- **"Closed stops are not working"** — need extra-wide stops + small size
- This is not a FEE market — can't put on full positions

### Process Over Picks — The Core Message

> "If there is one secret to being successful in this business, that is having a setup. And once you have a setup, having a process. Fine tune the process. Money is an outcome of following a process."

- **No setup, no process** = the #1 problem in Zoom meetings
- People asking about tickers instead of following setups
- "They have a setup, they don't have a process. If they have a process, they don't follow their own process."
- "Oh, I didn't put a stop. So it went down 20%. Then I said I don't want to sell. Now it's down 60%."
- **Three things**: Process, risk management, setup. Everything else is secondary.
- Like building six-pack abs: "You can look at all the Instagram videos, but unless you actually go to the gym and manage your diet, nothing happens."
