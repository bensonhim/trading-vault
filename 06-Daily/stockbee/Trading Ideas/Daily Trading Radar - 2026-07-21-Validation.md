## Key Improvements Needed

1. **Suppress DEP/ANTS generation in DISTRIBUTING regime** — When regime = DISTRIBUTING, DEP and ANTS candidates should be flagged as "high risk in current environment" or suppressed entirely. Pradeep found zero actionable DEPs and zero ANTS.

2. **Flag consolidation/cup-with-handle pattern** — CRCL was flagged as #2 SOS but was specifically called out by Pradeep as a failed consolidation breakout. The Radar should detect cup-with-handle / consolidation patterns and flag them as higher risk.

3. **Classify SIPs separately from SOS swing** — ACHR was a SIPs (day trade), not a swing SOS. The Radar should separate "stocks in play for day trading" from "SOS swing candidates."

4. **Add TSLA detection** — TSLA had robotaxi news and was Pradeep's best SIPs day trade. The Radar missed it because it didn't meet 4%+ SOS criteria. Consider a separate "SIPs news scan" that flags stocks with morning catalysts regardless of % move.

5. **20% study integration** — The Radar has breadth data but doesn't use it to suppress setups. When 20% study < 20 and falling, DEP/ANTS generation should be automatically suppressed.

6. **ANTS 15 count as regime filter** — Pradeep uses ANTS 15 scan count (normally 400-500, currently 50) as a breadth indicator. Consider adding this to the Radar's regime detection.
