## Key Findings

### Strengths
- **Distribution detection** (scores 8–9): Radar correctly flags DISTRIBUTING/NO_NEW_LONGS on days Pradeep identifies as dangerous (May 15, May 22, June 23)
- **Cautious bull alignment** (scores 7–10): When both see selective opportunity, radar matches well
- **Range-bound calls** (scores 7–9): Good agreement on "no clean setups" days

### Weaknesses
1. **Inflection point misses** (scores 2–4): Radar lags at market turns
   - May 19: FULL_BULL vs Moody's downgrade → Pradeep bearish
   - May 21: FULL_BULL vs rug pull → Pradeep went all cash
   - June 25: RANGE_BOUND vs breakout to all-time highs
2. **Systematic bearish bias at turns**: Radar downgrades to RANGE_BOUND/SIPs_ONLY too early on pullback days where Pradeep still finds selective plays
3. **Systematic bullish bias at extremes**: Radar upgrades to FULL_BULL when 20% study surges > 80, but Pradeep reads this as exhaustion

### Recommended Fixes
1. **Add EXCESS_BULL regime** for 20% bullish > 80 (contrarian pullback signal, not bullish confirmation) — catches May 13, May 19, June 9
2. **Relax CAUTIOUS_BULL action** from REDUCE_SIZE to SELECTIVE_PLAYS when 20% study > 40 and ratio_5d > 1.2 — catches May 12, June 24, June 25
3. **Add news/fundamental gate**: May 19 (Moody's downgrade), June 16 (500+ red day) were event-driven — pure quantitative regime can't catch these
