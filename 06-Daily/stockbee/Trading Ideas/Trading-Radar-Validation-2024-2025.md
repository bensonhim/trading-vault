## Key Findings

### Strengths

1. **Regime alignment strong in trending markets** (2024-07-10, 2025-04-15): When market has a clear direction (selective bull, cautiously bullish turn), Radar's LLM Judge aligns well with Pradeep's SA.

2. **SIPs detection reliable**: 30 SIPs consistently found with fresh catalyst counts that correlate with Pradeep's level of SIPs discussion activity.

3. **Suppression logic correct in low-20%-study environments**: When 20% study < 30, suppressing SOS/DEP/ANTS aligns with Pradeep's "funds not buying" / "breakouts not following through" assessment.

4. **T2108 values match**: Radar's T2108 readings align closely with Pradeep's session T2108 values (e.g., 2025-04-15: 16.38 vs 16.57; 2025-06-20: 50.64 vs 50.79).

### Gaps

1. **Regime mismatch in sideways/consolidation markets** (2025-06-20): Radar classified as DISTRIBUTING when Pradeep saw sideways-with-upside-bias. The 20% study=0 anomaly (possibly a Friday-before-long-weekend data gap) may trigger false DISTRIBUTING. **Recommend**: Add a "sideways/consolidation" regime between CAUTIOUS_BULL and DISTRIBUTING.

2. **Suppression too lenient in rotational corrections** (2025-05-15): Pradeep reported three consecutive breakout failures ("something in the water"), but Radar kept SOS/DEP/ANTS active because 20% study was 137 (elevated but not triggering suppression). **Recommend**: Add a "consecutive breakout failure" suppression trigger independent of 20% study level.

3. **20% study data discrepancy** (2025-01-15): Radar showed 20% study=1 while Pradeep described "approaching 20." This may be a lookback window difference (Radar uses 5d, Pradeep may use a different window) or a data freshness issue. **Recommend**: Investigate 20% study calculation window alignment with Pradeep's methodology.

4. **Missing frameworks**: Radar doesn't capture several Pradeep frameworks:
   - **Trade Factory** (home runs / mini home runs / singles)
   - **9M EP / delayed reaction** as distinct from SIPs
   - **First legs forming** (2025-04-15) — early trend detection
   - **Sector regime change** (tech → metals/mining, 2025-04-15)
   - **Extended stocks danger** (2025-05-15) — stocks with 2-3+ legs = avoid

5. **Source bug**: Sector RS computation fails with NoneType error on 2025-02-20 and 2025-03-20, causing `sorted_rs` UnboundLocalError. This blocks report generation entirely. **Recommend**: Fix RS computation to handle None values gracefully.

### Recommendations

1. **Add sideways/consolidation regime** — between CAUTIOUS_BULL and DISTRIBUTING, for low-20%-study + flat-T2108 + no-clear-direction environments.
2. **Add consecutive-breakout-failure suppression trigger** — independent of 20% study level, when 3+ consecutive days of breakout failures.
3. **Investigate 20% study lookback window** — verify alignment with Pradeep's oscillator methodology.
4. **Fix Sector RS NoneType bug** — blocks 20% of report generation attempts.
5. **Consider adding "first legs forming" detection** — TI65 young momentum (C65 ≤ 10) is a proxy but Pradeep's framework is broader (persistent continuous buying across 100+ stocks).
