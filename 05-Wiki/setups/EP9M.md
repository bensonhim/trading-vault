# EP9M

> *"I have reached my retirement age. I am only trading EP 9M. I am no more interested in anything else. EP 9M is the thing."*

EP 9M is a **volume-based variant of Episodic Pivot** — the stock trades **9 million or more shares** in a single day, and that volume itself is the primary signal. It turbocharges EP frequency by roughly **10x** compared to standard EP, making it Pradeep's single most important setup and the foundation upon which every other skill is built.

---

## Definition

A stock qualifies as an **EP 9M** when it trades **9M+ shares in a day** with a genuine catalyst (earnings surprise, guidance upgrade, sector shift, or news event). The 9M volume threshold is not arbitrary — it represents **institutional participation** so overwhelming that the move has structural backing, not just retail momentum.

Key attributes:
- **Volume is the signal** — the 9M scan filters for extreme volume days that confirm institutional buying
- **Catalyst is mandatory** — no catalyst = no EP 9M; it becomes "liquid lava" (popular but catalyst-free)
- **Bucket 0/1 required** — the stock must have earnings or sales catalyst (Bucket 0 or 1 in the Bucket System)
- **Stop range: 8–20%** (up to 40% for nano-caps)
- **Conviction: extreme** — this is not a "nice to have" setup; it is the primary setup

## Key Principles

### 1. EP 9M Is the Primary Setup — Everything Else Is Secondary

> *"Primary setup: EP 9M + momentum burst bullish. Everything else is secondary. Reversal bullish? Nice to have. Anticipation? Nice to have. Sugar Babies? Nice to have. Can I make a living just doing anticipation? No. Can I make a living doing reversal bullish? No. They are supporting actors."*

Pradeep explicitly ranks setups. EP 9M is the **lead actor** — the setup that can sustain a trading career on its own. Reversal bullish, anticipation, and Sugar Babies are supporting characters that supplement but cannot replace EP 9M.

### 2. Volume Confirms Institutional Participation

> *"If anything is in bucket zero and bucket one, it has a catalyst."*

The 9M volume threshold means institutions are participating. Without volume confirmation, a breakout is suspect — it may be retail-driven and unsustainable. The scan answers: *Is there real money behind this move?*

### 3. Catalyst + Neglect = Explosiveness

Stocks that have been **sideways or neglected for months** and then trigger an EP 9M on earnings or a genuine catalyst produce the most explosive moves. The formula: **neglect = pent-up energy → catalyst = release valve → 9M volume = institutional confirmation**.

### 4. Process Timing Is Non-Negotiable

> *"Running EP9M at 9:30 AM, not pre-market."*

Process edge comes from timing. Running the EP9M scan at the right time (opening 15–20 minutes) and executing within that window is where the edge lives. Running scans at 6 AM or after lunch destroys the signal.

### 5. EP 9M Proliferating to the Downside Is a Critical Breadth Signal

> *"You will see that EP 9M to the downsides are proliferating, and then they will, one day, just start going down in a smooth downturn."*

When EP 9M setups appear on the **short side** — not just the long side — it signals that institutional selling is accelerating. This is one of the most powerful breadth warnings Pradeep uses.

### 6. Lottery Tickets Flying = Immediate Caution

> *"When shitty stocks make 1000%+ moves, quality breakouts are about to fail."*

When the EP 9M scan is full of **broken structures** and low-quality names making parabolic moves, it is time to reduce risk. These "lottery tickets" signal an overheated market where the next move is likely down.

### 7. 48-Hour Study Plan for Mastery

> *"Pick ONE setup to master. Lock yourself away for 24–48 hours. Study 5,000+ examples of that setup."*

Pradeep's prescription for learning EP 9M: study **1,800+ examples** (every EP 9M in the last 1,200+ days), go through each stock one by one, hide all indicators, and mark the day the move started. Then ask: *Was it a 4% breakout? A 9M EP? A dollar breakout?*

### 8. EP 9M Is Agnostic to Market Direction

> *"EP9M is agnostic. Let's say the VIX is spiking because Russia attacked the US. Everything is going crazy. But in your EP9M scan, you see five stocks which are up — Donald Trump Media, RTX, Lockheed."*

Even in a crashing market, EP 9M can find long-side setups in defense stocks, safe havens, or counter-trend sectors. The scan filters for volume and catalyst, not market direction.

## Contradictions & Gaps

1. **"Primary setup" vs. daily flexibility**: Pradeep calls EP 9M his primary setup, yet frequently trades DEP, Sugar Babies, and FHP on days with no EP 9M candidates. The resolution: EP 9M is primary by *conviction and frequency*, but the market determines what's available each day.
2. **9M threshold rigidity**: The 9M threshold is stated as hard, yet Pradeep has noted that "crazy volume (100M+ shares) and no obvious earnings catalyst" also qualifies. The spirit is **extreme institutional volume**, not a rigid number.
3. **"EP 9M only" vs. complementary setups**: He states "I am only trading EP 9M" in retirement mode, but in practice uses DEP, reversal bullish, and anticipation as complements. The "only EP 9M" stance is about prioritization, not exclusion.
4. **Stop range variance**: The stated 8–20% stop range is wide; for working people, stops may be tighter (2–3% on FHP variant). Context matters — nano-caps allow up to 40% stops.
