# Lesson 6: Catalysts — What Makes a Move Real

> **Core Question:** Why is this stock moving, and is the move real?
> **Answer:** A catalyst is the structural reason for a move. If you can't identify the catalyst in 10 minutes, skip the trade.

---

## The Core Truth

> *"Stocks move based on catalyst. Most of the major moves in any stocks are started by a catalyst."*

A 4% breakout without a catalyst is **noise**. The market does random things on any given day. The 4%+ 9M+ scan gives you the *pattern* — the catalyst tells you whether the pattern is *real*.

**Volume validates the catalyst:**

> *"Volume tells you how good the catalyst is. Volume tells you whether this is a significant catalyst or not."*

9M+ volume = significant catalyst. Below that, the market doesn't care enough.

---

## Catalyst Types and Lifespan

| Type | Lifespan | Examples | Size When | Skip When |
|------|----------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Secular Theme** | 10–30 years | AI (NVDA), EV, digital payments, drones, clean energy | Early in trend; multi-quarter hold | Late in trend; already up 500%+ |
| **Cyclical / Turnaround** | 1–4 quarters | ANF, SNDK, bucket zero stocks | First 1–2 quarters of acceleration | After 4+ quarters; growth decelerating |
| **Earnings Surprise** | 1–10 days | SMCI, SNDK, Intel | Big beat with guidance raise | Miss, or already priced in |
| **Government Policy** | Months to years | Solar stocks, HSA providers, Obamacare insurers | When policy first announced | When policy is old news |
| **Product Launch** | Days to weeks | Tesla, Ozempic (LLY, NVO) | Revolutionary product | Incremental update |
| **Story / Fiction** | 1 day to 4 weeks | Oklo, QuantumScape, RGC, SMX | On 9M breakout with volume, plausible theme | After 2–3 legs, or 9M breakdown |
| **Biotech** | 1 day to 1 week | ABVX, clinical trials | Only if you understand the drug | If you don't understand biotech |
| **Analyst Action** | 1–2 days | Bank upgrades, Celsius | Influential analyst with reasoning | No-name analyst, already priced in |
| **Liquid Lava** | 3–10 days | NVDA, large-cap tech | For size/liquidity only | Expecting 40%+ moves |

## How to Validate a Catalyst

| Method | What It Tells You | When to Use |
|--------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Volume on trigger day** | Market cares; 9M+ = significant | Every trade — first filter |
| **Briefing.com** | Institutional-quality catalyst summary | Morning research |
| **MarketSmith X-ray** | Growth/turnaround validation | Weekend deep dive |
| **Earnings surprises** | Actual vs estimated EPS | Catalyst day or pre-market |
| **AI tools (Gemini/ChatGPT)** | Quick catalyst identification | When you need a fast answer |
| **Bucket system** | Bucket 0/1 = has catalyst | Stock selection filter |
| **Delayed reaction** | Stock holds gains = catalyst is real | Day 2 after breakout |

## The Bucket System (Quick Quality Check)

| Bucket | Criteria | Catalyst Quality | Typical Move |
|--------|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| **0** | IPO'd, 39%+ sales growth, <$10B cap | **Best** — pure growth, no coverage | 300–800%+ |
| **1** | 39%+ sales growth, any market cap | Good — established but accelerating | 50–200% |
| **2** | 39%+ EPS growth + 20%+ sales growth | Good — profitability + growth | 30–100% |
| **IBD 250** | MarketSmith curated list | Mixed — check individually | Varies |

> *"If anything is in bucket zero and bucket one, it has a catalyst."*

**The 39% sales rule is the strongest single predictor:**

> *"If a company has sales growth of 39%+ back-to-back for two quarters, that stock can double, triple, go up 500%, 600%, 800%."*

## Real Catalysts vs Story Stocks

| Aspect | Real Catalyst | Story Stock |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Fundamentals** | Earnings, sales growth, turnaround | No revenue, no earnings |
| **Move magnitude** | 20–50% typical | 100–1000%+ possible |
| **Duration** | 1–10 days | 1 day to 4 weeks |
| **Conviction** | High — can size big (25–50%) | Lower — size 5–10% |
| **Research** | X-ray, earnings, Briefing.com | Plausibility, theme, influencers, low float |
| **Stop** | Tight (0.5–2.5% for DEP) | Wider (8–20%) |
| **Examples** | SMCI, ANF, SNDK | Oklo, QuantumScape, RGC, SMX |

> *"Since COVID-19, most of the biggest moves in the market are by stocks which are basically stories. They have nothing — no earnings, no sales — and they will be the ones making the biggest move."*

Story stocks are **tradable** but require:
- Smaller position size (5–10%)
- Tighter stops
- Quick exits (1–3 days)
- Understanding that the move is based on narrative, not fundamentals

## The Broken Business Concept (Short Side)

When shorting, the catalyst must be **negative and structural** — a company where something has fundamentally gone wrong.

**How to identify a broken business:**
1. X-Ray shows **negative growth rate**
2. **Missed earnings + guides down**
3. Clear, identifiable problem (not "it went up too much")
4. Broken for **multiple quarters**

| Example | Problem | Source |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Dow | Continuously making losses, declining business | [[16. DEP Guide]] |
| CarMax | Missed earnings, negative growth trajectory | [[16. DEP Guide]] |
| Target | Loss of brand identity, missing earnings | [[16. DEP Guide]] |
| PayPal | Missed earnings | [[16. DEP Guide]] |
| Humana | Medicare policy change terrible for business | [[20. Stock Selection Guide]] |

**What is NOT a broken business:**
- Carvana ("not a broken business; can go up")
- NVIDIA ("those are not broken businesses")
- "It went up too much" — that's hope, not analysis

## Catalyst + Setup = Confluence

The **best trades** happen when catalyst and setup align:

| Confluence | Example | Size |
|-----------|---------|------|
| EP + Bucket 0 + Sugar Baby | NVDA on earnings + 39% sales growth + on SB list | Maximum (50%+) |
| DEP + Institutional + Catalyst | SMCI pullback after earnings + 1000 funds + identifiable catalyst | Large (25–50%) |
| SOS + Market Monitor Green | Standard breakout in bull market | Standard |
| No catalyst + SOS | Random 4% move without identifiable catalyst | Skip or very small |
| Story stock + 9M breakout | Oklo on nuclear theme + volume | Small (5–10%) |
