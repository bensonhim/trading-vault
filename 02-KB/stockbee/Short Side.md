## Core Principle

> *"Short side is not a mirror image of the long side. Intuitively, it might look like that, but it doesn't work like that."* — [[23. Bearish Trading Guide|Bearish Moves]]

> *"The market is structurally long side... but short side is very rapid. It might take a stock six months to make a 100% move, and it might take three days for that 100% move to be over."* — [[23. Bearish Trading Guide|Bearish Moves]]

## DEP on Short Side

| Aspect | Long Side DEP | Short Side DEP | Source |
|--------|--------------|----------------|--------|
| **Setup time** | 3–10 days to break out | 8–15+ days, sometimes up to a month | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 7]] |
| **Urgency** | "If it takes more than 10 days, no urgency to buy" | "There has to be urgency to sell" | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 7]] |
| **Catalyst** | Earnings/sales acceleration, new product | **Broken business**, missed earnings, guidance down | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 2]] |
| **Entry** | First valid pullback / SOS after catalyst | First valid negative bar / SOS after bounce | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 10]] |
| **Stop** | 0.5–2.5% | Even tighter — valid shorts work immediately | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 5]] |

## Broken Business Concept

**Definition:** Company where something has structurally gone wrong — declining/negative earnings, negative sales growth, deteriorating margins, or a failed business model.

**How to identify:**
1. X-Ray shows **negative growth rate**
2. **Missed earnings + guides down**
3. Clear, identifiable problem (not "it went up too much")
4. Broken for **multiple quarters**

**Examples:**
| Stock | Problem | Source |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Dow** | Continuously making losses, declining business | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 2]] |
| **CarMax** | Missed earnings, negative growth trajectory | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 2]] |
| **Target** | Loss of brand identity, missing earnings | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 2]] |
| **PayPal** | Missed earnings | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 5]] |
| **Humana** | Medicare policy change terrible for business | [[20. Stock Selection Guide|Stock Selection — Fund Ownership]] |

**What is NOT a broken business:**
- Carvana ("not a broken business; can go up")
- NVIDIA ("those are not broken businesses")
- "It went up too much" — "That is hope and pray, not a setup" — [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 2]]

## Weak Structure Short (WSS)

| Component       | Requirement                                                        | Source                                         |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Niagara (N)** | 3+ consecutive DOWN days with NO positive day                      | [[10. Weak Structure Short Guide|WSS Part 1]] |
| **3/10**        | Decline at least 10% or $10 during waterfall                       | [[10. Weak Structure Short Guide|WSS Part 1]] |
| **Weak (W)**    | 1–3 days of bounce, each day up < 2.5%                             | [[10. Weak Structure Short Guide|WSS Part 3]] |
| **Entry**       | Short on next negative day after weak bounce                       | [[10. Weak Structure Short Guide|WSS Part 3]] |
| **Timing**      | Near end of day — "if you short early, many times it will reverse" | [[10. Weak Structure Short Guide|WSS Part 3]] |

**Stop:** Half of entry day's range above entry. Move daily to protect profit.
