## The 80-20 Rule (Default Exit)

> *"The exit is selling to strength, eighty twenty per trail. Basically, 99% of the time, it is eighty twenty, and then on the 20% trail."* — [[21. Process Guide|Process — Develop Process Edge]]

| Stage | Action | Trigger | Source |
|-------|--------|---------|--------|
| **20% gap up** | Sell 80%, trail 20% | Morning gap up 20% | [[21. Process Guide|Process — Develop Process Edge]] |
| **100% move** | Sell 80%, keep 20% with trailing stop | Stock doubles | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 5]] |
| **40–50% move** | Tighten stop aggressively (don't peel yet) | Stock up 40–50% | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 5]] |
| **10% in one day** | Take 80-20 | Reversal setup, 1-day 10% | [[22. Reversals Guide|Reversals]] |

## Trailing Stop Progression

| Day | Action | Setup | Source |
|-----|--------|-------|--------|
| **Day 1** | Low of day or half of day stop | All breakouts | [[15. Start of a Swing Guide|SOS Part 4]] |
| **Day 2** | Move to breakeven if open positive | All setups | [[22. Reversals Guide|Reversals 3]] |
| **Day 3** | Tighten to just below close (1–2%); lock 80–90% of profit | Momentum Burst | [[Bullish Momentum Burst Guide-Section 1|Bullish MB Section 4]] |

| **Day 5+** | Trailing stop or exit | Momentum Burst | [[Bullish Momentum Burst Guide-Section 1|Bullish MB Section 4]] |

## Peeling (Advanced Technique)

> *"Peeling a position, doing eighty twenty is a very advanced trading technique... I'm putting in 60–80% of my account on a swing trade. And then I'm taking 80% off on a second day or a third day."* — [[Bullish Momentum Burst Guide-Section 1|Bullish MB Section 4]]

| Scenario | Action | Source |
|----------|--------|--------|
| **Large position (50%+ account)** | Peel 80% on day 2–3, trail 20% | [[Bullish Momentum Burst Guide-Section 1|Bullish MB Section 4]] |
| **Small position (10% account)** | Peeling doesn't make sense — just hold or exit | [[Bullish Momentum Burst Guide-Section 1|Bullish MB Section 4]] |
| **Home run hunting** | Don't peel — "you'll never hit a home run if you're peeling it" | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 2]] |
| **Liquid lava (large cap)** | Slow to peel — day 3–4 | [[21. Process Guide|Process EP9M]] |
| **NVIDIA 5% gap** | Peel immediately | [[21. Process Guide|Process EP9M]] |

## What NOT to Do

| Mistake | Why Wrong | Correct Way | Source |
|---------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Let winner turn into loser | Gives back all gains | Move to breakeven quickly; tighten at 40–50% | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 5]] |
| Hold momentum burst past day 5 | Probability of reversal increases | Time stop: exit day 3–5 | [[Bullish Momentum Burst Guide-Section 1|Bullish MB Section 4]] |
| Not peeling when at 100% | Gives back massive gains | Sell 80%, trail 20% | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 5]] |
| Peeling too early (home runs) | Never catch the big move | Hold full position if seeking 300%+ | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 2]] |
| Mental stops / no hard stops | Easy to skip taking loss | Hard stops in system, market hours only | [[Bullish Momentum Burst Guide-Section 1|Bullish MB Section 4]] |
