## Entry Methods

| Method | When | How | Stop | Notes |
|--------|------|-----|------|-------|
| **OPG** (Opening Price Guarantee) | Catalyst day, pre-09:28 | Market-on-open order | 2.5–5% below open | Requires conviction; best for clear catalysts |
| **Buy Stop Limit (BSLO)** | Anticipation, pre-market | Trigger at close+1%, limit at trigger+0.25% | Below tight consolidation | Must place before market opens |
| **Limit / Mid-Price** | High-priced stocks | Mid-price in IB or limit at desired price | Low of day | Never market order on $100+ stocks |
| **Direct Entry** | DEP, first 10 min | Buy as soon as stock shows life (up $0.10–$0.30) | 0.5–2.5% | No confirmation needed |

## Volatility Contraction Patterns

| Pattern | Definition | Used In |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **3T (3 Tight Days)** | 3 consecutive days up/down <1% | Anticipation, DEP watchlist |
| **2T (2 Tight Days)** | 2 consecutive days up/down <1% | Anticipation, DEP watchlist |
| **Orderly Pullback** | Low volume, going sideways after first leg | DEP, MDT, ANTS |
| **V-Contraction** | Linear, narrower negative days before consolidation | Bullish MB, ANTS |

## What NOT to Do

| Mistake | Why Wrong | Correct Way | Source |
|---------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Wait for confirmation / ORB | Stop becomes 8–10%; you miss the move | Enter early with tiny stop | [[16. DEP Guide|DEP Part 8]] |
| Buy second/third day | Move is 3–5 days; most gone by day 2–3 | Buy day 1 at SOS; or wait for new DEP | [[15. Start of a Swing Guide|SOS]] |
| Enter anticipation in morning | You miss EP/SOS breakouts | Place BSLO at 2:58 PM day before | [[08. Anticipation (ANTS) Guide|ANTS Part 3]] |
| Enter reversal in morning | Win rate very low; morning is for breakouts | Run reversal scan at 3 PM, enter at 3:58 | [[22. Reversals Guide|Reversals 3]] |
| Use market orders on high-priced stocks | Slippage kills tight stop edge | Use mid-price or limit order | [[15. Start of a Swing Guide|SOS Part 11]] |
| Do pre-market entry | Low liquidity, stops get blown out | OPG or first 10 min regular session | [[21. Process Guide|Process]] |

## Decision Tree

```
Is there a catalyst today?
├─ Yes → Is it clear and genuine?
│   ├─ Yes → Can I enter in first 15 min?
│   │   ├─ Yes → EP 9M / OPG entry
│   │   └─ No → DEP candidate; add to watchlist
│   └─ No → Skip
└─ No → Is stock in tight consolidation with momentum?
    ├─ Yes → Anticipation entry (BSLO at 2:58 PM)
    └─ No → Is it 3:30 PM with exhaustion setup?
        ├─ Yes → Reversal bullish entry at 3:58
        └─ No → No trade
```
