## Summary

- **Ranking strongest stocks**: Sort the bullish TI65 list by TI65 value and focus only on the **top 25, 50, or 100** stocks — these are the strongest and offer the highest velocity for pullback/bounce entries.
- **Example**: A stock ranked #17 by TI65 with an orderly pullback goes directly into the anticipation watchlist.
- **Ranking weakest stocks**: Similarly, find the lowest TI65 stocks on the bearish side for short candidates or deeply oversold bounce plays.
- **Core principle**: TI65 can be used for **ranking** (knowing which stocks are strongest/weakest) before looking for setups within that ranked universe.
