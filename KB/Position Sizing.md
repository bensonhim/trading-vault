# Position Sizing

> **Index note** — brief summaries with [[wikilinks]] to guides and transcripts.
> For full detail, follow the links. The transcripts are the most comprehensive source.

## Core Principle

> *"How much you can put in a trade is a function of your stop."* — [[15. Start of a Swing Guide\|SOS Part 9]]

| Relationship | Rule | Source |
|-------------|------|--------|
| **Closer stop = bigger size** | 1% stop → can put 50% of account; 10% stop → can only put 5% | [[03. Bullish Momentum Burst Guide\|Bullish MB Section 4]], [[18. Mind Clarity Guide\|Mind Clarity Part 1]] |
| **Wider stop = smaller size** | If using wide stops, drop position size proportionally | [[21. Process Guide\|Process EP9M]] |
| **Conviction = bigger size** | Real catalyst + expertise → 25–50%+ of account | [[17. Catalyst Guide\|Catalyst Part 1]], [[16. DEP Guide\|DEP Part 8]] |
| **Speculative = tiny size** | Uncertain catalyst → 1% risk max | [[21. Process Guide\|Process EP9M]] |

## Adding to Winners

| Setup | Can You Add? | When | Source |
|-------|-------------|------|--------|
| **DEP** | Yes | When it breaks out from entry area | [[16. DEP Guide\|DEP Part 5]] |
| **Momentum Burst (3–5 day)** | **NO** — "no way you can add" | N/A | [[21. Process Guide\|Process Create]] |
| **Trend Trading** | Yes | Within first 1/3 of the trend | [[21. Process Guide\|Process Create]] |
| **Sugar Babies / General** | Rarely | Only if "startup position" develops | [[21. Process Guide\|Process EP9M]] |

> *"I never add to a position, but I was so convinced about NBIS... I added to the position, which is the reason why I took a dump on this one."* — [[21. Process Guide\|Process EP9M]]

## What NOT to Do

| Mistake | Why Wrong | Correct Way | Source |
|---------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Add to a loser | Compounding losses | Never add to losing position; exit at stop | [[21. Process Guide\|Process EP9M]] |
| Same size regardless of setup | Wastes edge on best setups, overrisks on weak ones | Full size on A+, smaller on continuation/speculative | [[22. Reversals Guide\|Reversals 2]] |
| Too small on high-conviction DEP | Wastes the close-stop advantage | 25–50%+ on genuine catalyst DEP | [[16. DEP Guide\|DEP Part 8]] |
| Too big on story stocks | High volatility can wipe out account | 5–10% max on stories | [[19. Deep Dive Guide\|Deep Dive Part 6]] |
| Averaging down | Turning small loss into big loss | Exit at stop, redeploy capital | [[21. Process Guide\|Process EP9M]] |
