## Most Dangerous Mistakes (By Frequency)

**Top 5 mistakes Pradeep sees most often:**

1. **Buying second/third day** — "There is nothing in this trading which works by buying on a third day." — [[18. Mind Clarity Guide\|Mind Clarity Part 13]]
2. **Waiting for confirmation / ORB** — "If you wait for opening range breakout, your stop is going to be eight or 9%." — [[16. DEP Guide\|DEP Part 8]]
3. **Trading without catalyst** — "If you can't find a catalyst in 10 minutes, skip it." — [[16. DEP Guide\|DEP Part 2]]
4. **No process / too many stocks** — "Their focus list is never more than five, and that is why they are successful." — [[21. Process Guide\|Process — Fine Tune]]
5. **Mental stops / not moving to breakeven** — "I am not a fan of mental stop." — [[03. Bullish Momentum Burst Guide\|Bullish MB Section 4]]
