## Deep Work Methodology

> *"My number one recommended book for traders is a book called Deep Work by Cal Newport."* — [[16. DEP Guide\|DEP Part 9]]

**The 48-Hour Study Plan:**
1. **Pick ONE setup** to master (EP 9M, DEP, reversal, etc.)
2. **Lock yourself away** for 24–48 hours
3. **Study 5,000+ examples** of that setup
4. **Document for each:** pre-move price, catalyst, sector, capitalization, volume, legs, duration
5. **Do 3+ passes** — question quality improves each round
6. **Result:** Your brain permanently alters; you "unsee" false setups and instantly recognize valid ones
