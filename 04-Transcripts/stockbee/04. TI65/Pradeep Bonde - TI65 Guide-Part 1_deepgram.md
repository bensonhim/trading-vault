## Summary

- **What is TI65**: Trend Intensity 65 is a momentum indicator — the ratio of 7-day MA to 65-day MA. Value > 1.05 = bullish trend; < 0.95 = bearish trend.
- **Three things TI65 tells you**: (1) Whether a stock is trending, (2) Exactly when the trend started and ended, (3) How intense/fast the trend is (velocity).
- **Visual chart**: Green area = bullish trend intensity, red area = bearish, no color = neutral zone.
- **Example**: TI65 of 1.82 means the stock is moving very fast (7-day MA is 82% above 65-day MA); Tesla shown transitioning from negative to positive momentum.
- **Two uses**: (1) Find anticipation setups when momentum is positive, (2) Rank the strongest and weakest stocks in the market.
