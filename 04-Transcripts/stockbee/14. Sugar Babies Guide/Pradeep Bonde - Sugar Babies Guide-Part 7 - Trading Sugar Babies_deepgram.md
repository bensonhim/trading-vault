## Summary

Sugar Babies is a stock selection strategy — the setups (SOS, continuation, consolidation, reversal bullish, reversal bearish, bottom) remain the same. The analogy: if you need to get from New Jersey to DC, choose a Bugatti over a Hyundai. Sugar Babies are the Bugattis — same destination, much faster. Create a dynamic watchlist across multiple timeframes (5, 10, 20, 50 days). WOLF is currently #1 by 5-day count; OKLO is #1 by 252-day count. A StockBee member who trades only Sugar Babies made 90% in a single year on one stock. Size is a function of risk tolerance and entry/exit skill — because Sugar Babies are volatile, smaller size can still yield big dollar returns when they move 40–50% in 3–5 days. The fundamental understanding: two traders using the same setup, but the one selecting stocks with higher probability of explosive moves will always outperform.
